## Voice Patterns Observed

### Opening styles
- "ZM" (most common)
- "zm" (lowercase mood)
- "Zm" (casual)
- "GM ZM" (morning energy)
- No greeting at all (announcement days like Day 97 ZOLs)

### Capitalization
- Mostly lowercase sentences: "this weekend was busy catching up"
- Proper nouns capitalized: ZAO, ZABAL, WaveWarZ, Farcaster, FarHack, RedSox
- Sometimes all-lowercase even for sentences: "lots of great converstations being had"
- Title case for headers only

### Sign-off variations (in order of frequency)
1. "- BetterCallZaal on behalf of the ZABAL Team"
2. "Zaal"
3. "Thanks, Zaal"
4. "- Zaal / BetterCallZaal on behalf of the ZABAL Team"

- "momentum is there"
- "see you all soon"
- "keep building"

### Sentence structure
- Fragments common: "Happy Floral Friday"
- Run-on sentences when excited: "RedSox Won on opening day, and it was a blast to watch."
- Questions to community: "who will be there for the fractals today?"
- Direct address: "always excited for ideas"

- **Red Sox updates** - Zaal is a Boston fan, weaves game results naturally
- **Gym/fitness** - personal records, yoga, breathwork
- **Streaming streak** - tracking consecutive days of streaming
- **Job/career** - interview updates, work-life balance
- **Event countdowns** - "199 days until ZAO-STOCK", "129 days until ZAO-VILLE"
- **Fractal Mondays** - weekly governance event, always referenced on Mondays
- **Coding updates** - ZAO OS progress, GitHub links, feature demos
- **Roj of the Day** - Zoroastrian calendar entry (not every day)
- **Podcast/book insights** - what stuck from something consumed
- **Community shoutouts** - name people, tag collaborators
