## Funding: New Media Commons / Fractured Atlas

FailOften runs **New Media Commons (NMC)** — a fiscal sponsorship program through **Fractured Atlas** (501(c)(3)).

- Donations through NMC/Fractured Atlas are **TAX-DEDUCTIBLE** for corporations and individuals
- Opens door to $10K–$100K grants
- Q3/Q4 corporate donation cycles align perfectly with October event timing
- NMC = funding layer, **Interact** (FailOften's agency) = execution layer
- Requirement: must be public-facing + newbie engagement (ZAO Stock qualifies)

## Venues

### Franklin Street Parklet (Main Stage)
- Seasonal installation: May 19 – Oct 13 (city + Heart of Ellsworth)
- Full block of Franklin St between Main & Store St, in front of City Hall
- Power available (hosts amplified concerts + film screenings)
- Events through HoE are EXEMPT from mass gathering ordinance
- City public Wi-Fi available

### Black Moon Public House (After-Party)
- 142 Main St, corner of Main + Franklin — 30 seconds from Parklet
- Owned by **Katina Stanwood + Steve Peer**
- ~2,700 sq ft, restored 1890s bar, tin ceilings, original floors
- Live music 4 nights/week — busiest music venue in Ellsworth
- Co-tenants: Vinyl Vogue (records) + TRIO (home goods) = "Newberry Exchange"
- Already a Maine Craft Weekend venue
