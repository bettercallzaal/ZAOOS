# ZAO Stock 2026 (Oct 3, Ellsworth ME)

ZAO's first IRL music festival. **Oct 3, 2026, Franklin Street Parklet, Ellsworth Maine.** Outdoor, 12pm-6pm, after-party at Black Moon Public House. Official part of the 9th Annual Art of Ellsworth: Maine Craft Weekend (statewide promotion confirmed by Heart of Ellsworth).

**One-line pitch (internal):** Annual ZAO meeting / retreat. Anchored in Ellsworth because Zaal bought a house there — this is his town, year after year.

**Public framing:** Art-first festival. Tech is the invisible underlayer. "Anyone should be able to attend without knowing anything about blockchain."

**Goal:** 500-1,000 attendees year 1.
