# ZAO STOCK

> **The ZAO's flagship culture, art & music festival**
> **Date:** October 3, 2026 | **Location:** Franklin Street Parklet, Downtown Ellsworth, Maine
> **Part of:** 9th Annual Art of Ellsworth: Maine Craft Weekend (statewide)

---

## What Is ZAOstock?

A 1-day outdoor music festival during Maine Craft Weekend. Art first. Tech invisible. 10 artists performing 12pm-6pm at the Franklin Street Parklet, followed by an after-party at Black Moon Public House (Steve Peer & Katina Stanwood's bar, 30 seconds walk from the Parklet).

Year 1 of a multi-year vision that evolves into a culture + art + music + tech conference and eventually a residential summer camp for artist entrepreneurs.
