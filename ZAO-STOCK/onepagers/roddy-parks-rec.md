# ZAOstock 2026 - One Page

> **Music festival - Saturday October 3, 2026 - Franklin Street Parklet - Ellsworth, Maine**
> Submitted to Roddy Ehrlenbach, Director of Parks, Recreation & Facilities - meeting Tue 2026-04-28
> Contact: Zaal Panthaki - zaalp99@gmail.com - (207) [PHONE]

## What it is

ZAOstock is a one-day music festival celebrating local + regional independent artists, run by The ZAO (a small music community / artist organization based in Maine). It is part of the **Art of Ellsworth** program in partnership with **Heart of Ellsworth** (Cara Romano).

**Format:** afternoon-into-evening, 4-6 acts across one stage, all-ages, free admission with optional donations + sponsor support, food/beverage from local vendors.

**Anchor partners:** Wallace Events (staging + tents), Heart of Ellsworth (programming + community), Art of Ellsworth (umbrella series).

## Why Ellsworth, why this venue

I (Zaal) live in Ellsworth. The ZAO has spent four years building a music + community organization, and ZAOstock is its first public Maine event. Choosing Franklin Street Parklet keeps it walkable, downtown, low-impact, and integrated with existing Ellsworth foot traffic - small enough to do well, central enough to draw locals + day-trippers.

## Expected scale

| | Aug 15 dry run | Oct 3 main event |
|---|---|---|
| Attendance | 50 invited guests + team | 200-400 estimate |
| Hours | 2pm-6pm (4 hr) | early afternoon - early evening (~6 hr) |
| Acts | 2 local + 1 preview | 4-6 |
| Sound | modest PA | mid-size PA, daytime levels |
| Vendors | 0-1 light food | 2-4 local food + bev |

## What we bring

- Insurance (event policy, certificate naming City of Ellsworth as additional insured)
- Production: staging, sound, lighting, generator if needed (via Wallace Events)
- 19-person volunteer crew across 8 working circles (ops, music, partners, finance, merch, marketing, media, host)
- Cleanup commitment: site returned same-or-better than found
- All artist contracts + payments handled by ZAO
- Build-in-public: livestream, photo/video documentation (we publish process + content for community + sponsors)

## What we'd ask of Parks / Recreation

1. **Parklet permit hold** for Saturday October 3, 2026 (full day) and Saturday August 15, 2026 (afternoon, dry run / dress rehearsal).
2. **Power** - what's available at the parklet, what we'd need to bring in.
3. **Parking guidance** - overflow plan, signage, anything the city can pre-route.
4. **Day-of point of contact** - one name + number for the city if something needs coordinating in the moment.
5. **Security / safety coordination** - what triggers PD involvement, what's our scope, any required briefing.
6. **Noise / sound levels** - confirm expectations + cutoff time, how we keep neighbors comfortable.

## What success looks like

A small festival that proves the model: ZAO can produce a real event in Ellsworth that local + regional artists want to play, locals want to come to, sponsors see value in, and the city is glad happens here. If Year 1 lands, Year 2 grows.

## Why now

The ZAO has been in build mode since 2022. We have artists, content, sponsors, and a community ready. ZAOstock is the moment that builds turn into a tradition.
