# ZAO Stock 2026 - Emergency & Contingency Plan

## Weather

October in Ellsworth averages 55F high, 35F low. Rain is common.

| Scenario | Plan | Trigger | Who Decides |
|----------|------|---------|-------------|
| **Light rain** | Continue outdoors. Have tarps for equipment. | Drizzle, no wind | Zaal |
| **Heavy rain / storm** | Move to Black Moon Public House (142 Main St, 30 sec walk) | Sustained rain, wind, lightning | Zaal + Steve Peer |
| **Tent backup** | Wallace Events tent over stage area | Pre-scheduled if forecast is 60%+ rain 3 days before | Zaal (call Wallace by Sep 30) |
| **Cold (<45F)** | Announce "dress warm" on socials. Hot coffee/tea station. | Weather forecast | Auto |

**Decision timeline:**
- 3 days before: check forecast, decide on tent reservation
- Morning of: final call on outdoor vs. tent vs. indoor by 7 AM
- During event: Zaal makes all weather calls

## Medical

| Scenario | Plan | Who |
|----------|------|-----|

## Power Failure

| Scenario | Plan |
|----------|------|
| **PA system fails** | Sound vendor troubleshoots. If unfixable, acoustic sets or move to Black Moon. |
| **Generator fails** | Contact sound vendor for backup. City Hall (directly behind Parklet) may have power outlet access. |
| **Livestream drops** | Tech team switches to phone backup stream. Post "technical difficulties, stand by" on socials. |

---

## Artist No-Show

| Scenario | Plan |
|----------|------|
| **1 artist cancels (advance)** | Extend other sets from 25 to 30 min. Add extra DJ time. |
| **1 artist cancels (day-of)** | DJ fills the slot. Announce "change in lineup." No apologies needed. |
| **2+ artists cancel** | Shorten the event. 4-5 artists still makes a full afternoon. Adjust run-of-show. |
| **DJ cancels** | Backup: phone + Bluetooth speaker between sets. Not ideal but works for transitions. |

---

## Crowd Issues

| Scenario | Plan |
|----------|------|
| **Overcrowding** | Open up adjacent sidewalk space. Parklet can't be fenced - it's a public street. |
| **Disruptive person** | Volunteer approaches calmly. If escalates, call Ellsworth PD non-emergency: (207) 667-2168 |
| **Noise complaint** | Zaal addresses with neighbor. Lower volume if needed. Event ends at 6 PM - respectful hours. |

## Equipment / Logistics

| Scenario | Plan |
|----------|------|
| **Merch runs out** | Note what sold out for reorder. Take pre-orders via QR code. |
| **Food truck no-show** | Direct people to Fogtown (25 Pine St, 2 min walk) and downtown restaurants. |
| **Signage damage (wind)** | Extra zip ties + tape in ops kit. Volunteers re-secure. |
