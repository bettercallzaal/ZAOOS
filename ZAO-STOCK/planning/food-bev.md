# ZAOstock 2026 - Food & Beverage Plan

## The Experience

Six hours outdoors in October Maine. People need food, water, and warmth. The food experience should feel like part of the festival, not an afterthought.

---

## Water & Hydration (Non-Negotiable)

| Item | Source | Cost |
|------|--------|------|
| Water cooler/dispenser | Rent or borrow | $0-$50 |
| Cups (compostable) | Purchase | $15-$20 |
| Location | Near check-in table, visible from stage | - |

**Someone must be assigned to refill the water throughout the day.**

## Food Options

### Option A: Food Trucks (Recommended)
Invite 2-3 trucks. No vendor fee (they make money from sales, we get food for our audience for free).

**Local targets:**
| Truck/Vendor | Cuisine | Contact | Notes |
|-------------|---------|---------|-------|
| Fogtown Brewing | Wood-fired pizza + craft beer | 25 Pine St, fogtownbrewing.com | Right around the corner, already a community hub |
| Local food trucks (TBD) | Various | Search Ellsworth/Bangor food trucks | October = end of season, trucks may be available |
| Bakery/pastry vendor | Coffee + pastries | Precipice Coffee or local | Morning/early afternoon option |

### Option B: In-Kind Partnerships
- Fogtown provides pizza in exchange for logo on stage banner + social media mentions
- Precipice provides coffee in exchange for same
- This saves money AND builds local sponsor relationships

### Option C: BYOB/Picnic Style
- No formal food vendors
- Encourage attendees to bring food / visit downtown restaurants
- Least effort but worst guest experience

**Recommendation:** Option A + B hybrid. Fogtown pizza as an in-kind partner, 1-2 food trucks with no fee, Precipice for coffee.

## Alcohol

| Location | Situation |
|----------|-----------|
| **At the Parklet** | NO alcohol sales unless we get a special event liquor license. Probably not worth the hassle for Year 1. |
| **Black Moon (after-party)** | Full bar, their license. This is where the drinking happens. |
| **Fogtown (nearby)** | Full bar, their license. People can walk over and bring food back. |

**Recommendation for Year 1:** No alcohol at the Parklet. Keep it family-friendly. Direct people to Black Moon and Fogtown for drinks. This also avoids needing liquor liability insurance.

## Dietary Considerations

- Ask artists about dietary needs when confirming travel
- Ensure at least one vegetarian/vegan option available
- Water station covers hydration for everyone
- October weather: have hot coffee/tea available if it's cold
