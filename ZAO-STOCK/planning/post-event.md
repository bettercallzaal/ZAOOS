## Year 2 Decision Criteria

| If Year 1 hits... | Year 2 scope |
|-------------------|-------------|
| 100+ attendees, 2+ sponsors, team wants to continue | Same scale, better execution, apply for Levitt grant |
| 200+ attendees, 4+ sponsors, strong press coverage | Expand: 2 days, more artists, bigger budget, tent infrastructure |
| <50 attendees, 0 sponsors, team burned out | Scale down to COC Concertz-style virtual + small IRL gathering |
| Any outcome + Zaal still lives in Ellsworth | Year 2 happens. The commitment is to the town, not to metrics. |
