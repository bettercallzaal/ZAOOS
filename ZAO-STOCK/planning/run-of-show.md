## Notes

- 10 artists x 25 min = 250 min of music = 4 hours 10 min
- 9 DJ transitions x 10 min = 90 min
- 2 breaks x 15 min = 30 min
- MC/opening/closing = 15 min
- Total: ~6 hours (12:00-6:05)
- Set times are guidelines - adjust based on artist count if fewer than 10 confirmed
- If 8 artists: extend sets to 30 min or add a longer break
- DJ is the glue. They must be reliable and read the room.
