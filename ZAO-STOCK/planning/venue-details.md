# ZAO Stock 2026 — Venue & Logistics Details

## Franklin Street Parklet (Main Stage)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Franklin Street between Main Street and Store Street, directly in front of City Hall |
| **Type** | SEASONAL — street closed to vehicles annually for parklet installation |
| **2025 dates** | May 19 (7AM) through at least October 13 |
| **First opened** | May 28, 2021 (WABI TV reported official opening) |
| **Managed by** | City of Ellsworth (road closure, public works builds tables) + Heart of Ellsworth (programming/events) |
| **Features** | Picnic tables (built by Ellsworth Public Works), greenery, seating, life-size downtown wayfinding map, city public Wi-Fi |
| **Size** | Occupies full block of Franklin St between Main and Store St (one short block). Exact dimensions not published — need to measure on-site |
| **Power** | Not documented but hosts amplified concerts + film screenings (projector + audio). Directly in front of City Hall — electrical access available. Contact: 207-667-2563 |

### Events Already Held at the Parklet
- Ellsworth Summer Concert series (e.g., The Soul Benders, Jul 31 2025, 7-9 PM)
- Maine Outdoor Film Festival screening (Aug 20 2025, 7:30-9 PM)
- GoldenOak concert (folk) with opener Caroline Cotter (WERU/Artsworth/HoE)
- Food Fest Ellsworth and Market on Main (Sep 26 - Oct 4 2025)
- "Hopeful" illuminated sign lighting ceremony
- Library outdoor adventure storytimes
- Art of Ellsworth events

### Permit Requirements
- **Events sponsored by/under City of Ellsworth supervision = EXEMPT from mass gathering ordinance**
- Since Heart of Ellsworth partners with the city on parklet events, ZAO Stock coordinated through HoE likely falls under this exemption
- Non-city-sponsored events: application to Police Chief **45 days before** + corporate bond for cleanup/damages (City of Ellsworth Chapter 14)
- Maine state law (Title 28-A, Section 1054): separate permit needed for music/entertainment at licensed liquor establishments (applies to Black Moon, not the Parklet)

## October Weather — What to Plan For

| Metric | Value | Impact |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **Avg high** | 58°F (14°C) | Comfortable for outdoor daytime event with layers |
| **Avg low** | 44°F (7°C) | Evening gets chilly — after-party indoors at Black Moon is smart |
| **Temperature range** | 38-68°F possible | Dress in layers. Performers may need warm-up area |
| **Precipitation** | October is the **WETTEST MONTH** — 4.8 inches avg | **Tent backup is NOT optional, it's essential.** Wallace Events tent is critical |
| **Rain chance** | 34% on any given day | 1 in 3 chance of rain on Oct 3. Plan for it |
| **On rain days** | 0.56 inches average | Not torrential but steady — outdoor stage needs cover |
| **Humidity** | 82% average | High — electronics/instruments may need protection |
| **Wind** | 12.5 mph average ("breezy") | Secure signage, tent walls, lightweight items |
| **Sunrise** | ~6:30 AM EDT | — |
| **Sunset** | ~6:10 PM EDT | **Event ends at 6pm = just as sun sets.** Natural transition to indoor after-party |
| **Daylight** | ~11 hrs 40 min | Full daylight for 12-6pm show |

### Weather Planning Decisions
- **Wallace Events tent is MANDATORY, not optional** — October is the wettest month. Even if forecast is clear, have tent on standby
- **12pm-6pm timing is perfect** — all daylight, sunset transitions naturally to Black Moon indoor after-party
- **Wind at 12.5 mph** — need to secure any stage backdrop, banners, signage. Tent should be frame tent (more wind-resistant than pole tent)
- **82% humidity** — bring protective covers for any electronics, instruments. Sound vendor should know about outdoor Maine conditions
- **Temperature** — attendees will be comfortable with fall layers. Performers may want a warm-up/green room area (inside City Hall? Library?)

### MDI/Bar Harbor (Off-Season Deals)
- October rates significantly lower than July/August
- $500-600/week basic cabins, up to $10K/week luxury
- Many close mid-October — book early October dates
- 2,646+ Vrbo properties, 150+ Airbnb
- 20-minute drive from Ellsworth

### Booking Strategy
- **Best option for team:** One large Airbnb/VRBO that fits 6-10 people (cheaper per-person, communal)
- **Book by July** — October is late shoulder season but Maine Craft Weekend creates demand
- **Check Acadia Village Resort** for multi-unit group booking
