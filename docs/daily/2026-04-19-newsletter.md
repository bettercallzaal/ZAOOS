## MINDFUL MOMENT

There's a pattern I keep noticing: we build the wiring before we flip the switch. ZAOstock had its dashboard before it had its team on it. ZAO OS had its home screen before it had its apps. The portal had its auth before it had its workflows.

ZabalConviction is the same shape. The wiring is done. The switch is the deploy command.

There's something useful in this pattern — you build the container first, then you fill it. But there's also a trap: you can keep polishing the container forever to avoid the moment of commitment that filling it requires.

Eight days is long enough to wait. Today the switch gets flipped.
