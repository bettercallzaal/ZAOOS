## MINDFUL MOMENT

Two separate docs today — one about building a brain that remembers, one about building governance that stays visible — are solving the same problem from different angles.

Nouns learned it the hard way: proposals pass and then disappear into a void. No one knows if the work happened. The community funded things it couldn't see, got frustrated, and forked. The fix wasn't more voting — it was more legibility. Propdates made work visible. Once you could see it, you could govern it.

The Aware Brain research is the internal version of the same pattern. 540+ docs in the research library, and the question is the same: how do you make your own knowledge visible to yourself? Not just stored — visible. Queryable. Something that surfaces the right thing at the right moment.

Legibility is the infrastructure. For DAOs, for research libraries, for teams of autonomous agents. The thing you can't see, you can't improve.
