**MINDFUL MOMENT**

The theme Jose kept returning to — "I want to explain to my grandma" — isn't just a UX note. It's a diagnostic. If the story you're telling doesn't survive a translation to someone who loves you but has no context, the story is doing the work that jargon usually does: keeping outsiders out and insiders in. The ZAO's external language still has too much of that. The internal tools are getting good. The story needs to catch up.
