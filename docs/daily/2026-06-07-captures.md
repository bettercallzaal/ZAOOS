### Doc 804 — Colorado Artist Company Act (A-Corp)
Colorado SB 26-133, signed by Gov. Polis June 2, 2026. Takes effect August 12, 2026. First US legal entity built specifically for artists — LLC-based with three locked defaults: (1) 51% artist voting control in statute (cannot be overridden by operating agreement), (2) IP reversion on dissolution, (3) economic rights split from governance. First filings: not possible until July 1, 2027 (SoS forms don't exist until then).

**Decision:** Track it, don't form yet. Pre-register at artistcorporations.com.

This is the legal mirror of ZAO's "artists keep ownership + outsiders fund without controlling" thesis. Near-exact match to the ZAO incubator model.
