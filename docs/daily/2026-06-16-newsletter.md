**MINDFUL MOMENT**

The Bonfire relay PR changes one word in the persona: "reactive" becomes "proactive." One word is doing a lot of work. Reactive means waiting to be asked. Proactive means ZOE holds the relationship with the knowledge graph the way a good research assistant holds their own tools — not passing the question up the chain, but going to get the answer and coming back with it. Most of building autonomous systems is figuring out which decisions should stay with the human and which ones the system should just handle. The relay gets that boundary right: knowledge = agent, decision = human. That's the whole design pattern in two words.
