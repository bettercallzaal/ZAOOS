## What Shipped

### ZABAL Bonfire Drain Loop (PR #768 — doc 781 Phase 2)

The week's biggest technical ship. After sitting open since Tuesday with 120/120 tests passing, it finally merged Friday.

**What it does:** ZOE can now drain the `zg:bonfire:pending` Upstash queue and promote community-submitted Bonfire episodes into the canonical knowledge graph. Steward-gated — Zaal's FID (`19640`) is the initial steward. Approval via DM: `/bonfire` surfaces the oldest pending episode, `y` promotes, `n` removes.

This is the architecture that closes the community-contribution loop. Members can write to the graph. Stewards gate quality. The graph grows from the community, not just from Zaal.

### Security: Auth/Webhook Fail-Closed Sweep (PR #773)
