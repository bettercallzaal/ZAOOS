### ZAO Over-Audit (doc 841, tier: DISPATCH)

63 confirmed findings across security, tests, dead code, deps, infra, and agent reliability. Two P0s:
1. **Next.js DoS CVE** — `next@16.2.0` vulnerable (GHSA-q4gf-8mx6-v5v3, CVSS 7.5). Fix: upgrade to 16.2.9+.
2. **Agent dead-letter queue gap** — trade audit events are permanently lost if DB is down during a swap. Fix: retry/fallback in `events.ts`.

The adversarial method matters: 20 findings were refuted when the verifier read the actual code. Upload rate-limiting that looked missing? In middleware. Promise.all that looked risky? Supabase client can't reject. The discipline of trying to disprove findings before acting on them saved 20 ghost fix-PRs.
