## 3. Creator Economy

### Farcaster Pro Rewards

- **$25,000+ distributed weekly** to creators in USDC
- Farcaster Pro: $120/year subscription, 100% of revenue → weekly creator reward pool
- First 10,000 Pro sold in <6 hours ($1.2M raised)
- Uses cube root of "active follower count" to prevent gaming
- Expanding to pay **1,000 people per week** in USDC

### Earning Mechanisms

| Mechanism | How It Works |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Warpcast Rewards** | Weekly USDC based on engagement quality |
| **DEGEN tipping** | Daily allowance, tip via replies |
| **Moxie** | Automatic compensation for likes/recasts/comments |
| **NOTES (Sonata)** | Music curation rewards |
| **Rounds.wtf** | Community contests with reward pools |
| **Amps** | USDC-based likes for visibility |

### DEGEN Deep Dive

- Channels with boosted tips: /Base, /Founders, /Dev, /Design, /Product, /Frontend, /FarCon, /frames, /perl, /Zora, /farcastHER

- ERC-20 on Base
- Users earn MOXIE through engagement
- Fan Tokens: buy-and-burn on creator earnings
- Daily smart allowance based on previous week's engagement
- 40M+ MOXIE burned (~3% of circulating supply)

### Clanker Deep Dive

- Mention @clanker on Farcaster with a token idea → it deploys on Base
- Record: 13,000+ tokens/day (Jan 30-31, 2026); $300M+ in fees in two days
- $400K–$500K weekly even in quiet periods

## Key Implications for ZAO OS

1. **Music niche is underserved** — Sonata exists but no dominant gated music community client
2. **Token incentive layer is mature** — DEGEN, Moxie, NOTES, Rounds.wtf all available to leverage
3. **Small but high-quality user base** — 40-60K DAU, crypto-literate, 18-34, US-heavy
4. **Mini Apps are the growth vector** — app store discovery, wallet connectivity, notifications
5. **Creator earnings are real** — $25K+ weekly, expanding to 1,000 creators/week
6. **Analytics APIs are robust** — Neynar, Airstack, OpenRank provide everything needed
7. **Grant funding available** — Purple DAO, Optimism RetroPGF, Gitcoin
8. **Channels going onchain** — ZAO could own its community membership data on-chain
