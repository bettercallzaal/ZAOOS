## Full Post (Thread / Long-form)

**Q1 2026 was 25 wins. Here's the whole story.**

It started with one Incented campaign.

Artizen Fund sponsored a room at Carrot Castle for Boulder Week. I applied, rallied the community to vote with $ART, and won. Free room at the legendary web3 builder house — dinners, hot tub sessions, late-night poker with artists and builders.

I showed up to ETH Boulder in February and installed Claude Code. Within 24 hours, I upgraded a Discord bot I'd been procrastinating on for 5 months. Tested it at the next Monday fractal meeting. It worked.

That was the domino.

---

**The first month after Claude Code was all about the fractal bot.** Making it what I always wanted it to be. Integrating Artizen project curation so community members could use their ZAO Respect to decide which creative projects get funded. 33 submissions came in. 20 got curated.

Then I started thinking about front ends for our community.

That became ZAO OS — my first full coding project. A gated Farcaster client for The ZAO. Shipped it in the last two weeks of Q1. Few users so far, and that's okay. It's inspiring everything we're building in Q2.

---

**But the code was just one lane. Here's everything else that happened in Q1:**

**ZABAL ecosystem launched.** Token went live on Clanker on January 1st. Partnered with SongJam (Adam & Logesh) for the leaderboard — 1000+ participants in the December campaign alone. Integrated Empire Builder as the rewards layer — $168 distributed, 178M ZABAL burned, dual leaderboard system with boosts up to 9.2x. Daily airdrops running all year.

**ZAOndz went live.** Nouns-style subDAO on Base. 20% of the ZABAL token lives there. NFT holders make proposals. Launched in March with support from the IYKYK and Fractal Nouns communities.

**12 ZOLs recognized.** ZABAL Opinion Leaders — top 3 on the SongJam leaderboard each month. GodFactor, wildermax, Preshzinobabe, tabbytheblack, ohnahji, akele, jason alder, collinsxweb3, metamu, candytoybox. Some earned it twice.

---

**WaveWarZ kept growing.**

500+ quick battles. Nightly format, Mon-Fri 8:30 PM EST. We transitioned from X Spaces to YouTube Live — that was the biggest platform milestone. Stilo World vs It's Wonderful hit an all-time high on volume. 720 total battles, 442 SOL ($35K+) in total volume across the platform.

On Valentine's Day, we ran Indies vs Classics — a love songs benefit battle with PolyRaiders. Raised over $1,000 for kids in Africa.

---

**I ran 6 Incented campaigns** totaling $260+ USDC and 485 million ZABAL in rewards:

- Clip ZABAL on Den (45-sec clips from our streams)
- Top Curated Clip (curation challenge — signal over volume)
- Audius WaveWarZ Playlist (5+ tracks minimum)
- Design the First ZABAL Merch (one design becomes official)
- Top ZAO Meme (make a meme with a Z)
- Dappable Builder Challenge (vibe code an app for ZABAL)

Then I created the **ZAO Fund for Emerging Culture** on Artizen. Season 6. A community-owned funding vehicle for independent musicians, artists, technologists, and organizers. Full circle — from receiving funding to creating it.

---

**More wins:**

Co-purchased **Noun #1797** on mainnet with Civil Monkey and Fiona. 0.44 ETH, January 29th, after watching auctions for 2+ months.

Featured in **Crypto Magazine Issue 12** — 2-page "People in Crypto" spread. "Rebuilding Ownership for Independent Artists." Pages 160-161. Print and digital.

Produced **7 BCZ Yapz episodes** (eps 7-14), all on YouTube. Interview format, part of the BetterCallZaal content brand.

Made my **first PR to someone else's production code** — SongJam PR #21. Host controls: mute all, speaker timers, room settings, co-host system. 245 lines. Merged by Logesh on March 25th.

Had **COC Concertz #3** on March 7th — DUO DO, Dope Stilo, and Joseph Goats performing in the StiloWorld metaverse. Next one planned.

Started a **daily Twitch streaming streak** — 2 weeks of consecutive days, building in public across coding, music, community topics. Inspired by watching Ohnahji stream for 8+ months.

**ZAO Stock is back.** Re-energized the ZAO Festivals project with 3 standups in Q1. October 3rd, Franklin St Parklet. And **ZAO Ville** is set for July 25th in the DMV area.

Oh, and ZABAL got **shouted out on stage at ETH Boulder** by Willy Wonka. Blank space moment. That was surreal.

---

**The thesis of Q1:** One campaign win cascaded into an entire quarter of shipping.

Artizen → Carrot Castle → Claude Code → fractal bot → ZAO OS → everything else.

25 wins. Each one gets its own deep-dive post this month.

Q2 starts now.
