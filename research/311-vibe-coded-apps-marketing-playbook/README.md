## Validation Process

1. **Find a painful daily problem** - niches: focus/productivity, fitness, nutrition, meditation, language learning
2. **Check App Store** - look for competitors making $10K+/mo (proves demand)
3. **Download top competitors** - screenshot onboarding + core loop, study every detail
4. **Check TikTok/IG** - identify main growth strategy (influencers, faceless, UGC, paid ads)

## Key Marketing Channels (Ranked)

### 1. UGC Creators ($15/video + viral bonuses)
- Hire creators to post 30-60 videos/month on new accounts
- Payment: $15/video base + bonuses ($150 for 100K views up to $800 for 1M)
- Find via TikTok DM outreach (100 DMs/day)

### 2. Influencers ($1 CPM deals)
- Pay $1 per 1,000 views - no performance = no cost
- Cap deals at $1,000 max
- One good influencer can kickstart an entire app

### 3. Faceless Content (Free)
- Slideshows, screen recordings, text-on-screen, app demos
- 3-5 posts/day, consistent visual brand
- When one format hits, clone it across variations

### 4. Founder-Led Content (Free)
- Example: Cormac (Oasis Health) - viral format, $100K+/mo
- Find a viral format template, adapt it

### 5. Paid Ads (Scalable)
- Once you crack CPA + winning creatives, scale endlessly
- TikTok Spark Ads + Meta
- Use Singular for MMP/attribution tracking

## Onboarding = 70% of the App

The core insight: onboarding converts downloads to paying users. Structure:
1. Remind users why they downloaded
2. Make them realize they have a problem
3. Present your app as the solution
4. Hard unskippable paywall at the end (free trial or upfront)

## App Store Tips

- Clear app name, strong subtitle, 3-5 screenshots, simple description
- Screenshots are part of the funnel - big text, one idea per screen
- "Ugly screenshots that are clear win over pretty screenshots that confuse"
- Follow Apple's guidelines carefully to avoid rejection
