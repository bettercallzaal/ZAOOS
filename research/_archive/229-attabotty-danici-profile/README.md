## 1. Overview

AttaBotty and DaNici are a husband-and-wife creative team based in **Jacksonville, FL**. Together they run **AttaBotty Productions**, a studio specializing in custom animations and original music. Both are **co-founders of The ZAO** (ZTalent Artist Organization), the decentralized, community-driven hub for independent creators.

An NFT project with sci-fi theming: "Intergalactic space travel is fraught with dangers. When resting on an abandoned planet with an ancient mecha metropolis, be sure to bring a big gun — you're going to need it." Available as 5 editions on OpenSea with unlockable content.

### Aldur

A 1/1 NFT available on OpenSea with unlockable content.

### Moving Seasons

An original cutting-edge animated short film / art piece.

### Ghoulish Greeting

An animated work associated with the Moving Seasons collection.

### The Bone Zone (2017)

Original song inspired by the release of the "IT" movie. AttaBotty composed and produced the music. DaNici created all the 2D art for the accompanying music video. Every detail of how the animation accompanies the music was programmed manually from scratch.

### Run Babay (Remix)

A music video remix of "Run Babay," an original song produced by AttaBotty's son when he was **8 years old**. The 2D art in the video was created by DaNici.

### TikTok Girl (2021)

Music video released October 22, 2021. Available on AttaBotty's TikTok and website.

### Music Archive (2006-Present)

AttaBotty's music archive spans from 2006 to present. Tracks from 2012 and earlier represent his "humble beginnings." The full discography is available at [attabotty.com/attabotty-archive](https://www.attabotty.com/attabotty-archive).

### ZAO-Chella

AttaBotty + DaNici produced **ZAO-Chella** — a fusion of digital innovation and music hosted in **Wynwood** (Miami's cultural hub) during **Art Basel week**. Features included:
- Live performances by 10 Web3 musicians
- Collectable trading cards
- Art displays with AR (augmented reality) experiences
- VIP tables hosted by community partners
- Networking opportunities

The event was organized under the [@zaofestivals](https://www.instagram.com/zaofestivals/) brand, with a website at [zaofestivals.com](https://zaofestivals.com/).
