### 1.3 What "Web3's First HBCU" Means

Ohnahji University models itself after Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCUs) -- institutions founded to serve African American students. Ohnahji U applies this concept to blockchain/Web3 education specifically:

- **BIPOC-first:** Explicitly focuses on Black and Brown communities getting access to blockchain education
- **Affordability:** 0.01 ETH mint price -- deliberately low barrier to entry
- **Structured like a university:** Has a Chancellor (Ohnahji B), Deans, "majors" (Communications, Technology), "students" (Alphites), student IDs (NFTs), syllabi, spotlights, and a campus (Discord)
- **Gap they fill:** "There are not many places that teach the actual fundamentals of blockchain" -- the space lacks low-cost, accessible, fundamentals-focused education for underrepresented communities

Maceo is identified in ZAO documentation as a core member of the media and streaming team. The key evidence comes from:

1. **Doc 50 (The ZAO Complete Guide):** Lists Maceo as co-host of "Let's Talk About Ethereum" alongside BetterCallZaal and Ohnahji B (line 490 of the document).

2. **Doc 224 (ZAO Stock Multi-Year Vision):** Lists Maceo in two team capability areas:
   - **Media + Documentation** team (alongside Swarthy Hatter and Ohnahji)
   - **Livestream + Tech** team (alongside Ohnahji and Zaal)
   - Background described as: "Podcast co-host. Media production. Twitch streaming (platforms: bettercallzaal, ohnahji, ezincrypto)"

3. **Maceo appears to have replaced or evolved from the "EZ / EZinCrypto" role.** The LTAW3 podcast (Ep. 0-18) lists hosts as "Ohnahji, EZinCrypto, and BetterCallZaal." When the show transitioned from LTAW3 to "Let's Talk About Ethereum," the host listing changed to "BetterCallZaal + Ohnahji B + Maceo." This suggests either:
   - Maceo is a new addition who joined when the show rebranded
   - Maceo is an alias or evolution of EZ's involvement
   - EZ stepped back and Maceo took the co-host slot

#### Battle Mechanics

1. Two songs/artists are matched
2. Each side gets a Battle Vault PDA (Solana smart contract escrow)
3. Fans deposit SOL into the side they think will win
4. Trading happens live during X Spaces sessions
5. After judging, the battle settles onchain

**Triple Judging:** Human judge + X poll + SOL trading activity

**Settlement Economics:**

| Participant | Share |
|-------------|-------|
| Winning traders | Proportional share of winner pool + 40% of losing pool |
| Losing traders | 50% refunded (partial recovery) |
| Artists | 1% of all trade volume + 5-7% settlement bonus |
| Platform | 0.5% per trade + 3% of losing pool |
| **In ecosystem** | **98.5% stays** |

#### Fee Structure: 1.5% total (1% to artists, 0.5% to platform)
