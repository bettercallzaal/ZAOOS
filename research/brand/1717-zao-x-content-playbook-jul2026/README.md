# 1717 — ZAO X (Twitter) Content Playbook (Jul 2026)

**Type:** BRAND-PLAYBOOK  
**Topic:** Brand  
**Status:** ACTIVE — Canonical post template library for all ZAO X accounts. ZOE posts automatically from approved templates; Zaal posts manually for personal/founder-voice content. Update when: new events are confirmed, stats cross milestones, or new post patterns prove effective. Read in conjunction with doc 1663 (ZAO Brand Voice Guide).

## ZAO X Accounts

| Account | Handle | Voice | Who Posts | Purpose |
|---------|--------|-------|-----------|---------|
| ZAO OS | @TheZaoOS | Platform/org voice — data-led | ZOE (auto) + Zaal (strategic) | DAO announcements, governance, ecosystem |
| BetterCallZaal | @bettercallzaal | Founder voice — personal, direct | Zaal (always) | Founder perspective, ZAOstock, community |
| WaveWarZ Official | @wavewarzofficial | Platform voice — battle-focused | ZOE (auto) | Battle announcements, results, platform updates |

**Hard rules:**
- ZOE NEVER posts from @bettercallzaal — Zaal's personal account is human-only
- ZOE may draft for @bettercallzaal and send to Zaal via Telegram for approval + manual post
- No 3+ posts per account per day except show nights (COC Concertz or ZAOstock)
- No bullish SOL price framing — facts only, no price speculation

## Hashtag & Tag Usage

**Use sparingly — ZAO posts lead with data, not hashtags:**

| Tag | When |
|-----|------|
| #WaveWarZ | Every WaveWarZ post (optional) |
| #Solana | When mentioning SOL/Solana specifically |
| #DAO | Governance posts (avoid overuse) |
| #AfroBeat #Afropop | Africa Battle Week content |
| @wavewarz | Never — that's us |
| @bettercallzaal | Never in ZOE posts — Zaal's personal account |

**Avoid:** #NFT, #Web3, #DeFi, #crypto (unless the post is specifically for crypto-native audience)

**For Africa Battle Week:** Tag artists + organizers, not generic hashtags.

## Engagement Rules

**ZOE responds to:**
- Direct questions about battle mechanics: link to wavewarz.info + relevant doc
- "How do I get started?": link to wavewarz.info + doc 1693 (onboarding guide)
- Artist inquiries: link to doc 1716 (artist earnings reference)

**ZOE escalates to Zaal:**
- Press inquiries
- Investor questions
- Partnership requests
- Complaints or negative coverage
- Any question ZOE can't answer with existing docs

**ZOE never responds to:**
- Price-related questions ("is SOL going up?") — no engagement
- Political content
- Drama between community members on public threads
