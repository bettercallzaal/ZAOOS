## The positioning verdict (from doc 1097 - the honest read)

Sparkz's wedge (energy-first + AI-judged launch timing) is **real but narrow**. No competitor gates launch on readiness - but the quality gate deliberately cuts volume. So **Sparkz wins as the sustainability leader, not the volume leader.** Success = tokens that last 90+ days and build community, not 48-hour pumps.

The competitors (numbers below need verification before you quote them):
- **Clanker** - the volume leader and the raw launcher. Its gap: no pre-launch validation, high early-failure rate. Sparkz is the curated layer on top.
- **Zora creator coins** - auto-generates a coin per profile; widely seen as not having worked. Gap: no curation, thin liquidity, post-launch abandonment.
- **Bankr** - a weaker Clanker-style tool.

What Sparkz must nail to be worth existing: (1) energy-first validation before launch, (2) the AI launch-timing call, (3) post-launch utility scaffolding so engagement survives day 1, (4) creator agency framing ("we help you launch better," not gatekeeping).

## Post-launch durability - the make-or-break (from doc 1099)

Only ~4.55% of creator coins survive 90 days (verify). Speculation dies fast (Friend.tech collapsed in 24h; ~95% of Clanker tokens fail day 1). Degen lasted 24+ months because holders got recurring INCOME, not a bet. So Sparkz's whole thesis lives or dies on the post-launch utility stack, and the non-obvious rule is you must ship it DAY 1-30, not "later" (adding utility late is exactly what kills these coins).

The minimum durable-utility stack for a Sparkz music coin:
1. Recurring revenue-tipping to holders (a slice of streaming/creator revenue, allocated on a regular cadence)
2. Transparent on-chain revenue-share + vesting (prevents insider dumps)
3. Token-gated music tiers (stems, single-voting, private fan events by holdings)
4. Leaderboard + status (top holders showcased, badges, creator shout-outs)
5. Optional charity/cause splits (high stickiness)

Do NOT build: governance theater, whale concentration, airdrop dumps, celebrity endorsement without revenue.

This changes the MVP scope below: Phase 1 must include the durability stack, not just the launch. The energy engine gets a creator to a GOOD launch; the durability stack is what makes the coin SURVIVE - and almost no competitor does it.
