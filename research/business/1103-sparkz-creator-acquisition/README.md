## The Funnel: Step-by-Step Recruitment Motion

5-step process from first contact to token launch, optimized for warm-network speed.

### Step 1: Warm Introduction + Context Setting (Day 1-2)
- Who: Zaal or close collaborator with existing relationship
- Ask: 15-min call to explain how Sparkz validates energy + 99% payout
- Medium: Personal Farcaster DM (not public)
- Success rate: 80%+ (warm network)

### Step 2: Product Demo + Energy-Scoring Explainer (Day 3-5)
- Show Sparkz dashboard with creator's actual Farcaster data
- Explain 4 signals: follower growth (25%), engagement (35%), consistency (20%), community (20%)
- Demo launch flow: hit 60 - click "launch" - Clanker deploys token
- Address fear: 100% creator control, 1% fee on trading only
- Success rate: 70%+

### Step 3: Project Definition + Crowdfund Framing (Day 5-14)
- Define project (album, tour, collab?)
- Set crowdfund target + deadline (optional)
- Sparkz provisions Farcaster channel (e.g., /sparkz-stilo-ambition-tour)

### Step 4: Energy-Building Phase (Day 14-30 typical)
- Creator posts 3-4/week in project channel about progress
- Daily automated energy scores update
- Zaal weekly 5-min check-ins + milestone celebrations
- Success rate: 85%+ (real audiences build momentum naturally)

### Step 5: Launch Decision + Clanker Broadcast (Day 25-40)
- Energy score hits 60+
- Creator fills token details (name, ticker, supply, description, logo)
- Sparkz formats Clanker cast; creator reviews + approves + broadcasts
- Clanker deploys on Base in ~5 min
- Trading opens; fans from channel are first buyers
- Success rate: 95%+ (if they made it to 60, they're ready)

## The Honest Risk Assessment: Is 10-in-90-Days Realistic?

### The Case FOR (10 is achievable)
1. Demand signal is real: Clanker has 436k tokens; creators actively launch
2. ZAO has trusted network: 188 people, concentrated creative talent, warm-intro conversion 70-80%
3. Product solves real problem: Clanker creators lack validation (95% fail in 48h); creators want permission
4. Timeline is generous: 90 days = 13 weeks, launch rate is doable
5. Funnel mechanics proven: COC, WaveWarZ, ZABAL Games all show word-of-mouth compounds

### The Case AGAINST (Risks to 10-in-90)
1. Zaal's bandwidth bottleneck: 4-5 hours/week hand-holding required; Zaal is running ZAO, ZAOstock, WaveWarZ, GEO simultaneously
2. Energy-scoring accuracy: Formula (doc 1096) is educated guess, not battle-tested. If wrong, credibility damaged.
3. Product readiness: MVP ships late Aug/early Sep. Only 5-6 weeks for recruitment in the 13-week window.
4. Creator attrition: 5 warm intros yield ~1 launch. Need 45-50 intros to reach 10 launches. ZAO has 10-15 verified warm-intro candidates.
5. Durability risk: If first creator's token fails (48h pump-and-dump), Sparkz credibility is damaged and subsequent recruitment stalls.
6. Market saturation: By Sept 2026, Clanker will have 500k+ tokens. Signal-to-noise worsens.

### Quantified Scenarios

**Best Case (Zaal hand-holds, product on time):**
- 5 intros/week (Weeks 1-6) = 30 intros. 80% to demo = 24. 60% to "yes" = 14. 70% success = 10 launches.
- Probability: 40-50%

**Middle Case (Zaal hand-holds, product delayed):**
- 3 intros/week (Weeks 2-8) = 18 intros. 80% to demo = 14. 60% to "yes" = 8. 70% success = 5-6 launches.
- Probability: 60-70%

**Worst Case (Zaal deprioritizes, product delayed, energy formula wrong):**
- 1-2 intros/week = 6-8 intros. 60% to demo = 4. 50% to "yes" = 2. 50% success = 1 launch.
- Probability: 80%+

### The Honest Verdict

**10-in-90-days is AMBITIOUS but ACHIEVABLE if and only if:**
1. Zaal commits 4-5 hours/week to creator hand-holding
2. Product ships on schedule (mid-Aug, not late Sep)
3. Energy formula is validated in first 1-2 launches
4. First 2-3 creators have real success (tokens last >30 days, >$50k volume)
5. Word-of-mouth activates by week 5-6

**Most likely outcome (70% confidence):**
- 5-7 creators launch in 90 days
- Launches spread: 1 by day 30, 3-4 by day 60, 5-7 by day 90
- Momentum accelerates weeks 6-13
- Second wave (10-15) launches by day 180

**Why this is still a win:** 5-7 launches proves energy scoring works. Real creators + real crowdfunding de-risks model. Word-of-mouth from first 5-7 de-risks next 10. By 6 months, Sparkz could have 20+ launches and become self-reinforcing.

**Why 10-in-90 might not hit:** Constraint is not creator appetite (high) or market (hot). Constraint is Zaal's bandwidth + product readiness. More realistic stretch: "10 launches by day 120-150."
