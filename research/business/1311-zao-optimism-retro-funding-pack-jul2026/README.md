## Section 3: Project Description (500 words, OP-optimized)

The ZAO is a music DAO that has been running Fractal Respect governance on Optimism Mainnet every week since October 2022. In 63+ consecutive on-chain weeks, 157 unique Respect holders have participated in governance through the OREC contract (0xcB05F9254765CA521F7698e61E0A6CA6456Be532), accumulating Respect through contribution and showing up — not through token purchase.

**What makes The ZAO different on Optimism:**

The ZAO runs the Fractal Respect model (pioneered by Eden Fractal), which means governance participation is earned through weekly attendance and peer evaluation, not bought. Every vote is an on-chain transaction. The 505 total governance transactions are the receipts. This is the only DAO we're aware of running continuous weekly on-chain Fractal governance on Optimism.

**WaveWarZ: The ZAO's Revenue Product**

The ZAO built WaveWarZ, a music prediction market on Solana where listeners bet SOL on which song wins a head-to-head battle. Since May 2025:
- 1,245 battles completed
- 524 SOL (~$39K) in total volume
- 921 unique songs submitted by independent artists
- 34 Audius-rostered artists rostered
- 9.07 SOL ($677) paid directly to artists
- $1,497 raised for HuRya charity via Community Battles

The "loser-earns" model means both the winning AND losing artist earn from every battle — a structural inversion of the winner-take-all music industry. WaveWarZ pays artists ~1.73% of battle volume per battle, compared to Spotify's $0.004/stream.

**Open-Source Infrastructure**

The ZAO has open-sourced its analytics layer:
- **wwtracker** (MIT license): 9 analytics modules for WaveWarZ — battle data, leaderboards, artist standings, rivalry tracking, economic breakdown. Live at wwtracker.com.
- **ZAOOS**: 1,310+ research documents in a public GitHub repo — all governance research, strategy docs, and ecosystem data publicly available.

**The ZAO as a DAO Case Study**

The ZAO is building toward being the definitive case study of a functioning music DAO: transparent governance, artist-first economics, open-source tooling, and weekly accountability. In contrast to DAOs that hold one governance vote per quarter or exist only as a token, The ZAO's model is:
- Show up every week
- Earn Respect through contribution, not capital
- Govern what you built, on-chain
- Publish everything

**Alignment with Optimism Values**

Optimism's thesis is that public goods are good for the world, and that work done in public — open-source, verifiable, replicable — deserves retroactive funding. The ZAO:
- Runs governance on Optimism Mainnet (not a fork or L3 — actual OP Mainnet)
- Open-sources its analytics and research
- Governs in public every week (X Space + on-chain transaction)
- Shares all findings freely in ZAOOS

This is exactly the kind of ecosystem participant Retro Funding was designed to reward.
