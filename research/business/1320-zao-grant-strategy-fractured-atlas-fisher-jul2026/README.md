## Section 1: PRIORITY 1 — Fractured Atlas Fiscal Sponsorship

**Why this is the most urgent item in the ZAOOS doc pile:**
Fractured Atlas is a nonprofit fiscal sponsor for artists and arts organizations. It allows you to receive tax-deductible donations and apply for grants (including the Fisher grant) that require nonprofit status. Without fiscal sponsorship, ZAO cannot apply for most arts grants, foundation money, or tax-deductible donations.

**What Fractured Atlas provides:**
- 501(c)(3) umbrella status for grant applications
- Ability to receive tax-deductible donations (critical for ZAOstock fundraising)
- Access to Fractured Atlas's grant database and opportunities
- Fiscal sponsor name on applications ("fiscally sponsored by Fractured Atlas")

**The Fisher grant specifically requires:** fiscal sponsorship or 501(c)(3) status

### How to Apply (step-by-step)

1. **Go to:** fracturedatlas.org
2. **Click:** "Get Fiscal Sponsorship" → "Apply Now"
3. **Application type:** Model A (Sponsored Project) — for artists/groups without their own nonprofit
4. **Cost:** $50 application fee + 8% of all revenue received through the sponsorship
5. **Timeline:** Typically 2-4 weeks for approval — apply TODAY to make Aug 15 Fisher deadline
6. **Approval probability:** High for documented arts organizations with track record
