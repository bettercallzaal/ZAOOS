## Sponsor Tiers

### TIER 1: Community Partner — $250
*Small business, indie crypto project, local Maine business*

**Benefits:**
- Logo on event website + social posts (4-6 posts)
- Name mention in all newsletters (3 issues pre-event)
- "Presented by [Sponsor]" mention in 1 X Space (pre-show or during)
- 2 VIP tickets
- Post-event recap mention

**Best for:** Ellsworth local businesses, small crypto projects, individual patrons.

---

### TIER 2: Artist Sponsor — $500
*Music companies, creator economy platforms, small Web3 projects*

**Benefits (includes all Tier 1) +:**
- Logo on artist reveal graphics (8 reveal posts, Aug 4-18)
- Mention in podcast appearances (doc 1328 — "this episode is supported by...")
- Artist "thank you to [Sponsor]" post from 1 ZAOstock artist's channel
- 4 VIP tickets
- Logo on day-of event materials (banner or flyer)

**Best for:** Audius, sound.xyz, creator economy tools, regional music businesses.

---

### TIER 3: Platform Sponsor — $1,000
*Mid-size crypto protocols, regional businesses, aligned foundations*

**Benefits (includes all Tier 2) +:**
- Co-branded artist reveal (one reveal post leads with "[Sponsor] presents Artist #N")
- Sponsor spotlight in newsletter (50-word feature)
- Logo on stage banner / backdrop
- 6 VIP tickets + 1 backstage pass
- Mentioned in every X/Farcaster post for 2 weeks leading up to show
- Dedicated post-event thank-you post (300+ words, cross-posted)

**Best for:** Fractal ecosystem projects, Optimism Foundation, Maine Arts Commission.

---

### TIER 4: Presenting Sponsor — $2,500+
*Major crypto protocols, regional banks, music industry organizations*

**Benefits (includes all Tier 3) +:**
- "ZAOstock 2026 presented by [Sponsor]" in all event branding
- Top billing on all marketing materials
- 30-second verbal thank-you at show opening (Zaal on mic)
- Custom integration: 1 branded battle on WaveWarZ ("The [Sponsor] Battle" week of Oct 1-2)
- 10 VIP tickets + 2 backstage passes
- Featured in press release (doc 1329 press release template)
- First right of refusal for ZAOstock 2027 presenting sponsorship

**Best for:** Franklin Savings Bank, Bangor Savings, Optimism Foundation, regional crypto companies.
