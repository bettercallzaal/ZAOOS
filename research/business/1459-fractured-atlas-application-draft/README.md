## Why Fractured Atlas First

Fractured Atlas (fracturedatlas.org) is a 501(c)(3) fiscal sponsor for performing arts projects. Once approved (~2 weeks), ZAO gets:
1. **Legal 501(c)(3) status** for grant applications (Fisher, NEA, MaineCF, all require it)
2. **Donation collection** capability with tax receipts
3. **Credibility signal** — Fractured Atlas is highly respected in arts community

**Application time:** ~30 minutes  
**Cost:** Free to apply; Fractured Atlas takes 8% of donations received

### Project Description (500–1,000 words)

```
ZAOstock 2026 is a free, all-ages outdoor music festival produced by The ZAO, a decentralized music community that has operated weekly music programming since March 2024.

**The Event**

ZAOstock takes place on Saturday, October 3, 2026 in Ellsworth, Maine. The festival features live performances by independent musicians affiliated with The ZAO's online music community, WaveWarZ — a music platform where artists compete through community voting.

We anticipate 75–150 in-person attendees, with additional virtual viewers through a live stream. All performances are free to attend. Optional paid ticket tiers ($15 supporter / $35 WaveWarZ Backer / $100 patron) are available for those who wish to contribute additional support.

**WaveWarZ: Our Music Platform**

The centerpiece of ZAOstock is the first-ever live WaveWarZ music battle on a public stage. WaveWarZ is a music battle platform where artists submit original tracks and community members vote on their favorites. The platform is distinguished by its "loser earns" mechanic: the artist who loses the battle still earns income from the betting pool. To date, WaveWarZ has processed 1,245 music battles and distributed $1,820 to losing artists — on the principle that musical participation has economic value regardless of whether it "wins."

At ZAOstock, this vote will happen live. The community in attendance becomes the jury. Both artists earn compensation from the battle pool regardless of the outcome.

**About The ZAO**

The ZAO is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) operating on the Optimism blockchain since March 2024. We have held 63 consecutive weekly community governance sessions — including during holidays and summer months — without interruption. Our community includes over 157 unique members who hold ZOR governance tokens earned through participation.

The ZAO operates the WaveWarZ music battle platform (Solana blockchain), maintains the ZAO OS — a public knowledge base of over 1,450 open-source community documents (Creative Commons CC-BY licensed) — and hosts Chain of Custody (COC) Concertz, a monthly music event series.

ZAOstock is our first in-person public event, bringing two years of online community building into a physical, accessible community gathering.

**Community Impact**

ZAOstock is explicitly designed to be free and accessible. There is no admission charge. Our community members span multiple states and countries; ZAOstock is designed to be the moment where the digital ZAO community meets in physical space and is visible to the broader Ellsworth-area public.

The festival will include a charitable giving component: 5% of paid ticket revenue will be donated to an Ellsworth-area music education nonprofit (charity partner to be confirmed by July 25, 2026).

**Why Fiscal Sponsorship**

The ZAO does not currently have 501(c)(3) status. Fiscal sponsorship through Fractured Atlas will allow us to:
1. Accept tax-deductible donations for ZAOstock production costs
2. Apply for grants from foundations that require 501(c)(3) status (including the Phyllis G. & Donald M. Fisher Foundation)
3. Issue official donation receipts to sponsors and supporters

We are building a sustainable community arts model. Fractured Atlas fiscal sponsorship is the first step in formalizing our relationship with the Maine arts funding ecosystem.
```
