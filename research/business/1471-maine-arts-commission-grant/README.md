## Overview

The Maine Arts Commission (MAC) is the state arts agency for Maine. MAC administers multiple grant programs that could fund ZAOstock and ZAO's broader music programming. This doc maps the relevant grant programs, their eligibility requirements, and how ZAO/ZAOstock qualifies.

**MAC website:** mainearts.maine.gov  
**Primary contact:** Maine Arts Commission, 193 State Street, Augusta, ME 04333-0025

---

## Grant Programs — ZAOstock Relevance

### 1. Creation to Presentation (C2P) — HIGH PRIORITY

**Purpose:** Supports Maine-based individual artists and organizations to create and present new work.

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Award range | $500–$5,000 (typical) |
| Eligibility | Maine-based artist OR Maine-based arts organization |
| Key requirement | Applicant must be Maine-based; ZAO must demonstrate Maine presence |
| Deadline | Typically spring (March-April) and fall (September) cycles |
| Match required | Often 1:1 match (ZAOstock ticket revenue qualifies) |

**ZAOstock fit:** ZAOstock (Oct 3, Ellsworth, ME) is a Creation to Presentation project — a new community music event in Maine with original programming (WaveWarZ live battle, ZABAL musician performances). If Zaal is classified as a Maine-based artist/presenter, this is a strong fit.

**Key question:** Is Zaal's Maine address (or Ellsworth as project location) sufficient for Maine residency eligibility? Ask MAC directly.

**Application approach:**
- Frame ZAOstock as a Maine community music event presenting new web3-native art forms
- Emphasize loser-earns as a novel economic structure for Maine musicians
- Reference the Ellsworth permit + Heart of Ellsworth partnership
- Budget: request $2,000–$3,000 toward PA rental (doc 1461) + artist fees

### 2. Maine Arts Commission — Traditional Arts

**Purpose:** Supports Maine traditional arts practitioners and their apprentices.

**ZAOstock fit:** Lower fit — this is focused on folk/traditional Maine art forms, not electronic/web3 music. Skip unless specific traditional artists are performing.

---

### 3. Artists Residency Program

**Purpose:** Residency support for Maine artists creating new work.

**ZAOstock fit:** Medium — if ZAOstock is framed as a residency-style creative period for ZABAL musicians (Sep 1–Oct 3 = 4-week pre-festival residency). Stretch argument.

---

### 4. Maine Arts Commission — Organizational Support

**Purpose:** General operating support for Maine-based arts organizations.

**ZAOstock fit:** BLOCKED — requires organizational 501(c)(3) status. ZAO does not have a Maine 501(c)(3). GATED on Fractured Atlas fiscal sponsorship (doc 1459).

**Action:** If Fractured Atlas approves (doc 1459, expected Aug 5-15), ZAO becomes eligible for MAC organizational grants under Fractured Atlas's 501(c)(3) umbrella. Ask Fractured Atlas if their fiscal sponsorship extends MAC eligibility.

---

### 5. Maine Community Foundation — Hancock County Arts

**Note:** MaineCF is a separate entity from MAC (not state-administered), but often co-cited. The Hancock County Arts Fund makes grants of $500–$2,500 to arts organizations and projects in Hancock County, Maine (Ellsworth is the Hancock County seat). See doc 1262 for MaineCF research.
