## What Fractured Atlas Is

Fractured Atlas is a fiscal sponsor for arts projects. As a FA-sponsored project, ZAO can:
- Accept tax-deductible donations
- Apply for grants that require 501(c)(3) status (using FA's)
- Issue donation receipts
- Hold funds in a FA-managed account

**FA does NOT:** control ZAO's creative decisions, require approval for ZAO's activities, or take a large cut (they charge 8% platform fee on donations raised through them).

**Application URL:** apply.fracturedatlas.org
