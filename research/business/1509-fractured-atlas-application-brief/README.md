## What Is Fractured Atlas

Fractured Atlas is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that provides fiscal sponsorship services to independent artists and arts organizations. Fiscal sponsorship means donors can make **tax-deductible donations** to ZAO through Fractured Atlas, even though ZAO itself is not a 501(c)(3).

**Why this matters for ZAO:**
- Accepts donations for ZAOstock and ZABAL S2 through an IRS-compliant channel
- Strengthens MAC grant application (fiscal sponsor = evidence of arts org legitimacy)
- Strengthens Fisher grant application (fiscal sponsor = accepted alternative to 501(c)(3))
- Makes it possible to receive larger institutional donations (the $500+ range where donors want tax deduction)
- Required by some grant programs as an alternative to direct 501(c)(3) status

**Application URL:** apply.fracturedatlas.org

---

## Decision: Which Fiscal Sponsorship Model

Fractured Atlas offers two models. Choose one before starting the application.

| Model | Name | Best For | Fee | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Model A** | Comprehensive Fiscal Sponsorship | ZAOstock + ZABAL (ongoing) | 7% of funds raised | 2-4 weeks approval |
| **Model C** | Pass-Through | One-time projects | 7% of funds raised | 1-2 weeks approval |

**Recommendation: Model A (Comprehensive)**
- ZAO is ongoing, not a one-time project
- Model A allows multiple campaigns (ZAOstock + ZABAL S2 + future seasons)
- Model A provides a dedicated donation page on fracturedatlas.org/donate
