## What Fiscal Sponsorship Unlocks

Fractured Atlas is a 501(c)(3) arts service organization that provides fiscal sponsorship to independent arts projects. FA fiscal sponsorship means:

1. **Donors to ZAOstock/ZAO can deduct contributions** (tax-deductible donations via FA)
2. **Fisher Fund application is eligible** — Fisher requires a fiscal sponsor or 501(c)(3) (Aug 15 deadline)
3. **MAC grant application is eligible** — Maine Arts Commission grants require fiscal sponsor or 501(c)(3)
4. **Eventbrite and donation receipts are official** — FA issues receipts on ZAO's behalf

Without FA fiscal sponsorship, ZAO cannot accept tax-deductible donations and Fisher/MAC grant eligibility is uncertain.

## Contingency: If FA Denies

FA might deny if:
- Project is primarily technology/crypto (not arts-forward enough)
- Location not sufficiently arts-based

**Response if denied:**
1. Reapply next FA cycle with ZAOstock-focused framing (arts events = more likely to qualify)
2. For Fisher Aug 15: apply as individual arts project (Zaal as individual artist/creator)
3. For MAC: check if MAC accepts individual artist applications without fiscal sponsor
4. For donations: use Open Collective as fiscal sponsor alternative (lower bar for tech-adjacent projects)

**Open Collective alternative:**
- opencollective.com — fiscal hosting for projects
- Lower bar than FA (accepts more project types)
- 5% platform fee vs FA's annual flat fee
- Donations may not be tax-deductible (Open Collective hosts are 501(c)(3) but check specific host)
