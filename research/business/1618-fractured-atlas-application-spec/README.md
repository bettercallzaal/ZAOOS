## Why Fractured Atlas

Fractured Atlas (fracturedatlas.org) is the largest arts fiscal sponsor in the US. Fiscal sponsorship means:
- ZAO is not a 501(c)(3), but donations and grants received through FA are tax-deductible to the donor/grantor
- FA holds funds on behalf of ZAO and disburses to ZAO after project completion (minus ~8% admin fee)
- FA membership is required BEFORE submitting applications that require fiscal sponsor proof

**Specifically blocks:**
1. Fisher Fund — requires fiscal sponsor letter before applying (Aug 15 deadline)
2. Maine Arts Commission — requires 501(c)(3) or fiscal sponsor (Sep 1 deadline)

**Cost:** FA application fee is free. FA admin fee (~8%) is taken from grants received, not upfront.
