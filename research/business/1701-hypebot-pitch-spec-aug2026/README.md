## Pitch Angles (Pick One per Contact)

### Angle A: Artist Economics (Recommended for Main Pitch)
"What if losing a music competition paid you?"

Focus: the structural inversion — losing earns you money, automatically. No claim. No middleman. The comparison to 9,000–93,000 Spotify streams per battle is the hook for music-industry readers.

### Angle B: The Free Festival Model
"A DAO is running a free public music festival in Maine on October 3 — with on-stage automatic artist payouts"

Focus: ZAOstock is the tangible real-world event. A music DAO funding a free IRL festival with automatic blockchain payouts is genuinely unusual. This angle works for music-business readers who are skeptical of blockchain/crypto but interested in new festival economics.

### Angle C: The Governance Innovation
"157 music fans have voted every week for 100+ consecutive weeks on which artists battle — no quorum failures"

Focus: the governance track record. Hypebot occasionally covers DAO governance when it has direct music-business relevance. The 100+ consecutive sessions with zero quorum failures is rare for any organization.
