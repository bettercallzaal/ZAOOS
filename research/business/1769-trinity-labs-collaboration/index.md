## What Trinity Labs Is

Trinity Labs operates two distinct entities:

### 1. Trinity Labs (Web3 Aggregation Platform)
**Domain:** trinitylabs.gitbook.io | **Focus:** DeFi, token launches, community governance

A blockchain aggregation platform built around three core components:

- **TrinityLaunch** - NFT issuance and token launch mechanism
- **TrinitySwap** - Trading using their proprietary BAMM (Batch Automated Market Maker) protocol
- **TrinityDAO** - Multi-level governance for community-driven decision-making

Emerging modules (TrinityMKT, TrinityCA, TrinityAI) point toward an expanding vision combining chain abstraction, market infrastructure, and AI-integrated services.

**Mission:** "Build a fair, secure, and sustainable aggregation platform that meets the diverse needs of global users and promotes the healthy development of the crypto market."

**Market focus:** VC tokens, MEME tokens, fair NFT distribution; rewards-based community engagement; token scarcity mechanisms.

### 2. Trinity AI Labs (AI-Native Software Development IDE)
**Domain:** trinityailabs.com | **Focus:** AI agents, developer tooling, collaborative engineering

A shared IDE workspace where teams and fleets of AI agents plan, build, review, and ship code in parallel.

**Pipeline:** Describe (natural language) → Plan (AI structures work) → Execute (5 parallel AI workers) → Ship (auto-PR + releases)

**Supported AI models:** Claude, GPT, DeepSeek, Kimi, GLM, Qwen, Ollama (bring-your-own-API-key model)

**Status:** Beta, 678K lines of code, 169+ days of active development, $10/seat/month
