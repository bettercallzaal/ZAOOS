## 1. What The Arena Is Precisely (May 2026)

### Platform Architecture

The Arena is a **full-stack SocialFi platform on Avalanche C-Chain** that combines:

- **arena.social** - Creator-focused social feed with bonding-curve ticket system
- **arena.trade** - Native DEX for meme coin launches and trading
- **Arena Stages** - Live audio/video streaming with real-time crypto tipping
- **Arena Groups** - Community-owned token launch capability
- **Arcade2Earn integration** (as of April 2026) - GameFi reward infrastructure bridge

**Core mechanic:** Users buy creator "tickets" (shares) via quadratic bonding curves. Each ticket purchase = investment in a creator's influence. Holders unlock private chats, exclusive content, and livestream access. Creators earn 70% of all secondary-trade fees automatically and perpetually.

### SocialFi Model vs. Competitors

Unlike **friend.tech** (dead as of 2024-2025, raising $0 in recent months):
- The Arena ships **public feeds** (not just private chats)
- **Lower Avalanche fees** (~$0.01 vs Ethereum/Base $0.50+)
- **Integrated DEX** for instant meme coin trading
- **Arena Stages** for live monetization
- **TVL is 3x friend.tech**
- Community ownership: fees split 30% ecosystem fund + 30-70% creator+platform

## 4. Creator Economics: The 70% Claim Verified

### How Creator Earnings Work

**Ticket trading generates fees:**
- Up to **10% fee per ticket transaction** (buyer pays)
- Creator receives **70%** of that fee stream
- Platform + ecosystem get 30%

**Example:** If 100 tickets sell at increasing prices (bonding curve), total transaction value = $1,000 → 10% fee = $100 → Creator gets $70, Platform gets $30.

**Perpetual nature:** Creator receives royalties on **every secondary trade forever**, not just initial sale. This is the key difference from traditional creator platforms (YouTube, Spotify) where creators earn once and platform controls the rest.

### Other Revenue Streams

1. **Tipping:** Direct AVAX sent to creator wallet (no fee)
2. **Arena Stages (livestreams):** 
   - Audience can pay admission to join
   - Audience can tip during stream
   - Creator keeps 80%+ (estimates vary by feature)
3. **Groups/community tokens:** Creator can launch own token with airdrop mechanics
4. **Referrals:** 2.6% rebate on trades by referred users' networks

### Typical Creator Earnings Profile

- **Small creator (100-1K tickets held):** $50-500/month (tipping + slow trade royalties)
- **Mid creator (1K-5K tickets held):** $500-5K/month (if community actively trades)
- **Large creator (5K+ tickets held):** $5K-50K+/month (if ticket trades are frequent/high-value)

**Risk:** Earnings are 100% dependent on ticket holder demand. If community stops trading or posts drop, revenue falls to zero.
