## 1. Ava Labs - The Company

**Founder & CEO: Emin Gun Sirer**
- Turkish-American computer scientist, Cornell Associate Professor since 2001
- Developed Avalanche Consensus (breakthrough in consensus protocols post-Bitcoin)
- Founded Ava Labs 2018 to commercialize Avalanche mainnet (launched Sep 2020)
- Vision: "Frictionless world by redefining how people build and use finance applications"
- Decentralization principle: "We will show the world how to do this in the crypto spirit." Emphasis on permissionless validators, no gatekeeping.

**Leadership Team (verified contacts)**
- **John Wu** - President, Business Development
- **Luigi D'Onorio DeMeo** - Chief Operating Officer, Institutional & Capital Markets

- **Lee Schneider** - General Counsel
- **Akash Gupta** - VP Core
- **Nick Mussallem** - AvaCloud CEO

**Company Stats**
- Headquarters: Brooklyn, New York (also Miami office)
- Employees: 194 (as of 2026-05-02 filing)
- Total Funding: $640M across 8 rounds
- Status: Profitable operation, not fundraising

**Strategic Positioning**
- Not a monolithic L1 competitor. Ava Labs builds the *infrastructure layer* — tools, documentation, interoperability (Teleporter).
- Multi-chain vision: Primary Network (X/P/C-Chains) + 66+ sovereign L1s sharing security model + cross-L1 messaging.
- Institutional adoption: J.P. Morgan, Citi, WisdomTree, FIFA, MapleStory, Gunzilla (Off The Grid) all run Avalanche L1s.

**Takeaway for ZAO:** Ava Labs prioritizes enterprise and gaming. A music community is niche. But if ZAO ever launches a music-specific L1 or tokens, John Nahas (CBO) is the contact point for ecosystem alignment.

## 7. Avalanche9000 & L1 Strategy (Architecture Context)

**The Upgrade (Etna, Dec 2024)**
- Renamed Subnets to "Layer 1s" (L1s) to emphasize sovereignty
- Dropped validator stake requirement from 2000 AVAX (~$560K) to 0
- Introduced pay-as-you-go model: ~1.3 AVAX/month (~$10-15) per validator
- Enabled custom validator sets (L1s no longer require Primary Network validators)
- Enabled Teleporter (native cross-L1 messaging, atomic composability)

**Why This Matters**
- **Economics:** L1 launch cost dropped from ~$560K to near-zero (~$50 one-time Fuji testnet deploy + ~$15/month per validator)
- **Accessibility:** 100+ new L1s launched in 2025. ZAO could launch a music L1 for ~$5-10K/year operating cost (for 1-2 validators)
- **Interoperability:** Beam (gaming), Dexalot (DeFi orderbook), Henesys (MapleStory), FIFA (collectibles) all L1s with seamless Teleporter bridges to C-Chain liquidity

**Live L1s (Notable)**
- Beam (gaming): 4.5M wallet addresses, 100+ game studios, Merit Circle DAO
- Dexalot (trading): On-chain central limit order book, low latency
- DeFi Kingdoms (Crystalvale): GameFi pioneer, multi-chain (Kaia, Metis, AVAX)
- Off The Grid (Gunzilla): AAA extraction royale, 10M+ players console/PC
- MapleStory Universe (Henesys): MMORPG on Avalanche L1
- FIFA World Cup NFT L1: Permissioned, KYC-enabled for global sport fans

**Takeaway for ZAO:**
- If ZAO ever wants a dedicated music/artist L1 (tokenized drops, artist royalties, streaming micro-payments), **cost is now ~$100-150K/year to run 2-3 validators**, not $500K+.
- Timing: Not immediate, but a 2027-2028 possibility if ZAOstock or artist roster spins out.
- Architecture: C-Chain for initial dApps (music discovery, artist profiles, SANG token), L1 later if throughput demands justify it.

## 10. Community Sentiment & Realistic Expectations

### What Works
- Authentic founder presence (Zaal personally answering Discord questions builds trust faster than CMO)
- Shipping on testnet first (transparency > hype)
- Small, tight community over vanity follower counts
- Long-term IRL events (consistency signals reliability)
- Technical depth (explaining *why* your design choice matters to Avalanche)

### What Doesn't
- "We're the Spotify of blockchain" pitches (too generic, nobody cares)
- Follower farming or bot engagement (detected immediately, reputation wreck)
- Asking for airdrops before shipping (will be mocked)
- Claiming "ecosystem first" while centralized (hypocrisy + transparency loss)
- Silent launches (zero Community Rounds or founding team outreach = zero traction)

### Market Reality
- 80+ L1s competing for mindshare, but most are gaming/DeFi/RWA
- Music/creator tooling = novel narrative (green field, less competition)
- Avalanche Foundation = actively looking for *differentiation*, not cookie-cutter apps
- Timing: Post-Avalanche9000, institutions + games getting ink. Creator economy has window, not crowded yet

### Adoption Curve (Evidence)
- Beam gaming L1: 4.5M wallets, 100+ game studios (2 years post-launch)
- Dexalot orderbook: 1000s of daily traders (high-intent users)
- MapleStory L1: millions of game transactions (mainstream IP, Avalanche trust came first)
