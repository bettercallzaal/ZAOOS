## Observations & Limitations

1. **Leaderboard opacity**: The Arena's public leaderboard for top creators/ticket holders is not accessible via web search. Direct login required to see live rankings. RootData lists "Top Holders" but does not name them publicly.

2. **Music adoption gap**: Despite posture as "creator-first," research found zero prominent musicians with public profiles on Arena as of May 2026. Platform dominated by crypto traders, DAO builders, meme-coin communities, and influencers. This is a genuine market gap for ZAO.

3. **Cast3 as discovery mechanism**: The largest third-party integration is a micro-influencer payment network (Cast3). This suggests Arena's user acquisition relies on paid amplification, not organic virality. Insight: Cost-per-user acquisition may be higher than visible metrics suggest.

4. **Champions tier unlocking**: Arena Champions status requires staking ARENA, creating a financial barrier. This concentrates governance and airdrop upside among deep pocketed / early hodlers. Community perception TBD.

5. **Sustainability questions**: Platform peaked at $55.5M market cap (June 2025); currently at $5M (May 2026). Tipping revenue growing 340%, but absolute fee generation ($7.65K AVAX in H1 2026) is still modest. Long-term sustainability depends on token utility and retention.

6. **Team size**: Sources list 13 employees (as of April 2026), down 5.9% YoY. Small team; concentrates operational risk on Jason Desimone's vision.
