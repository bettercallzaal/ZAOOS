**Realistic May 2026 estimate:** 50K-150K daily active addresses, or 5K-15K daily active users (assuming 10-15 addresses per active user average, typical for DeFi). Platform likely lost 50-70% of 2025 peak activity.

## 10. The Honest Verdict

**The Arena is a quiet platform in May 2026, not a fading one.**

- It remains **live and functional** with a core user base of ~200K.
- **Recent strategic moves (iOS, GameFi)** show active product development, not abandonment.
- **Token and economic metrics are severely depressed** (market cap down 92%, volume down 85%+), indicating either a collapse in user monetization or a shift away from speculation toward utility.
- **The platform has lost 70-90% of its trading volume and creator cohort** vs. mid-2025 peak, but retained the user base count (suggesting dormant wallets, not churn).
- **No public DAU/MAU data exists**, making it impossible to confirm whether 200K users are active or registered-but-dormant.
- **The profitability claim (83% of traders profitable)** from June 2025 would likely be much lower today, given token price collapse and volume contraction.

**In crypto terms:** The Arena is neither "live, busy" nor "fading." It's in a **"recovery phase"** - built platform, lost momentum, attempting re-engagement via mobile + gaming. Success is uncertain; execution will determine whether it rebounds to $100M+ quarterly volume or continues contracting toward zombie status (< $1M DAV, < 10K DAU).
