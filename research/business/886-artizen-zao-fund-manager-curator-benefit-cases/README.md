## 1. The ZAO as Fund Manager

The ZAO already runs the **ZAO Fund for Emerging Culture** (Season 6, `artizen.thezao.com` -> the S6 fund;
public roster at `zaoos.com/artizen`). Pool seeded at ~$10k (the first $10k came from Artizen). Roster = 32
projects. Ranked top-tier among funds (#2 by pool at capture, ~#9 by drive raise).

What a fund manager actually controls (from [885](../885-artizen-roles-and-capital-flow/)):

- **Capital in:** deposits go to the fund pool - 90% match, 10% end-of-season prize for the fund's top project.
  Money never goes straight to a project. Match unlocks $1-per-$1 only as curated projects SELL Artifacts.
- **The real lever is distribution, not capital.** A $100k fund that curates projects nobody buys deploys
  almost nothing. The ZAO's edge is the opposite of most funds: 188 members + WaveWarZ / ZABAL / Farcaster
  reach can actually drive Artifact sales + boosts for the projects it backs. That is the moat (doc 844).
- **Five fund-manager jobs:**
  1. **Curate** - decide which projects enter the fund (see section 2).
  2. **Deploy match** - rally the ZAO community to buy curated projects' $10 Artifacts so the pool actually flows.
  3. **Cross-back** - get ZAO projects curated into OTHER funds too, so each $1 sale stacks match from many pools.
  4. **Spotlight** - the 32-post daily-spotlight series (kit) + comments/engagement; visibility = sales.
  5. **Grow the pool** - recruit sponsors/partners to add match capital behind the ZAO's curation taste.
- **Standing strategy (doc 850 superseded, per [[project_zartizen_repo]]):** do NOT rush to spin up a second
  fund (the "ZAO Festivals Fund"). A 79-fund scan killed the "music white space" - We're Loud, Global Music,
  and Greenpill already fund music events, and 6 ZAO projects are already stacked in Global Music. Move now =
  **curate ZAOstock / Zaoville / ecosystem projects INTO existing funds**, run the ZAO Fund well, decide on a
  second fund later. The `/proposal` page puts this to the team.

## 2. The ZAO as Curator

Curator and Fund are different hats (885): the **Fund** holds the money; the **Curator** chooses who gets
backed and rallies people around the cause. The ZAO wears both. As Curator the ZAO:

- **Selects on values** - "emerging culture": independent music, regen/impact, community-owned, web3-native art.
  The ZAO's taste IS the product; curate winners and the fund's reputation compounds.
- **Can run curation by community vote.** On the call Zaal tested exactly this: once a project applies, the
  ZAO can curate it and let members use **ZAO votes (yes/no)** to decide inclusion. That turns curation into a
  participation surface, not top-down gatekeeping - on-brand for The ZAO and a reason for members to show up.
- **Is judged by its picks.** Curate InfiniteZero (#1 overall, already in the fund) and other risers and the
  ZAO Fund reads as a tastemaker; that pulls more strong projects to seek ZAO curation.
- **Curation priority order for the ZAO Fund:**
  1. ZAO ecosystem projects that can actually sell (COC Concertz, PolyRaiders, WaveWarZ Africa, ZAOstock).
  2. Aligned emerging-culture externals already proving traction (InfiniteZero, Edge City, regen music).
  3. Relationship channels - accepted grantees become warm ties (doc 843 already maps several).

## 3. COC Concertz - benefit case

What it is: a 50/50 ZAO x COC joint venture live-music concert line; Thy Revolution leads it; The ZAO does
NOT own it (it "sits in the middle of both brands") - see [[project_coc_concertz_framing]], doc 866.

How Artizen helps:
- COC Concertz becomes a **Project** on Artizen. Each concert can mint $10 Artifacts - the live recording,
  a photo edition, a poster drop, a ticket-linked collectible. Every $1 sold unlocks $1 match from the ZAO
  Fund, and stacks if also curated into Global Music / We're Loud / Greenpill (multi-fund stacking, doc 885).
- It is non-dilutive, 0% platform fee, 100% to the creator - real money for an independent concert series that
  otherwise lives on ticket margin.
- Distribution fit is strong: Thy Rev + COC have a crowd, the ZAO community boosts + buys; concert attendees
  convert to Artifact buyers. Artizen's model literally rewards "can you rally a crowd" - a concert series can.

Framing caution: respect the partnership. The message is "your project, the ZAO Fund backs you" - it
strengthens Thy Rev's independent ownership (Zaal's explicit intent), never "the ZAO's COC."

Move: Thy Rev applies COC Concertz as a Project -> ZAO curates it (member vote) -> mint one Artifact per
concert -> ZAO + COC rally buyers/boosts -> cross-submit to Global Music for stacked match.

## 7. Cross-backing / stacking synthesis - the "ZAO bloc"

Multi-fund stacking (885) is the whole game. A project curated into N funds gets matched by all N on each $1
sale. So the ZAO's play is not one fund in isolation - it is a coordinated bloc:

- **Curate every ZAO ecosystem project into the ZAO Fund AND into aligned externals** (Global Music, We're
  Loud, Greenpill, Bonfires). COC Concertz, PolyRaiders, WaveWarZ Africa, ZAOstock each want 2-4 backing funds.
- **Point one community at all of them.** 188 ZAO members + COC + PolyRaiders + WaveWarZ Africa audiences,
  pooled and aimed at whichever ZAO-curated project is live during a high-multiplier drive, deploy match no
  solo fund can. Each member who buys/boosts lifts the whole bloc's standings together.
- **Reciprocity is the glue:** PolyRaiders backs COC, COC backs WaveWarZ Africa, etc. The bloc raises every
  member's SCORE at once, which is what the leaderboard rewards.

Net: the ZAO's edge on Artizen is not capital, it is a coordinated crowd. The fund-manager job is to organize
that crowd around a rotating spotlight, drive by drive.

## 8. Boost + ranking strategy - how the ZAO Fund climbs

Ranking axis = **SCORE** (per 844: raised x boosts; formula unverified, flagged in 885). Both sales AND boosts
matter, so the strategy is broad participation, not a few big checks:

- **Sign-ups are free boost.** Each new member signup = +100 boost; +10 boost per $1 contributed. 188 members
  signed up = a large baseline boost pool most funds cannot match. Step 1 is simply getting members onto Artizen.
- **Broad beats deep.** Many members each buying a $10 Artifact generates more boosts (and more genuine
  supporters) than one large donor. Mobilize the crowd.
- **Time pushes to the multiplier.** Buy/boost when a 2x or 3x drive is live so each dollar deploys more match.
- **Engagement = visibility.** Comments and active presence in the ecosystem raise placement; Zaal flagged this
  on the call ("the comments are really important too, and being active in the ecosystem").
- **Mind the settlement window.** SCORE/boost move live; the prize settles at drive/season end - do not read the
  mid-week prize number as final.
- **Close the formula gap first.** Confirm the real Boost Score formula (Artizen support or the
  `artizen-fund` GitHub org) before heavily optimizing boosting - 885 flagged it [FAILED] / unverified.
