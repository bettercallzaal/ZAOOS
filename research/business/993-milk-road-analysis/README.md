## How it grew (the engine)
- **Paid acquisition = 85%+ of growth.** Facebook + TikTok ads at ~$1.00-1.30 per subscriber; ~150k+ from Facebook alone. Memes + social-proof creative. They ran it like a startup - once CAC < LTV, they scaled hard.
- **Referral loop (dead simple).** ONE referral unlocked a high-value, zero-cost report ("What 12 Crypto Whales are Betting On"). One referral, not five - roughly doubled the viral coefficient vs competitors. Automated via Beehiiv.
- **The $1M public wallet stunt.** Put $1M in a publicly viewable wallet, let readers watch + vote on trades. Built trust faster than any "about" page and created a daily reason to open. Signups kept multiplying even when it was down 70% - transparency was the moat.
- **Speed over perfection.** Launched on Beehiiv no-code, iterated live.

## Business model
- **Primary:** native sponsorships written in-voice ($10-25k/send at peak).
- **Secondary:** affiliate (hardware wallets, tax software) + PRO premium tier (weekly reports, analyst portfolios, private community, AMAs, portfolio tools; $1 trial advertised, exact price undisclosed).
- Free daily (5 days/week, 5-min read) funds the top of funnel; PRO monetizes the core.

## Content + voice
- "Smart friend explaining crypto in plain English." Casual, funny, anti-jargon, personality-driven (Morning Brew template for crypto). Mascot: the Milk Man.
- Sections: Milkbusters (myth-busting), Quick Bites (3-5 headlines), Meme of the Day, Fear & Greed index, Vitalik pic of the day. Reader polls in every issue.
- Multi-format: email (primary) + The Milk Road Show (daily 20-min pod/YouTube) + Macro + AI shows on Spotify/Apple/YouTube.
