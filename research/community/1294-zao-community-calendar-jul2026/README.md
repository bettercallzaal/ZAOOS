## Event Profiles

---

### WaveWarZ Battle Space
**Type:** Daily live X Space  
**Schedule:** Monday – Friday, 8:30 PM EST  
**Platform:** X @wavewarz  
**Duration:** 45–60 minutes  
**Format:** Zaal or a guest host covers active battles, leaderboard updates, artist spotlights, and community predictions  
**Who should attend:** WaveWarZ fans, bettors, artists, curious music fans  
**How to find it:** Follow @wavewarz on X — Space starts automatically at 8:30 PM EST  

**What makes it unique:** Real-time prediction market commentary. You can bet on a battle WHILE discussing it on the Space.

**Full format guide:** doc 1292

### ZAO Fractal Governance Session
**Type:** Weekly governance  
**Schedule:** Thursdays (time TBD — varies; check ZAO Discord for current schedule)  
**Platform:** Discord (voice channels) + frapps.xyz (for peer ranking)  
**Duration:** ~60–90 minutes  
**Format:**
1. Intro + announcements (15 min)
2. Breakout groups — small peer ranking rounds (30–45 min)
3. Results announced, Respect scores calculated
4. On-chain settlement queued for OREC (settles within 72 hours)

**Who should attend:** ZAO members and anyone interested in joining the community  
**How to join:** Join the ZAO Discord (link in ZAO newsletter) and show up in the #fractal channel  
**What you need:** No wallet required to attend and observe. To earn Respect, you must participate in peer ranking.

**On-chain record:** 63 of 100+ sessions are settled on Optimism blockchain (Blockscout-verifiable).

**Full documentation:** doc 1280 (governance explainer), docs 1200, 1202 (on-chain facts)

### COC Concertz (Circles of Community Concertz)
**Type:** Monthly virtual concert  
**Schedule:** Monthly — typically 3rd or 4th Friday of the month, 4:00 PM EST  
**Platform:** Spatial.io (Dope Stilo Music Club — browser-accessible, no download) + Twitch twitch.tv/bettercallzaal  
**Duration:** 2–3 hours  
**Format:** DJ Zaal + WaveWarZ battle-circuit artists performing live, with live WaveWarZ battles running during the show  
**Who should attend:** Anyone interested in music + blockchain, COC Concertz fans, WaveWarZ artists  
**How to join:** Head to the Spatial.io link (posted to @wavewarz and @bettercallzaal before each show)  

**Show #7 (July 18, 2026):** TONIGHT — open access (no wallet gate). First show with live WaveWarZ battles during performance.

**Full series record:** doc 1256 (shows 1–7)  
**Show #7 brief:** doc 1284
