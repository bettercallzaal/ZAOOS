1. **Onchain-native audience** — Farcaster users are more likely to use WaveWarZ (SOL on Solana) and ZAO Fractal (Optimism) than general social media users

5. **ZOE can post autonomously** — ZOE can cast from @bettercallzaal and potentially @wavewarz without requiring Zaal to manually post

## Channel Strategy

### /zao Channel

**Current state:** 93 followers, 4 members  
**Goal:** 500 followers, 20 active members posting, by end of Q3 2026

**Tactics (no GATED approval needed):**

1. **Post the ZAO newsletter recap to /zao daily** — every newsletter edition gets a 3-sentence cast in /zao with the link. This is what the 93 followers subscribed to see.

2. **Cross-post Fractal session announcements** — before and after every Thursday Fractal, cast in /zao: "ZAO Fractal is tonight → [time]. Earn Respect." and "Last night's Fractal results: [top 3 contributors]."

3. **WaveWarZ battle highlights** — when a notable battle concludes (big upset, charity milestone, artist hitting a record), cast it in /zao: "GodclouD just won their 8th straight battle on WaveWarZ. 71.4% win rate. wavewarz.info"

4. **Invite builders to /zao** — at the end of every ZABAL Games workshop, remind builders: "Join the /zao channel on Farcaster for daily ZAO updates."

5. **Reply to WaveWarZ-adjacent casts** — when anyone in the Farcaster ecosystem mentions music, prediction markets, Solana, or DAO governance, @bettercallzaal replies with relevant ZAO context.

## Growth Tactics That Work on Farcaster

Based on ZAO's onchain-native audience, these formats consistently drive follows and engagement on Warpcast:

1. **Number posts** — "1,289 battles. 878 SOL. 921 songs. WaveWarZ." Short, verifiable stats drive recast.
2. **Milestone celebrations** — "100 weeks of ZAO Fractal governance. No missed weeks. Onchain." Milestone posts get shared.
3. **Artist spotlights** — tag the artist: "@[audius-handle-if-on-farcaster] just crossed 10 battles on WaveWarZ. Total SOL: [X]."
4. **Frame embeds** — when WW Mini App is live, embed the battle frame directly in the cast
5. **/zao-exclusive content** — post something in /zao that doesn't go to X first (e.g., "this is the early look before we announce on X")

---

## What NOT to Do

- **Don't repost everything from X verbatim** — Farcaster users can tell when content is cross-posted without thought
- **Don't post promotional content without value** — "Check out WaveWarZ!" alone does nothing; pair it with a stat or story
- **Don't neglect the /zao channel** — the 93 followers joined to see ZAO content, not just @bettercallzaal's personal feed
- **Don't automate opinion casts** — ZOE should only automate data-based posts (stats, milestones, results), not commentary
