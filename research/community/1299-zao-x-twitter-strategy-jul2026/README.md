## 2. Account Roles + Content Split

Each account serves a distinct audience. Don't cross-post blindly.

### @bettercallzaal (Zaal's personal)
**Audience:** web3 builders, DAO founders, creators who want to build
**Tone:** builder-to-builder, warm, tight, factual (per doc 898)
**Content split:**
- 40% build-in-public updates (ZAOOS research shipped, WW stats, ZABAL milestones)
- 30% ecosystem amplification (quote-tweet builders and partners with a real insight added)
- 20% X Space announcements and live recaps
- 10% ZAO governance and Fractal milestones

### @WaveWarZ (WaveWarZ product)
**Audience:** music fans, Solana traders, streaming-fatigue artists, casual web3
**Tone:** hype-forward, data-driven, artist-first
**Content split:**
- 50% battle highlights and results (stat + artist name + outcome)
- 25% artist spotlights (quote artist win, tag their handle)
- 15% platform milestones (new stat records, charity totals)
- 10% product announcements (new battle types, WW mini app)

### @theZAOdao (governance layer)
**Audience:** DAO researchers, Optimism ecosystem, grant reviewers, protocol investors
**Tone:** formal, evidence-based, institutional
**Content split:**
- 60% governance milestones (on-chain weeks, Respect holder counts)
- 25% research drops (link to new ZAOOS docs)
- 15% ecosystem citations (when ZAO is referenced by others)

## 5. WaveWarZ Battle Space X Spaces Protocol

The Mon–Fri 8:30PM EST X Space is ZAO's highest-reach live channel. Doc 1292 covers the full format. X strategy addition:

**Pre-Space (30 min before):**
- @bettercallzaal posts: "Going live in 30 minutes. [Battle of the day stats]. Join the Space: [link]"
- @WaveWarZ posts: "BATTLE SPACE starts in 30. [One compelling stat or rivalry teaser]."

**During Space:**
- Pin a tweet with the Space link on both accounts while live

**Post-Space (within 30 min of end):**
- @bettercallzaal posts a 3-tweet recap thread: (1) best moment, (2) stat of the night, (3) CTA to join tomorrow
- @WaveWarZ posts: final battle result if not already posted

**Why this matters:** X algorithmically surfaces Spaces to non-followers. The pre-Space tweet + the recap thread are the highest-reach moments of the day.

## 8. Growth Tactics (Ranked by Impact, No GATED Required)

1. **Reply to artists who win WW**: Immediately after a battle ends, @WaveWarZ tags the winner with their stats. This is the highest-converting growth action and takes 30 seconds per battle.

2. **Quote-tweet music industry conversations**: When a journalist or music executive tweets about Spotify payouts or streaming royalties, @WaveWarZ quote-tweets with a single WaveWarZ stat. Specific target: tweets about the $0.004/stream figure.

3. **Mutual amplification with partners**: @WaveWarZ retweets ZABAL Games launch posts; @bettercallzaal retweets WaveWarZ milestones. Builds cross-account following.

4. **Thread-format research drops**: When a major ZAOOS doc ships, @bettercallzaal turns the key findings into a 5-tweet thread with one insight per tweet. Threads get 4-7x more reach than single tweets.

5. **Archive clip tweets**: Take one 60-second clip from a past Battle Space, upload it as a native video, post from @WaveWarZ. Algorithm heavily favors native video over external links.
