## 5-Topic Outline (45 min)

### Topic 1 — What WaveWarZ Is (10 min)
Zaal explains: a prediction market for music battles on Solana. Artists submit songs, listeners pick winners, SOL trades on outcomes. Artists get paid instantly (3% protocol fee, rest to creator).

**Eduard's angle:** "So it's like a Battle of the Bands that actually pays people in real-time?"

**Numbers to cite:** 1,245 battles, 921 songs, 524 SOL total volume, $1,497 to charity.

---

### Topic 2 — WaveWarZ vs Streaming (10 min)
The 1.73% vs Spotify comparison. Streaming pays 0.003–0.005 cents/play = 600x more payout on WaveWarZ if your song wins.

**Eduard can share his perspective:** What does a web3 music creator see that Spotify misses?

**ZAO angle:** We don't replace streaming. We give artists a second revenue stream that rewards quality + community engagement.

---

### Topic 3 — COC Concertz + the ZAO Ecosystem (8 min)
How COC Concertz connects: WaveWarZ artists battle → best ones get featured in COC shows → concert archive goes on Arweave.

**Tonight is relevant:** COC #7 is happening July 18. Eduard can give context about why this is unique.

**ZAOstock angle:** Live events where WaveWarZ battles happen physically on stage.

---

### Topic 4 — ZABAL Games + How to Join (7 min)
Eduard can ask: "If someone listening wants to be part of this, what's the path?"

**Answer:** (1) Submit a song to WaveWarZ. (2) If you're a builder, apply to ZABAL Games Cohort 2 (details after August). (3) If you're a listener, vote in battles at wwtracker.xyz.

---

### Topic 5 — Open Q&A (15 min)
Any listener can join the Space and ask questions. Eduard moderates.

Likely questions:
- "How is WaveWarZ different from Sound.xyz or Catalog?" → No pre-mint, battle-based curation
- "Can I bet on songs I haven't heard?" → Yes, but preview is available
- "How do you get artists to join?" → Direct DMs + ZAO Fractal community
