## How to Use This Document

This is the master content calendar for all ZAO channels: @bettercallzaal (X), @wavewarz (X), /zao (Farcaster), @wavewarz (Farcaster), Telegram, and Newsletter.

**ZOE manages this calendar.** Each week, ZOE:
1. Checks which content moments are coming in the next 7 days
2. Drafts auto-posts for scheduled events
3. Alerts Zaal via Telegram for posts that require Zaal's voice or live info

**Legend:**
- `[ZOE]` — ZOE drafts and posts automatically
- `[ZAAL]` — Zaal posts personally (Zaal's voice, live info, or decisions)
- `[GATED]` — requires a prior decision or external input before posting
- `⚡ URGENT` — time-sensitive, within 48 hours

## ZOE Scheduling Protocol

For each major content moment (events with [ZOE] tag):
1. ZOE pre-schedules posts in Paragraph/Buffer/direct API 1 week in advance
2. ZOE alerts Zaal T-24h: "Tomorrow at 9am: ZAOstock ticket launch posts. Check draft: [link]"
3. ZOE monitors engagement for first 2 hours after post — if no reply/repost, ZOE alerts Zaal: "Engagement low on [post] — consider boosting"
