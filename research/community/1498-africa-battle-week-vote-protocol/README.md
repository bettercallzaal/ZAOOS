# 1498 — Africa Battle Week: ZOR Holder Vote Protocol (Sep 26 Community Battle)

**Type:** GOVERNANCE-PROTOCOL  
**Topic:** Community  
**Status:** CHARITY PARTNER VOTE → ZOR holders vote by Jul 25. Battle execution Sep 26.

---

## What This Is

Africa Battle Week is ZAO's first geographically-themed community battle — a US-based WaveWarZ veteran vs. a West African artist, executed Sep 26 as a WaveWarZ community battle. The full payout (loser share + winner share) is donated to a charity partner voted on by ZOR holders.

This doc covers:
1. How to nominate and select the charity partner (by Jul 25)
2. How to run the ZOR holder vote
3. How to book the West African artist
4. How to execute the battle on Sep 26

## Part 2 — ZOR Holder Vote (Jul 24–25, 48 Hours)

### Vote Post (ZOE posts on Jul 24 at 12PM)

**Farcaster /zao:**
> **[VOTE] Africa Battle Week Charity Partner**
> 
> ZOR holders: vote on which organization receives 100% of the Sep 26 community battle payout.
> 
> Your options:
> 1. [OPTION A — fill from nominations]
> 2. [OPTION B]
> 3. [OPTION C]
> 
> To vote: Reply with the number of your choice. ZOR holders only (check our pinned post if you need to verify).
> 
> Vote closes Jul 25 at 12PM. Lazy consensus — if only 1 option gets votes, it wins by default. Minimum 3 voters for a valid vote; if under 3, Zaal selects.
> 
> ⚡ This is an on-chain community. The winner gets real money.

**Telegram:**
> 🗳️ VOTE: Africa Battle Week Charity Partner
> 
> ZOR holders — pick the organization that receives 100% of the Sep 26 WaveWarZ battle payout.
> 
> Options:
> 1. [OPTION A]
> 2. [OPTION B]  
> 3. [OPTION C]
> 
> Reply with your choice number by Jul 25 at 12PM.

### Vote Count Rules

- Zaal tallies replies manually on Jul 25 at 12PM
- Winning option is highest vote count
- Tie: Zaal breaks tie
- If fewer than 3 ZOR holders vote: Zaal selects charity unilaterally (document the fallback in session notes)
- If a nominated charity fails verification after vote: use runner-up

## Part 4 — Battle Execution (Sep 26)

### Pre-Battle Protocol (Sep 19–25)

| Task | Owner | Due |
|---|---|---|
| Battle created in WaveWarZ platform | Zaal | Sep 19 |
| Battle ID confirmed | Zaal | Sep 19 |

| Both artist tracks submitted | Artists + ZOE DM | Sep 22 |

### Announcement Post (Sep 22 — ZOE Posts)

**@wavewarz X:**
> 🌍 Africa Battle Week starts Sep 26.
> 
> US: [ARTIST A]
> West Africa: [ARTIST B]
> 
> 100% of the payout goes to [CHARITY NAME] — voted on by our community.
> 
> Even the loser earns. That's WaveWarZ.
> 
> Battle: wavewarz.info/battle/[ID]
> Vote opens Sep 26. Runs 48h.

### Battle Day (Sep 26)

- Battle opens at 12PM EST
- Voting window: 48 hours (closes Sep 28 12PM)
- ZOE posts mid-vote update at Sep 27 12PM (vote status)
- Battle closes Sep 28 12PM; results auto-distributed by WaveWarZ smart contract

### Charity Payout (Sep 28–Oct 1)

- WaveWarZ distributes winner and loser shares to artist wallets automatically
- Charity share: Zaal manually sends SOL to charity wallet (or converts to USD + PayPal)
- Exact SOL amount depends on battle pool (community battles: ~0.1 SOL entry minimum × number of voters)
- ZOE posts proof of transaction on X + Farcaster + Telegram

**Payout post template:**
> 🌍 Africa Battle Week: Done.
> 
> [WINNER] won. [LOSER] earned too — that's WaveWarZ.
> 
> [TOTAL SOL] donated to [CHARITY NAME]. Proof: [TX HASH on Solana explorer]
> 
> This is what ZAO governance looks like — community picks the charity, smart contract sends the money.
> 
> See you at ZAOstock Oct 3 for the live version.
