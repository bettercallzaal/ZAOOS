## Path A: WaveWarZ Fan / Trader

WaveWarZ is a prediction market for music battles on Solana. You stake SOL on artists. The artist you back doesn't have to win — the artist who loses still earns automatically.

### What You Need
- A Solana wallet (Phantom is recommended)
- Some SOL (as little as 0.01 SOL to start)
- wavewarz.info

### Getting Started
1. **Go to wavewarz.info** — look for an active MAIN or Quick battle
2. **Connect your Phantom wallet** (top right)
3. **Listen to both artists** — each battle features two tracks you can play before voting
4. **Back the artist you think will win** — stake any amount in SOL
5. **Wait for the battle to close** — battles are time-limited (MAIN battles close at show time for COC Concertz events; Quick battles close on a fixed timer)
6. **Settlement is automatic** — when the battle closes, payouts go to:
   - Winning traders: 80% of the losing pool
   - Winning artist: 10% of the losing pool
   - Losing artist: 10% of the winning pool (this is what makes WaveWarZ different)

### What to Watch
- **Battle volume** — how much SOL is staked tells you how contested the battle is
- **Vote split** — the % each artist is getting from traders
- **Losing artist payout** — even if your artist loses, they still earn SOL automatically

### Next Steps
- Follow @bettercallzaal on X for battle announcements
- Join the /wavewarz Farcaster channel
- Subscribe to the ZAO Newsletter (paragraph.xyz/@thezao)
- Attend a COC Concertz show (monthly, check wavewarz.info for schedule)

## Path B: Artist

If you're an independent musician, there are two ways to participate in WaveWarZ:

### Option 1: Quick Battle (Anytime)
Quick battles run continuously. Any artist can be in one — ask in Telegram or DM @bettercallzaal.

**What happens:**
- Your track goes head-to-head with another artist's track
- Fans stake SOL on each artist
- When the battle closes, both artists earn SOL automatically
- No labels. No streaming royalty minimum. Just automatic payout.

**Requirements:**
- A Solana wallet (to receive payouts)
- A track on Audius (free; Audius is the primary ZAO Music platform)
- Agreement to the WaveWarZ artist terms (on wavewarz.info)

### Option 2: MAIN Battle at COC Concertz
MAIN battles feature ZOR holders voting on artists in advance. Artists in MAIN battles:
- Perform live (or remotely via stream) at the COC Concertz show
- Have a dedicated battle at the show with live audience participation
- Receive significantly higher trading volume than Quick battles

**How to get into a MAIN battle:**
- Artists are nominated and voted on by ZOR holders in the weekly Fractal session
- Best path: participate in the community, get known, get nominated
- Or: apply to ZABAL (see below) — ZABAL alumni often get MAIN battle slots

### Option 3: ZABAL (ZAO Artist Builder Accelerator Lab)

ZABAL is a 12-week cohort for independent artists:
- Learn on-chain music tools: Audius, 0xSplits, WaveWarZ, Arweave
- Release music on the ZAO Music pipeline (Audius → DistroKid → Sound.xyz)
- Build audience using WaveWarZ battles as release events
- Govern: participate in weekly Fractal sessions, earn ZOR

**ZABAL S2 (2026):**
- Applications open Jul 21, 2026
- Deadline Aug 4, 2026
- Starts Sep 1, ends Nov 21
- Track A: Artist (music creation + release)
- Track B: Builder (on-chain tools + ZAO ecosystem)

**Apply:** Apply at the ZABAL page on zaoos.com

## Path C: Governance Member (ZOR Holder)

ZOR is the ZAO governance token. It's soulbound — it can't be bought or transferred. You earn it by participating in the weekly Fractal Democracy session and being recognized by peers.

### What ZOR Holders Do
- Vote on WaveWarZ MAIN battle artists at COC Concertz shows
- Vote on community proposals (events, partnerships, charity selection)
- Vote on governance parameters (ZABAL curriculum, Africa Battle Week charity)
- Set the direction of ZAO as a DAO

### How to Earn ZOR
1. **Join the weekly Fractal session** — every Friday/Saturday, announced on X, Telegram, and Farcaster
2. **Participate in good faith** — show up, contribute to the group discussion
3. **Be recognized by peers** — the Fractal process has groups rank each other by contribution
4. **Receive ZOR** — if recognized, ZOR is sent to your Optimism Mainnet wallet after the session

### Requirements
- An Optimism Mainnet wallet (MetaMask, Rainbow, or any Ethereum wallet)
- Enough ETH on Optimism for gas (very small — Optimism L2 gas is cents)
- Being a real community member (not a bot — ZOR is earned, not bought)

### Current ZOR Holders (Jul 2026)
- 157 wallets hold ZOR on Optimism Mainnet
- These 157 people have collectively governed ZAO through 100+ consecutive weekly sessions with zero quorum failures

## What ZAO Is NOT

**ZAO is not:**
- A record label (we don't own your music or take publishing)
- An NFT platform (WaveWarZ doesn't use NFTs — it uses prediction market stakes)
- A paid community (everything is free or earned through participation)
- A streaming service (we use Audius for streaming, which is separate)
- Anonymous-only (ZAO community uses real handles; many members use real names)

## FAQ for New Members

**"How do I make money on WaveWarZ?"**
You back artists by staking SOL. If your artist wins, your stake grows. There's no guaranteed return — this is real money at risk.

**"Do I need crypto to participate?"**
For WaveWarZ voting and trading: yes (SOL). For attending ZAOstock (Oct 3): no, it's free. For ZABAL S2: no direct cost, but you'll need a Solana wallet to receive any payouts.

**"Who runs ZAO?"**
ZOR holders govern ZAO through weekly Fractal sessions. Zaal Panthaki (BetterCallZaal) co-founded ZAO and is the primary operator, but governance decisions are made collectively by ZOR holders.

**"Is ZAO safe?"**
ZAO never holds your funds. When you stake on WaveWarZ, your SOL goes into smart contracts on Solana — not into ZAO's wallet. Payouts are automatic and on-chain. ZAO cannot block or redirect your payout.

**"Where can I verify ZAO's track record?"**
- WaveWarZ stats: wavewarz.info/api/public/stats
- Governance sessions: OREC contract on Optimistic Etherscan (`0xcB05F9254765CA521F7698e61E0A6CA6456Be532`)
- ZAOOS knowledge base: github.com/bettercallzaal/ZAOOS (1,600+ CC-BY docs)
