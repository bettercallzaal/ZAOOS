**ZAO Update — July 2026**

Here's what's live, what's coming, and what you can do right now.

---

**WaveWarZ: 1,245 battles settled**

Since we launched, WaveWarZ has run 1,245 music battles on Solana. Both the winning and losing artist earn automatically when each battle closes — no claim, no label, no wait.

Stats as of July 2026:
- 523.991 SOL in total battle volume
- 9.09 SOL paid directly to artists (including losing artists)
- 127.343 SOL claimed by winning traders
- 36 community battles organized by ZAO members

The mechanic: fans stake SOL on artists. Winning traders take 80% of the opposing pool. Winning artist takes 10%. **Losing artist takes 10% of the winning pool — automatically.**

[Battle now → wavewarz.info]
