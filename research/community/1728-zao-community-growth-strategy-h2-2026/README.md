## Growth Levers by Event

### Jul 21 Launch Cluster (Newsletter + ZAOstock + ZABAL + COC #8)

**Growth play:** Four simultaneous announces create a "ZAO is active" signal. Each announcement should drive subscribers to the newsletter.

**Specific tactic:** Eventbrite (ZAOstock) collects emails → import into newsletter. Every RSVP is a potential subscriber. ZOE prompts Zaal to add Eventbrite subscribers to newsletter list after event.

**Target:** +10-20 newsletter subscribers from Jul 21 cluster.

### Africa Battle Week (Sep 22-26)

**Growth play:** The largest ZAO content event of the year. Five days of content = five days of amplification opportunity.

**Specific tactics:**
1. Daily battle result posts on X + Farcaster (ZOE auto-posts)
2. Charity angle drives shares: "ZOR holders voted on which charity gets paid. The vote is live."
3. African music press coverage (doc 1715) amplifies to new audiences outside ZAO's current base
4. Battle participants (artists + their fanbases) are new-to-ZAO audiences
5. Charity TX on Sep 26 is the "proof" post that gets reshared outside the ZAO base

**Target:** +30-50 /wavewarz followers, +20 newsletter subscribers during ABW week.

### ZAOstock (Oct 3)

**Growth play:** The first IRL ZAO event. Physical attendance creates emotional connection that drives online follow.

**Specific tactics:**
1. Event sign-up collects emails → newsletter imports
2. QR code at event linking to wavewarz.info + ZAO Telegram
3. Live battle result shown on screen → "go to wavewarz.info right now" verbal CTA
4. Post-event: Zaal posts from @bettercallzaal (personal account) → highest engagement for ZAO content
5. Press coverage (from doc 1715 and similar) links back to wavewarz.info

**Target:** +50 Telegram subscribers, +30 newsletter subscribers, +100 wavewarz.info signups from the Oct 3 event.

### ZABAL S2 (Sep 1 – Nov 21)

**Growth play:** ZABAL cohort = 10-20 new builders who each have their own audiences. Each cohort member cross-posts their ZAO work to their existing communities.

**Specific tactics:**
1. Cohort members post their builds to Farcaster, X, Discord (their home communities)
2. ZABAL graduation (Nov 21) is a showcase → new audience exposure
3. ZABAL builder applications drive awareness among builders who don't convert but follow ZAO

**Target:** +20 /zabal Farcaster followers, each cohort member brings 3-5 followers to /zao or /wavewarz.

## Channel Strategy

### Keep

**ZAO Telegram Public:** Primary announcement channel for the core community. Fastest for time-sensitive ops (battle results, governance votes). Keep.

**Farcaster /wavewarz:** The most important growth channel for WaveWarZ. Crypto-native audience who already understand staking mechanics. Invest here.

**Newsletter:** Highest-quality subscriber relationship. Email subscribers convert to ticket buyers (ZAOstock) and ZABAL applicants. Invest here.

**@bettercallzaal:** Zaal's personal account is the highest-engagement ZAO touchpoint. Keep it human — Zaal writes, ZOE never posts here.

### Grow

**Farcaster /zao:** Governance audience. Grow by having ZOR holders post regularly. Each Fractal session recap post should be cast to /zao.

**@wavewarzofficial:** ZOE auto-posts battle results. Volume of content here is limited by battle frequency. Grow by running more battles.

### Don't Prioritize

**Discord:** ZAO has no active Discord presence. Building one from scratch is a distraction. The Telegram Ops channel covers internal coordination. Don't open Discord unless a specific use case demands it.

**TikTok/Instagram:** Not ZAO's audience. COC Concertz show clips might warrant a clip account eventually — but not in H2 2026.

## ZOR Holder Growth Path

The ZOR holder pipeline is the most important long-term growth vector — these are the people who vote on ZAO's direction.

**Current pipeline:**
1. Discover ZAO via WaveWarZ, Farcaster, X, or personal connection
2. Join ZAO Telegram Public
3. Attend first Fractal session
4. Become regular Fractal attendee (3-5 sessions)
5. Accumulate OG Respect score
6. Qualify for ZOR (8-12+ sessions)
7. Receive ZOR and become governance participant

**Bottlenecks:**
- Step 3 → 4 (first-time → regular): Most first-timers don't come back. Need better onboarding hook.
- Step 4 → 5 (regular → OG accumulation): Takes time — the barrier is intentional.

**ZOE action:** After every Fractal session, ZOE DMs all first-time attendees with doc 1693 (community onboarding guide) and a "here's what you need to know about the path to ZOR" message referencing doc 1719 (ZOR holder guide).

**Target:** 10 new ZOR holders by Nov 21 (157 → 167).

## Growth Risks

**Risk 1: ZAOstock doesn't convert to online community**  
Attending a live event doesn't automatically mean people follow ZAO online. Mitigate: QR code, verbal CTAs, post-event email sequence, ZOE sends "thanks for coming to ZAOstock" welcome sequence to Eventbrite attendees.

**Risk 2: Africa Battle Week doesn't get press coverage**  
If no African music outlet covers ABW, the growth is limited to ZAO's existing base. Mitigate: send Tier 2 pitches early (Aug 15), work with artist contacts to co-promote, charity angle is a strong standalone story even without press.

**Risk 3: ZABAL S2 cohort is small**  
If fewer than 5 ZABAL builders join, the cross-pollination effect is minimal. Mitigate: ZABAL S2 applications close Aug 4 — if under 10 applications by Jul 28, ZOE alerts Zaal to run an outreach sprint.

**Risk 4: Newsletter platform not set up by Jul 21**  
Newsletter Issue 1 can't go out without a platform. Mitigate: if Substack/Paragraph not ready, use Mailchimp free tier or plain BCC email. Don't miss the Jul 21 date.
