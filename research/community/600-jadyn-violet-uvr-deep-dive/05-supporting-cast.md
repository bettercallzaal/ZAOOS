# 600.05 — The Supporting Cast

> **Supplements:** `600-jadyn-violet-uvr-deep-dive/README.md`
> **Goal:** The people in Jadyn Violet's story — family, the early UVR inner circle, the music collaborators, the Twitch network, the web3 peers, and the ZAO connection. A biography needs more than its subject.

A note on pseudonymity: much of the web3 cast goes by handles only. Where a real name is not public, that is stated, not guessed.

---

## Family

### The father — the foundational influence

Jadyn's father has been a professional singer his entire life. In Jadyn's own words: "My music making journey started 6 years ago, but stems from my early childhood having a father that was a singer his entire life. Growing up with this energy unknowingly sparked my interests in the arts at an early age." His name is not public. His genre (Indian classical, Bollywood playback, devotional, Western) is not specified in any source. He is alive and still a stated source of inspiration.

The father is the subject of Jadyn's November 2025 short film, **"Why Does The World Hate Indians?"** A line attributed to the film: "We're celebrated when we become doctors or engineers, but the moment we step into a creative field, we're told we don't belong." (This quote surfaced in one research pass and could not be independently re-verified — treat as likely-but-soft.) The film is documented on Instagram; runtime, full platform, and reception beyond the IG post are not established.

### The mother — the dropout conflict

The mother is the figure of the central family conflict. Jadyn: "My mom became super disappointed, believing that I had given up in life and was not interested in working hard anymore." His framing is the first-gen immigrant-household script — academic achievement and a secure career as the expectation, the arts as forbidden. He had to argue his case: "I explained that I could accomplish great things in whatever I fully set my mind to." Whether the relationship has healed is not public. Her name is not public.

### The household

First-generation Indian American, central New Jersey (Somerset area). The recurring childhood image: dressing as Michael Jackson and dancing "Billie Jean" at family functions, "with their celebration and encouragement." No siblings are mentioned in any source. The immigrant-household pressure is the gravitational center of the early biography.

### The breakup

A breakup spurred his very first song ("when a breakup spurred the need for catharsis, Jadyn looked to music as a creative outlet and produced his first song"). The 2023 track "Under The Same Stars" is explicitly about it: "It has been almost 3 years since I left someone that meant the world to me" — note he says *he* left. The 2024 track "FIRE" he described as "a song where I explore our unusual attachment to toxic relationships in life." The person's identity is not public and should stay that way; current relationship status is undisclosed.

## The Early UVR Inner Circle

### The first rave

The first Underground Violet Rave: **Gold Sounds Bar, 44 Wilson Avenue, Brooklyn, during NFT NYC (June 2022)** — 250+ attendees from South America, Canada, California, Europe. DJs on the first lineup: **Twinny Twin, Mutahadir ("Muta"), and BABYOIL**. (This corrects the "September 2022" date that appeared in an earlier doc — NFT NYC 2022 was June 20-23.) Who in the Twitter Spaces community first suggested throwing an IRL rave is not credited to a single name — NOISE DAO only says community members were "spurring on the suggestion."

### Co-hosts and regulars

- **Tricky** — a recurring early co-host in UVR Twitter Spaces; also appears in WaveWarZ / ZAO-ecosystem spaces.
- **Manin Gauba** — co-host of later UVR events (listed alongside Jadyn and Mutahadir on a Nov 2023 Lu.ma event page).
- **Recurring community fixtures** in the Spaces archive: Shiba King, Bricks, "max pretends," Donna, Peanut, Wayak, Satwik. Roles undocumented, but they recur enough to read as the core community.

## The Twitch Network

- **GoldCity (Ryan)** — 18, started streaming May 31 2025 (got into it after meeting Funny Mike at Dream Con). The Aug 30 2025 viral incident: his parents found him streaming on Hollywood Boulevard after he'd skipped football practice; Jadyn was co-streaming and is documented mentoring him and defending his streaming ambition to the parents, with the mother reportedly promising to "invest" in Ryan's career. Covered by Dexerto and Primetimer. Jadyn plays a big-brother / mentor role here.
- **Flashieboi** — Twitch streamer, "Road to 10k"; co-streamed "Song Battles" with Jadyn (June 5 2025). Real name not public.

The Twitch network is thin and opportunity-driven — a new network built mostly from scratch in 2025, not the old web3 crowd ported over.

## The ZAO / Zaal Connection

The relationship is **associational, not relational** — peers in the same web3-music ecosystem, not close collaborators.

Evidence:
- Jadyn appears on The ZAO's site as an associated-community founder: `thezao.com/community/jadyn-violet`.
- Confirmed co-appearance: a Nov 10 2023 Twitter Space, "WEB3 | +C.O.C ft | Jadyn Violet | MUSIC NFTs | sponsor META-BUILDERS," with Zaal, Thy Revolution, The Bacon Sandwich (COC co-founder), and others. Hurric4n3ike is a recurring shared node.
- No joint projects, shared events, mutual features, or 2024-2026 collaboration found.

**Implication for the biography:** Zaal is writing about a peer he knows from the ecosystem, not a close friend or collaborator. An interview is plausible to land on "fellow web3-music builder" terms, but the biography should not assume deep personal familiarity. This is also a [feedback_no_unconfirmed_anchor_partners] consideration — frame the relationship accurately as "associated community," nothing more, in any public material.
