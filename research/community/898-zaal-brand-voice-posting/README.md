# 898 - Zaal / ZABAL Gamez brand voice for posts

> **Goal:** Document Zaal's actual posting voice so every cast, post, and Firefly cross-post sounds like him - not generic, not corporate. Pairs with the mechanics in [Doc 897](../897-zao-social-posting-playbook/).

## Key Decisions

| Decision | Call | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| Voice in five words | **Warm, tight, factual, generous, builder-to-builder** | These are the recurring traits across Zaal's real casts. Every draft filters through them. A consistent voice drives ~33% higher recognition and ~20% more engagement (Monolit). |
| Default opener | `ZM` on its own line, then a blank line, then a hook | ZM is the ZAO greeting and Zaal's standing rule. After ZM, lead with a hook, never the title/logistics (Doc 897). |
| Signature close | A short rally line - "Let's cook." / "we bring the build." / "we got you." | Zaal ends warm and forward-leaning, builder-to-builder, never salesy. |
| Recurring tag line | "Free, live, recorded." and "Recorded and kept, so missing it live never means missing it." | His proof+reassurance line - keep it. |
| Hard rules | No emojis. No em dashes (hyphens only). No crypto/web3/onchain jargon ("digital creators" / "builders"). "100+" never a specific member count. | Per CLAUDE.md brand rules - non-negotiable. |
| Person + tense | First person, present, talking to one person | "I'm hosting...", "come up and ask anything", "this is your session." |

## Findings

### We are / We are not

| We are | We are not |
|--------|-----------|
| Warm, generous, drops-the-ladder | Hypey, salesy, FOMO-baiting |
| Tight, plainspoken, factual | Wordy, corporate, "we are pleased to announce" |
| Direct builder-to-builder | Condescending or jargon-heavy |
| Confident, states things plainly | Emoji-spammy, exclamation-heavy |

### Signature moves (pulled from real casts)

- **Time-hook opener** (promos): `Tonight, 8pm EST: AZKAL on building FlowStage.` then one outcome line, then `Free, live, recorded. RSVP: <luma>`.
- **Host-forward framing:** "I'm hosting a ZABAL Gamez Spaces fireside AMA with @farcaster. Open mic, come up and ask anything."
- **Self-qualifier line** so the right person leans in: "If you're just starting out, this is your session."
- **Contrast framing:** "Los Fomos brings the culture and heat, we bring the build."
- **Reddit-style AMA mechanic:** "Reply with your questions below - the most-liked ones are the ones we open with. Likes are the upvotes."
- **Outcome over feature list:** say what someone walks away able to DO, never a comma-list of features.

### Phrase library (use)

cook / let's cook, build / come build, tap in, own your [X], come up, drop your question, the mic is open, kept, we got you, ship, builders, digital creators, Free live recorded, let's get into it.

### Never-say list

emojis; em dashes; crypto / web3 / onchain / mint / token-gating-as-jargon (translate to plain language); a specific ZAO member number (use "100+"); hype words (game-changer, insane, revolutionary, huge); AI-tells (delve, in today's landscape, leverage [as verb], unlock potential, "we are excited/pleased to"); "link in bio"; "join us for an amazing".

### Register by platform (voice stays, tone flexes)

- **Farcaster:** short, channel-native (post in /zabal). 320-byte cast / 1024-byte longcast for announcements. Link can sit in the body. Tag the guest by handle so they get pinged + reshare.
- **X:** hook in the first 1-2 lines; put the RSVP link in the FIRST REPLY (body links cut reach ~50% - Doc 897).
- **Tone shifts:** launches = more energy; recaps = measured-proud + credit people by name; asks/AMAs = generous and inviting.

### Template - workshop announcement (his voice)

```
ZM

[Hook - a tension or a bold line, not the title]

[Time] EST: [Guest] from @[org] on [topic, as an outcome]. [one line on who it's for / what they walk away with]

Free, live, recorded.
RSVP: <link>   (on X, move this to the first reply)
```
