## 2. Fractured Atlas -- Fiscal Sponsorship

**What it is:** Fractured Atlas is a 501(c)(3) arts service organization that provides fiscal sponsorship to independent arts projects. With fiscal sponsorship, ZAOstock can:
- Accept tax-deductible donations from individuals
- Apply to grants that require 501(c)(3) status (including most Maine arts grants + the traditional grant world)
- Run crowdfunding campaigns via their Fundraising by Fractured Atlas platform

**Cost:** $10/month membership + 8% fee on fiscally-sponsored transactions. No minimum revenue. Cancel anytime.

**Eligibility:** Project must have clear artistic merit. ZAOstock qualifies: live music festival with local artists, free public admission, community-building.

**What Fractured Atlas unlocks:**
| Grant Program | Amount | Now Accessible Via FA |
|---------------|--------|----------------------|
| Maine Expansion Arts Fund | $5,000-$25,000 | Yes (needs FA) |
| NEFA (New England Foundation for the Arts) | $10,000-$25,000 | Yes (needs FA) |
| New Music USA | $5,000-$20,000 | Yes (needs FA) |
| MaineCF program grants | $2,500-$15,000 | Varies -- some need FA |
| Heart of Ellsworth Downtown Grants | $500-$5,000 | NOT needed (these fund via LLC direct) |

**How to apply:**
1. Go to fracturedatlas.org -- apply for fiscal sponsorship ($10/mo membership first)
2. Complete the project application: name, description, budget, timeline
3. Approval typically 5-10 business days
4. Once approved: your Fractured Atlas project page accepts donations; Fractured Atlas handles 501(c)(3) compliance

**Timeline for ZAOstock Oct 3:**
- Apply NOW (July 17): approved ~July 27
- Fundraising opens: July 27
- Campaign window: July 27 - Oct 3 (10 weeks)

**GATED -- Zaal must:** submit the Fractured Atlas application at fracturedatlas.org. Claude cannot submit on your behalf.

## 3. Maine Community Foundation (MaineCF) -- Hancock County

**What it is:** Maine Community Foundation manages $800M+ in assets for Maine communities. Their county-level program (Hancock County Community Fund) provides grants to nonprofit organizations AND fiscally-sponsored projects serving Hancock County residents.

**Eligibility for ZAOstock:**
- Location: Ellsworth is in Hancock County -- fully eligible
- Fiscal sponsorship required: most MaineCF grants require 501(c)(3) or fiscal sponsor (Fractured Atlas satisfies this)
- Project type: arts, music, community gatherings all align with MaineCF's cultural priorities

**Key MaineCF programs for ZAOstock:**
| Program | Amount | Cycle | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|-------|
| Hancock County Community Fund | $2,500-$10,000 | Typically spring + fall cycles | Community projects in Hancock County |
| Maine Community Foundation General Fund | $5,000-$15,000 | Rolling | Statewide; arts + culture category |
| Field of Interest Funds | Varies | Rolling | Donor-advised; some target Ellsworth/Hancock specifically |

**How to apply:**
1. Set up Fractured Atlas FIRST (enables full program access)
2. Go to mainecf.org -- navigate to Grants section
3. Check the current open grant cycles for Hancock County / Hancock County Community Fund
4. Application typically requires: project description, budget, timeline, community impact narrative, fiscal sponsor letter from Fractured Atlas

**What ZAOstock should emphasize in the application:**
- Local Ellsworth anchor: Franklin Street Parklet, Art of Ellsworth / Maine Craft Weekend
- Free public event: no ticket cost, accessible to all
- Local artist showcase: Maine-based musicians + WaveWarZ battle-circuit artists
- Economic activation: daytime foot traffic + after-party at Black Moon Public House
- Community partnership: Heart of Ellsworth alignment, City of Ellsworth co-sponsorship
- Digital-physical bridge: livestream reaches ZAO's 188-member global network -- Hancock County on the world stage

**Realistic ask for Year 1:** $2,500-$5,000 (seed round). This is a new event; first-year asks should be modest and relationship-building focused.
