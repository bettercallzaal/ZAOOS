## 1. Fractured Atlas — Project Description

*Use this in the "Project Description" field of the Fractured Atlas fiscal sponsorship application.*

---

**ZAOstock** is a free, community-produced independent music festival taking place October 3, 2026 (12PM–6PM) at the Franklin Street Parklet in Ellsworth, Maine — a public green space in the heart of Hancock County's downtown arts district during Art of Ellsworth weekend and Maine Craft Weekend.

ZAOstock is produced by The ZAO (ZTalent Artist Organization), a decentralized artist collective and impact network dedicated to returning profit, data, and IP ownership to independent musicians. Founded by Zaal Panthaki (BetterCallZaal) and rooted in the Ellsworth / Maine music community, The ZAO has operated continuously for 3+ years: publishing 400+ newsletter editions, holding 100+ consecutive weekly governance sessions with 188+ active members globally, hosting 7 virtual concerts (March 2025–July 2026), and building WaveWarZ — an onchain music battle platform that has processed $40,000+ in on-chain trades and raised $1,497 for HuRya Empowerment Foundation through charity benefit battles.

ZAOstock Year 1 will showcase local Maine artists alongside WaveWarZ's national battle circuit artists, feature live WaveWarZ battles on a public stage, and stream the event to the ZAO's global audience. The after-party continues at Black Moon Public House. The event is free to all attendees — no ticket required.

**The goal:** Put Hancock County on the global independent music map by merging the physical energy of a Maine community gathering with the reach of web3 music culture.

## 2. Fractured Atlas — Impact Statement

*Use this in the "Impact" or "Community Benefit" field of the Fractured Atlas application.*

---

ZAOstock creates direct economic and cultural value for Ellsworth and Hancock County in four ways:

1. **Local artist income:** All performing artists receive direct payment from event revenue. WaveWarZ's onchain payment model enables instant, transparent artist compensation.
2. **Downtown foot traffic:** A free public event at Franklin Street Parklet brings foot traffic to Ellsworth's downtown business district on a Saturday afternoon, supporting the Heart of Ellsworth Main Street America initiative.
3. **Global visibility for local artists:** The ZAO's 188-member global community, 400+ newsletter editions, and WaveWarZ's social reach (X, YouTube, Farcaster) will amplify Ellsworth artists to audiences far beyond Maine.
4. **Community gathering:** ZAOstock is designed as a recurring annual anchor event — Year 1 builds the relationships and infrastructure for Year 2 and beyond.

The ZAO has a proven track record of following through: 100+ unbroken weeks of community governance, 7 virtual concerts, a complete run-of-show plan, an experienced production team, and an established partnership with Heart of Ellsworth. This is not a first-time producer — it is an established community deploying a new format in a new location.

### Background

The ZAO is a decentralized music artist collective with 188+ active members globally, founded in Ellsworth-area network connections and maintained by a Maine-based organizer (Zaal Panthaki, Ellsworth area). For three years, The ZAO has operated a daily newsletter (400+ editions), weekly community governance sessions (100+ consecutive weeks), and WaveWarZ — an online music battle platform that has processed $40,000+ in artist-to-audience trades with zero platform lock-in.

ZAOstock brings The ZAO's proven community organizing model to the physical world in Hancock County.

### Project Description

ZAOstock is a free, family-friendly independent music festival at Franklin Street Parklet, Ellsworth ME, on October 3, 2026 — during Art of Ellsworth weekend and Maine Craft Weekend. The event runs 12PM–6PM with no ticket cost. Performing artists include Maine-based independent musicians alongside artists from The ZAO's national WaveWarZ battle circuit.

ZAOstock will feature:
- Live performances by independent artists (Maine-based + national network)
- Live WaveWarZ music battle demonstrations on an open stage (community votes on who "wins" each round)
- Streaming via Restream to The ZAO's global audience
- Community co-working space (laptops + creators documenting the event)
- After-party at Black Moon Public House
