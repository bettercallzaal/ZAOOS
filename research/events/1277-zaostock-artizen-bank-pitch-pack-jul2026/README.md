Ellsworth — the local Main Street America partner that has received $391K in 
downtown revitalization grants. ZAOstock is Year 1 of what The ZAO intends to be 
an annual Maine festival.

WHY REGENERATIVE: ZAOstock channels economic activity back to independent artists 
via automatic onchain royalties (WaveWarZ: ~1.73% of every trade, instant SOL 
settlement). It builds local community in Ellsworth by bringing national Web3 music 
culture to a Maine Main Street. It is free, sustainable, and designed to repeat.
