## Section 1: Why Artist Reveals Matter More Than Ads

Artist reveals are the primary organic growth lever for music events. Each reveal:
- Gives the artist's fans a reason to share
- Creates a discrete, shareable piece of content
- Builds week-over-week momentum
- Generates newsletter content (each reveal = a newsletter section)
- Creates repeat social posts (announcement → recap → interview)

ZAOstock has no ad budget. Reveals are the engine.

---

## Section 2: Reveal Sequencing Strategy

### Principle: Lead with community, close with headliners

For ZAOstock, the hierarchy is:
1. **Week 1-2 (Now — Jul 31):** Announce "ZAOstock is coming Oct 3" + artist sign-up open/now closed
2. **Week 3-4 (Aug 1-14):** Reveal Artist #1 (Fellenz — ZAO community anchor, safe opener)
3. **Week 5-6 (Aug 15-28):** Reveal Artist #2 (DCoop/VEC — brings DC/ZAOville energy)
4. **Week 7-8 (Aug 29 - Sep 11):** Reveal remaining artists (1 per week, save biggest for last)
5. **Week 9 (Sep 12-18):** Full lineup drop — "Here's everyone playing ZAOstock Oct 3"
6. **Week 10-13 (Sep 19 - Oct 2):** Countdown, logistics, reminders

### Why save Fellenz for week 3-4 (not week 1):
Lead with "the event is happening" before the lineup. Audiences share events when they're excited about attending. Priming the event first, then revealing artists, builds curiosity.
