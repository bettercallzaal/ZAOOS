## Section 3: Artist Onboarding Checklist (Per Artist)

After agreement is signed, complete this checklist per artist:

**Booking phase:**
- [ ] Verbal agreement via DM/call
- [ ] Confirmation email sent (Section 1 template)
- [ ] Performance agreement signed (Section 2 template)
- [ ] 50% deposit sent
- [ ] Set time confirmed
- [ ] Soundcheck time confirmed

**Promo phase (due Sept 19 = 2 weeks before):**
- [ ] Artist bio received (for reveal post)
- [ ] Artist photo received (high-res, for reveal post)
- [ ] 2-3 songs confirmed for reveal playlist
- [ ] WaveWarZ song submitted (strongly encouraged — links to ZAO ecosystem)
- [ ] Artist social handles confirmed for tagging

**Show week:**
- [ ] Set list received (48 hrs before)
- [ ] Hotel/travel confirmed (if applicable)
- [ ] Wristbands allocated
- [ ] Soundcheck confirmed
- [ ] 50% balance prepared for day-of payment

**Post-show:**
- [ ] Balance paid
- [ ] Artist tagged in recap posts
- [ ] Performance archived (Arweave or similar)
- [ ] Artist invited to join WaveWarZ (if not already)
- [ ] Thank-you DM sent

## Section 6: Artist Fee Guidelines

[Zaal fills in actual numbers — these are guidelines based on ZAO's current economics]

**Budget framework:**
- Total artist fee budget: [Zaal to set — suggestion: $1,500-$3,000 for 8 acts = $187-375/act]
- Travel stipend for non-Maine artists: $100-200 each (for artists within driving distance)
- Hotel: 1-2 nights at [Stephen's hotel from doc 455, if available] for headliners

**Fee suggestions by role:**
- Headliner (Fellenz, DCoop): $300-500 each
- Mid-bill (WaveWarZ top artists): $150-250 each
- Opening act (emerging): $50-100 each
- Community acts (ZAO members / ZABAL winners): $0-50 or "exposure + recording"

**On-chain vs cash:**
- Offer SOL payment first — it creates a citable on-chain transaction ("all ZAOstock artists paid on-chain")
- If artist prefers fiat, that's fine — use Venmo or Zelle
- The on-chain narrative is more valuable for North Star than the mechanism
