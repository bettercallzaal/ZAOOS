# 1361 — ZAOstock Sponsor Outreach Guide (Jul 2026)

> **Budget context:** ZAOstock's budget (doc 1333) assumes $1,000-$2,000 in local sponsorship to offset costs. Ticket revenue (~$3,000 at 200 × $15) + Fisher grant ($2K-$3K target) + sponsorship ($1K-$2K) = $6,000-$8,000 total budget → covers the $5K-$7.5K projected cost. Without sponsorship, ZAO breaks even only if ticket sales hit targets. With sponsorship, there's margin to pay artists more and cover contingencies.
>
> **The pitch:** Sponsors get their name at a community music festival, on the livestream overlay, in the newsletter, and in the press release. For local businesses, this is extremely cheap community marketing compared to alternatives.

---

## Part 1: Sponsorship Package Options

### Three sponsor tiers

| Tier | Investment | What sponsor gets |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| **Gold Sponsor** | $500-$1,000 | Logo on all print materials + livestream overlay + newsletter mention + verbal shoutout from MC (Zaal) + banner at event |
| **Silver Sponsor** | $250-$500 | Logo on print materials + newsletter mention + banner at event |
| **Community Partner** | $100-$250 | Listed as community partner on all materials + newsletter mention |

**Target:** 1-2 Gold sponsors + 2-3 Silver sponsors + 2-3 Community Partners = $1,000-$2,500 total

### What sponsors actually get (specifics)

- **Logo on livestream overlay:** Thousands of virtual viewers see it for the entire 7-hour stream
- **Verbal MC mention:** Zaal thanks the sponsor on mic during the event (3-5 times throughout)
- **Newsletter mention:** ~500 subscribers see sponsor name in the ZAOstock preview issue
- **Press release mention:** "ZAOstock is made possible with support from [sponsor]" — potential Maine press pickup
- **Photo in post-event report:** Doc 1337 includes sponsor logos in recap

## Part 2: Who to Target

### Local Ellsworth / Hancock County businesses

The ideal sponsor is a local business that benefits from increased foot traffic and community goodwill.

| Business type | Why they sponsor | Pitch angle |
|-------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Local restaurants + bars | 200+ festival attendees = potential customers | "ZAOstock brings 200+ people to Ellsworth — many will be looking for food and drink. Your logo on the flyer they see." |
| Local brewery / beverage | Natural fit for outdoor music event | "Music festival + your beer/beverage = natural partnership. Beverages at the event?" |
| Local hotel / B&B | Out-of-town attendees need lodging | "We'll direct out-of-town attendees to your establishment for lodging." |
| Local music store | Direct alignment with music mission | "ZAOstock celebrates independent music. Would you like to be our instrument/gear sponsor?" |
| Local tech / startup | ZAO's tech angle is relatable | "ZAOstock is organized by a tech-forward community organization — aligns with your brand." |
| Regional healthcare (hospital, clinic) | Community health = community events | "Community wellness through arts and music — [hospital name] as community partner." |
| Maine credit union / bank | Community banking = community events | "ZAOstock builds Ellsworth community — [bank name] as a community partner?" |
| Cellphone / internet provider | Sponsoring connectivity for a livestream event | "WaveWarZ livestream needs reliable internet. Would [Spectrum/T-Mobile/etc.] want to be our connectivity sponsor?" |

## Part 3: Pitch Emails

### Local business pitch (general template)

**Subject:** ZAOstock (Oct 3, Ellsworth) — local sponsorship opportunity

> Hi [name],
>
> My name is Zaal Panthaki. I'm organizing ZAOstock, a one-day community music festival happening October 3 in Ellsworth. We expect 200-300 attendees plus a virtual livestream reaching hundreds more.
>
> I'm reaching out to a small group of local businesses about becoming a ZAOstock sponsor. Here's what that looks like:
>
> **Gold Sponsor ($500):** Your logo on the event banner, livestream overlay, festival program, and newsletter (500 subscribers). Verbal recognition from the MC throughout the day. Your support directly funds artist payments — every artist performs knowing they get paid night-of.
>
> ZAOstock is community-organized, non-corporate, and designed to put Ellsworth on the map for independent music. We're raising funds for [charity name] as part of the event.
>
> Would this be a fit for [business name]? Happy to meet for coffee or answer any questions.
>
> Best,
> Zaal Panthaki
> The ZAO / ZAOstock
> [email] | [phone]

## Part 5: Sponsor Package Delivery (What Sponsors Get, Confirmed)

Once a sponsor commits:
1. Send a confirmation email: "Welcome as a ZAOstock [tier] sponsor. Here's what to expect..."
2. Request their logo (PNG or SVG) for the overlay + print materials
3. Add their name to the Fisher grant application under "revenue sources"
4. Add their logo to the livestream overlay design (ATEM layer)
5. Note the sponsor in ZOE's day-of post templates (doc 1353)

## Part 6: North Star Impact

Sponsorship directly affects:
- ZAOstock budget health → artist payments more secure → IP catalog (artists more likely to return for 2027)
- Fisher grant: "sponsorship revenue = $X" in the budget section → stronger application
- Press release: "[Sponsor] supporting ZAOstock" = co-PR opportunity if sponsor has their own press contacts
