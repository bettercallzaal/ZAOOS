## Part 5: Artist Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to bring my own PA/speakers?**
> No. We have a front-of-house PA at the stage. You'll need your own interface or DI box. If you have specific monitor needs, let us know in advance.

**Q: Can I sell merch at ZAOstock?**
> Yes — set up a small table near your stage area. Keep it simple. We don't take a cut.

**Q: Can I bring my own guests for free?**
> Each artist gets 2 comp tickets. Let me know names in advance.

**Q: Is this a cash or crypto payment?**
> Your choice: cash, Venmo, USDC, or SOL. Same night. Tell us in advance which you prefer.

**Q: Will the performance be recorded/livestreamed?**
> Yes — ZAOstock will be livestreamed via Restream (YouTube + Twitch). Your performance will be part of the stream. The VOD will be archived permanently. By confirming your slot, you consent to this recording and archive. (Also covered in the performance agreement — doc 1321.)

**Q: What if I need to cancel?**
> Let me know as early as possible. We have a waitlist of artists who want a slot. No hard feelings — life happens.
