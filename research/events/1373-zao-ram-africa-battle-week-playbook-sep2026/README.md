# 1373 — ZAO × RAM Africa Battle Week Playbook (Sep 2026)

> **What it is:** A 5-day collaborative music battle series between The ZAO and RAM Africa, running September 22–26, 2026. WaveWarZ provides the platform; RAM Africa mobilizes African artists and audience; The ZAO provides governance framework and documentation. This is The ZAO's first international co-promotion event.
>
> **Why it matters:** Africa Battle Week is the North Star's geographic and cultural expansion move. It creates: (1) international press angles, (2) new artist pipeline for ZAOstock 2027, (3) WaveWarZ volume spike (projected +200-400 battles in one week), (4) ZAO's first non-Maine community partner.

## Week Structure (Mon Sep 22 – Fri Sep 26)

### Monday Sep 22 — Opening Day: "Africa Introduces Itself"
- **Format:** Open Quick Battles — any artist can join, Africa-focused promotion
- **Launch post (ZOE posts at 9am ET):** *"Africa Battle Week starts NOW. 5 days. One stage. @wavewarz x RAM Africa. If you've been sleeping on African talent in music battles — wake up. Submit a track and compete. Link in bio."*
- **RAM Africa posts:** coordinated launch announcement on their channels (Zaal sends them the caption)
- **Target:** 40-60 battles on Day 1

### Tuesday Sep 23 — "West Africa Day"
- **Format:** Quick Battles; ZOE tags regional spotlight (Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire)
- **Content angle:** Afrobeats vs. global producers — who wins on the algorithm?
- **ZOE post (midday):** "*West Africa is holding it down today on @wavewarz. Afrobeats on the leaderboard. Who's watching?*"

### Wednesday Sep 24 — "East + Southern Africa Day"
- **Format:** Quick Battles; spotlight East/Southern Africa (Kenya, Tanzania, South Africa, Zambia)
- **Content angle:** producers from East/Southern Africa on the same stage as global artists
- **ZOE post:** "*East Africa in the building. @wavewarz x RAM Africa — today we highlight Kenya, Tanzania, South Africa. Follow the leaderboard.*"

### Thursday Sep 25 — "Semifinal Stakes"
- **Format:** Highlight top battlers of the week; ZOE posts a "leaderboard at day 4" recap
- **ZOE leaderboard post (7pm ET):** Pull top 5 battlers from /api/public/stats; post with handles
- **RAM Africa post:** "One day left — who's going to take Africa Battle Week?"
- **MAIN event teaser:** Announce Sep 26 MAIN event with top participants

### Friday Sep 26 — MAIN Event: "Africa Battle Week Grand Final"
- **Format:** WaveWarZ MAIN event — top battlers from the week; new submissions welcome
- **Stakes:** Africa Battle Week Champion title; featured in ZAO newsletter + ZAOOS doc
- **ZOE pre-event post (9am ET):** "*Africa Battle Week Grand Final. Tonight. @wavewarz MAIN event. The best of the week, on one stage. Who's your pick?*"
- **ZOE result post (after event):** Pull winner from API; post results with full stat block
- **Post-event:** winner gets "Africa Battle Week Champion" recognition in doc 1339 stats + ZAO newsletter
