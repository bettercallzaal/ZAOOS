## Virtual Stream Logistics

**Platforms:** YouTube Live + Twitch (simultaneous via Restream — from doc 1346)

**Start time:** 9:00 AM ET (stream goes live ~15 min before first set)

**Duration:** ~9 hours (9am to ~6pm, with breaks between sets)

**Chat:** YouTube Live Chat + Twitch Chat enabled. ZOE monitors both — flags questions or battle predictions for Zaal to see.

**Stream quality target:** 1080p, 30fps. Restream auto-handles platform encoding (from doc 1346 tech setup).

**Intermission WaveWarZ battle:** During the 15-20 min break between sets (mid-day), ZOE launches a WaveWarZ community battle open to all viewers — in-person and virtual. Virtual attendees vote via wavewarz.info.

**VOD:** Full stream auto-saved to YouTube. Clips and highlights available on @wavewarz YouTube channel within 48hrs.
