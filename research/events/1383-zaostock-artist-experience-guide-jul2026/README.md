## Part 6 — WaveWarZ Loser-Earns Economics (For Artists New to WaveWarZ)

> *This is the thing that makes ZAOstock different from every other music festival.*

**The model:** In a WaveWarZ battle, both artists earn — not just the winner. The community votes, and the "loser-earns" mechanism pays both sides.

**Why it matters for ZAOstock:**
- The mid-show WaveWarZ Live Battle isn't a competition where one artist loses — it's a community moment where everyone wins
- Both battling artists earn SOL from the battle regardless of who the community votes for
- The crowd is the judge; the community is the economic mechanism

**How to run a WaveWarZ battle (if you haven't yet):**
1. Go to wavewarz.info
2. Submit a track to an open battle
3. The community votes — you earn either way
4. Your battle stats (wins, battles run, SOL earned) become your profile as a ZAO artist

**Artist payout note:** Current WaveWarZ stats: 9.0988 SOL paid directly to artists across all battles. Every ZAOstock performer is already part of this track record just by participating.
