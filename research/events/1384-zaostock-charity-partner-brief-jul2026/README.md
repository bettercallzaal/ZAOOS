# 1384 — ZAOstock Charity Partner Brief (Jul 2026)

> **Decision needed by Jul 25:** Choose ZAOstock's charity partner. This doc gives Zaal everything needed to make the pick in under 30 minutes: criteria, 5 candidates, outreach script, announcement copy, and on-stage ask protocol.
>
> **Why it matters:** The charity component (1) gives press a human angle beyond "music DAO", (2) unlocks Fisher grant language ("community benefit — % of proceeds to local charity"), (3) gives on-stage energy for a fundraising moment, and (4) builds Maine goodwill for ZAOstock 2027 permit renewal.

---

## Decision Framework

**Ideal charity partner:**
1. **Maine-based** — supports permit goodwill + local press angle
2. **Music, arts, or youth focus** — aligns with ZAO's artist-first mission
3. **Small organization** — $500-$1,000 donation is meaningful to them; larger orgs won't care
4. **Online donation capability** — ZOE can link directly; virtual attendees can give remotely
5. **Responds to DMs/email quickly** — need confirmed by Aug 1 to include in marketing

---

## The 5 Candidates

### Option 1 — Ellsworth Arts (Local, Highest Fit)
*If an arts organization exists in Ellsworth, ME — the ZAOstock venue city*

- **Why:** Venue-city alignment is a permit + community goodwill win; press writes "local festival gives back to local arts"
- **Action:** Search "Ellsworth ME arts organization" or "Hancock County arts nonprofit" — if found, DM or email director
- **Grant language:** "ZAOstock donates [%] of ticket proceeds to Ellsworth arts/community [org name]"
- **Estimated donation:** $200–$500 (5-10% of ticket revenue target $4K-$5K)

### Option 2 — Maine Arts Commission Artist Grants
*Maine government arts agency — statewide legitimacy*

- **Website:** mainearts.maine.gov
- **Why:** MAC funds Maine artists; ZAO directs ZAOstock proceeds to fund their artist grant pool = perfect symmetry
- **Why not:** Government agency, not a charity in the traditional sense; harder to get a named partnership
- **Action:** Check if MAC accepts event donation partnerships; email info@mainearts.maine.gov

### Option 3 — Music for Life Alliance (National, Music Mission)
*A smaller national org supporting music education/therapy*

- **Why:** Music mission aligns with WaveWarZ/COC artist-first story; national press understands it
- **Action:** Find equivalent org via "music education nonprofit" search
- **Grant language:** "ZAOstock directs 5% of proceeds to music education charity [name]"

### Option 4 — A Specific Maine Artist in Need
*Not a charity — a direct artist support moment*

- **Why:** Most authentic to ZAO's mission; ZAO literally funds an independent artist
- **Format:** On-stage "we're giving $[X] directly to [Maine artist]" — community votes who receives it
- **Risk:** Complicates grant language (not a 501c3 donation); harder to get press coverage
- **How:** Survey ZAO community (Fractal session or Telegram vote) before ZAOstock

### Option 5 — Local Ellsworth Food Bank / Community Need
*Broad community benefit, Maine city goodwill*

- **Why:** Maximizes permit goodwill + local press angle; no alignment risk
- **Why not:** Least aligned with music/arts mission; weaker for OP RF and arts grants
- **Example:** Ellsworth Food Pantry, Hancock County Community Foundation grantees
- **Grant language:** "ZAOstock gives back to Ellsworth community — [%] to local nonprofit"
