## Part 1: Volunteer Roster and Roles

Minimum crew needed: 6 people (including Zaal and Iman). Ideal: 8-10.

### Role R01 — Event Director (Iman)

**Reports to:** Nobody. Escalate only on venue/safety issues.  
**Scope:** All logistics, volunteers, timeline, vendor coordination, crisis decisions  
**Zone:** Full venue  
**On-day comms:** Primary contact for all volunteers. Holds master timeline. Makes go/no-go calls (weather, late artists, tech failures).  
**Pre-event owner:** Venue setup, volunteer briefing, checklist sign-off

### Role R02 — Stage Host / MC (Zaal)

**Reports to:** Iman for timing; nobody else  
**Scope:** Stage, microphone, artist introductions, WaveWarZ live battles, ZAO governance announcements  
**Zone:** Stage only (don't pull Zaal off stage)  
**Key moments:**
- Opening announcement (ZABAL S2, "Season 3 starts January")
- Artist introductions
- Live WaveWarZ battle during [TBD slot from doc 1336]
- Charity donation reveal
- Closing + "see you at ZAOstock 2027"

### Role R03 — Door + Ticketing Volunteer

**Reports to:** Iman  
**Scope:** Check Eventbrite QR codes, collect walk-up cash sales, hand out wristbands, maintain headcount  
**Zone:** Venue entrance  
**Tools needed:** Phone with Eventbrite Organizer app, cash box, wristbands, clicker counter  
**Briefing:** Arrive 7:45 AM. Doors open [venue standard — confirm with Suzanne McLean]. Wristband colors: [in-person = X, VIP = Y — confirm before Sep 15].  
**Edge case:** Walk-up buyers without Eventbrite ticket → take cash, record name + phone number on paper sheet, report count to Iman by noon.

### Role R04 — Artist Liaison

**Reports to:** Iman  
**Scope:** Meet artists at venue entrance, escort to green room, ensure soundcheck slots run on time, handle artist requests (water, time check, "when am I on?")  
**Zone:** Green room + stage backstage  
**Pre-event:** Confirm all 8 artists' arrival time by Sep 26 (update schedule in doc 1336). Share this guide with each artist — they already have doc 1383.  
**Tools needed:** Printed artist schedule (from doc 1336), green room location map  
**Key rule:** Don't let artists wander into the house until their soundcheck slot. Audience first experience of the lineup should be the stage.

### Role R05 — Stream + Tech Volunteer

**Reports to:** Iman (tech issues), Zaal (stage audio cues)  
**Scope:** YouTube/Twitch stream (via Restream, doc 1346), venue PA sound, stage monitor levels, microphone check, laptop/slide deck if any  
**Zone:** Tech table (house left or back of house — confirm with venue)  
**Pre-event briefing:** Run full test stream from the venue on Sep [date before Oct 3 — confirm with Iman]. Know the reboot procedure for OBS. Know the Restream stream key location.  
**ZOE coordination:** ZOE auto-posts "We're live now → [stream link]" at 10:05 AM (per doc 1375). R05 confirms stream is actually live by 10:00 AM, then gives Iman thumbs up.  
**Contingency:** If stream dies, ZOE posts "Technical pause — back in [10] minutes." R05 troubleshoots. If unresolvable in 15 min: cut stream, continue IRL only.

### Role R06 — Social + Content Volunteer (ZOE Handler)

**Reports to:** Iman  
**Scope:** Photos, short video clips for TikTok/Instagram Stories, feed ZOE the manual inputs ZOE can't auto-pull (crowd count, energy check, charity total at end), one-tap approve ZOE-drafted posts  
**Zone:** Full venue (mobile)  
**Tools needed:** Phone charged to 100%, ZOE Telegram access, backup power bank  
**ZOE coordination:** ZOE's day-of automation is in doc 1375. R06 only needs to handle 3 manual inputs: (1) crowd count at 11:30 AM, (2) stage energy descriptor at set break, (3) charity total at 5:00 PM for the reveal post.  
**Priority:** Capture the live WaveWarZ battle result for TikTok within 5 min of result.

### Role R07 — Floor + Hospitality Volunteer(s)

**Reports to:** Iman  
**Scope:** Water station, merch table (if any), general attendee questions ("where's the bathroom?", "when is [artist] on?"), venue tidiness  
**Zone:** House floor  
**Notes:** This role can be filled by 1-2 ZABAL S2 participants (their Week 8 contribution milestone). Coordinate with Iman.

## Part 5: Contingency Protocols

### C01 — Artist No-Show

**Trigger:** Artist does not arrive within 30 min of scheduled soundcheck  
**Who handles:** R04 + Iman  
**Action:** (1) R04 calls/texts artist. (2) If no contact in 15 min, Iman declares them MIA. (3) Shift remaining artists earlier by 15-30 min. (4) If gap is >45 min, Zaal fills with WaveWarZ battle or impromptu Q&A from stage. (5) ZOE posts nothing about the no-show — Zaal handles from stage only if asked.

### C02 — Stream Failure

**Trigger:** YouTube/Twitch stream drops for >5 min  
**Who handles:** R05  
**Action:** (1) ZOE posts "Technical pause — back in 10 min" (ZOE template, R06 approves in one tap). (2) R05 troubleshoots OBS + Restream. (3) If unresolvable in 15 min: stream ends, event continues IRL only. (4) Post-event: upload full VOD as YouTube recording within 48h. Virtual ticket holders get refund offer (doc 1379 fallback).

### C03 — Sound System Failure

**Trigger:** PA drops or feedback loop, not self-correcting in 3 min  
**Who handles:** R05 + Iman (call venue contact)  
**Action:** (1) Iman calls venue A/V contact immediately. (2) If failure is <15 min, delay current set. (3) If failure is >30 min, discuss with Zaal: acoustic sets (voices only) vs. reschedule tail of show. (4) Never run with bad sound — acoustic pivot is better than distorted PA.

### C04 — Weather (Outdoor Component)

**Applies if:** Venue has outdoor stage or component.  
**Trigger:** Rain forecast >60% for event hours.  
**Who decides:** Iman, by 7:00 AM Oct 3.  
**Options:** (1) Full indoor pivot (confirm indoor capacity vs. expected attendance). (2) Delay outdoor segments by 1h and reassess. (3) Full cancellation (last resort — virtual-only fallback per doc 1379).

### C05 — Permit or Venue Issue

**Trigger:** Venue requires something unexpected on event day (e.g., noise ordinance, fire marshal capacity check)  
**Who handles:** Iman  
**Contact:** Suzanne McLean 207-667-2563 (permit contact from docs 1285+1333)  
**Action:** Follow whatever Suzanne says. Don't argue with the city on event day. Zaal says nothing from stage until resolved.

### C06 — Medical Emergency

**Priority:** All other protocols suspended.  
**Action:** Call 911 immediately. Iman coordinates with emergency services. Zaal pauses event from stage ("we're taking a brief pause"). Resume only after all-clear from Iman.
