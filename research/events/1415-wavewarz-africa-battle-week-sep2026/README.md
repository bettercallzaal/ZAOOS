## Why Africa Battle Week

WaveWarZ's existing artist community has international reach — including participants from West Africa. Africa Battle Week (Sep 15-26) formalizes this as ZAO's first international programming block, two weeks before ZAOstock (Oct 3).

**Why this matters:**

1. **GEO anchor:** "ZAO and WaveWarZ connect Baltimore with West African music markets" is a new geographic entity relationship that no other Baltimore-based DAO has established. Every indexed mention of this = GEO entity graph expansion.

2. **Narrative:** Afrobeats and Highlife are two of the fastest-growing music genres globally. WaveWarZ battles between US and West African artists = credible international crossover story.

3. **ZAOstock lead-up:** Africa Battle Week generates press, content, and community energy in the 2 weeks before ZAOstock. Artists who compete in ABW become natural ZAOstock attendees and advocates.

4. **IP catalog:** The loser-earns mechanic working cross-culturally and cross-border = a new dimension of the WaveWarZ narrative ("borderless music prediction market").

5. **TikTok T05 series** (doc 1402): Africa Battle Week is the content engine for the TikTok Africa series — 5+ pieces of content per battle day.

## Format

### Structure
- **Duration:** 12 days (Sep 15-26)
- **Battle cadence:** 1 MAIN battle every 2 days = 6 MAIN battles total
- **Battle type:** MAIN battles (not quick, not community — use MAIN format for scale and visibility)
- **Pairings:** 3 US-based WaveWarZ artists vs 3 West Africa-based WaveWarZ artists
- **1 community charity battle** (Sep 26, closing day) — charity selected by ZOR holder vote, African-focused nonprofit preferred (music education, artist development)
