## Why Jul 21 Ticket Launch Is Early (and That's Fine)

ZAOstock is Oct 3 — 74 days away when tickets launch Jul 21. That's an unusually long lead time for a 200-person event. The rationale:

1. **Early buyers = core community.** The first 50 tickets should be ZAO community (Telegram, Farcaster, governance participants). They're the ones who'll bring guests.

2. **Lineup drops Sep 1** — that's when external ticket sales will spike. But we need existing ticket sales to prove demand before sending the national press release.

3. **Fundraising signal.** Grant applications (Fisher, OP RF) are stronger if they can say "ZAOstock has [X] tickets sold" at the time of submission.

4. **ZABAL S2 kicks off Sep 1.** 20 ZABAL S2 participants + their guests = potential 40+ attendees who need tickets.
