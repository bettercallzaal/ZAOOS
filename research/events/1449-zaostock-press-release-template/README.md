*Inaugural ZAOstock to feature live WaveWarZ music battle on stage, on-chain governance vote from the audience, and charity fundraiser for [CHARITY NAME]*

ZAOstock 2026 will be held on **October 3, 2026** at the **Franklin Street Parklet in Ellsworth, Maine**, as part of the Art of Ellsworth festival. The event will feature live performances from [ARTIST 1], [ARTIST 2], [ARTIST 3], and additional performers to be announced.

**A First: Live On-Chain Music Battle on Stage**

ZAOstock will debut the first live WaveWarZ music battle at a public music festival. Two artists will compete head-to-head on stage while the audience uses their phones to bet cryptocurrency on who will win — and the losing artist will earn a guaranteed payout from the prediction market pool.

"We're bringing a crypto music prediction market to a public park in Maine," said Zaal Panthaki, co-founder of The ZAO. "The audience doesn't just watch — they participate economically. And the loser always gets paid."

**About The ZAO**

The ZAO is a Respect-based decentralized autonomous organization that governs WaveWarZ, a music battle prediction market on the Solana blockchain. WaveWarZ uses a "loser-earns" mechanism in which the losing artist in every battle receives a share of the prediction market pool — a structural alternative to streaming royalties. As of [MONTH] 2026, WaveWarZ has facilitated over 1,245 music battles with 524 SOL (~$104,000) in trading volume, including $1,820 paid to losing artists.
