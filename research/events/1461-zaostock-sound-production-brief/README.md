## Venue Power Assessment

**Key question from permit call (doc 1439):** What is the Parklet's available power? (20A, 30A, 50A outdoor outlet?)

**Power draw estimate for ZAOstock:**
- PA system (mains + subs): ~1,200-2,000W (~10-17A at 120V)
- Monitors (4 wedges): ~600W (~5A)
- Mixer + outboard: ~200W (~2A)
- Lighting (if any): ~500W (~4A)
- **Total: ~2,500-3,200W (~21-27A)**

**Implication:** 30A outdoor circuit is the minimum; 50A preferred. If Parklet only has 20A, need a generator (~$150/day rental). Confirm on permit call.
