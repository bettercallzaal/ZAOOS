## Ticket Tier Structure

ZAOstock uses a **free + donation tier** model (NOT standard paid ticketing):

| Tier | Price | What It Is | Eventbrite Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| General Admission | **FREE** | Standard entry; outdoor festival, first come first served | Free ticket (requires RSVP) |
| ZOR Holder | **FREE** | Early entry 30 min before doors, stage-front section | Free ticket; ZOR verification via wallet check-in on site |
| Supporter | **$20 donation** | Same access as GA + name in credits + ZAO community recognition | Paid ticket; donation goes to ZAO/artist fund |
| VIP (limited) | **$50 donation** | Early entry + backstage area access + meet artists + name in credits | Paid ticket; very limited (10-20 spots) |

**Why this structure:**
- Free GA keeps the "free community festival" grant narrative intact (required for Fisher, MAC)
- ZOR holder tier reinforces governance utility (ZOR = early entry)
- Donation tiers are optional — ZAO never requires payment to attend
- $20/$50 donation tiers convert engaged fans without creating a paywall

**Expected ticket distribution:** 80% free GA, 10% free ZOR, 8% $20 Supporter, 2% $50 VIP
