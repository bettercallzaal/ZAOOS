## Live WaveWarZ BattleVote: How It Worked

**The COC #7 BattleVote was the first time ZAO ran a live WaveWarZ vote during a show:**

1. **Pre-show:** A WaveWarZ MAIN battle was created between the two featured artists
2. **During the show:** Zaal announced the battle and directed the audience to wavewarz.info to vote
3. **Audience participation:** Viewers voted in real time via the WaveWarZ platform (web browser)
4. **Result announced on-stream:** Zaal read the live vote count mid-show and again at close
5. **Payouts:** Both artists received their WaveWarZ payouts on-chain (winner ~80%, loser ~10%)

**Why this matters:**
- First documented WaveWarZ battle with a live IRL/streaming audience driving votes
- Proof of concept for ZAOstock Oct 3 (where the battle happens from the main stage, in person)
- Citable precedent: "ZAO ran its first live audience-driven WaveWarZ battle at COC #7 on Jul 18, 2026"
