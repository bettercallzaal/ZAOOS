## Artist Selection Criteria

ZAOstock is not a fame-based lineup. It's a community-first, economics-demonstrating lineup.

**Criteria (in priority order):**

1. **Has WaveWarZ battles on record** — the festival is built around WaveWarZ; performing artists should have used the platform. Prioritize artists who have battled and lost (loser-earns story is strongest with real data).

2. **Active in ZAO ecosystem** — COC Concertz performers, ZABAL S1 graduates, ZOR holders.

3. **Diverse genres** — at least 4 distinct genres represented (hip hop, R&B, electronic, singer-songwriter, indie, etc.)

4. **At least 1 Maine-based artist** — makes the "community festival" narrative true locally. Required for Maine Arts Commission grant track (doc 1471).

5. **Available Oct 3 in Ellsworth, Maine** — obvious but must be confirmed upfront.

6. **Willing to do ZAOstock's format** — artists must agree that a live WaveWarZ battle may happen between two of them during the show (see charity battle protocol in doc 1476).

## What Each Confirmed Artist Must Know + Agree To

Before confirming a booking:

1. **Show date and logistics:** October 3, 2026, Ellsworth, Maine. Doors ~12PM, show ~1PM-8PM. Artists should arrive by 10AM for soundcheck.

2. **Pay:** ZAOstock honors artist guarantee. Minimum: $100 honorarium per artist (from ZAO/grant budget). WaveWarZ battle participants also earn from the battle pool (separate from honorarium).

3. **Technical rider:** 2-4 songs for festival slot (20-30 min). Provide input list (stems or live band) by Sep 15. (ZOE sends input list request — doc 1461)

4. **WaveWarZ battle:** 2 of the artists will face off in a live WaveWarZ battle from the stage (audience votes). Artists for the battle are selected by community vote (doc 1476). Being confirmed for ZAOstock does NOT automatically mean you're in the battle. Artists who are in the battle must consent explicitly.

5. **Charity contribution (battle only):** The battle payout goes to the ZAOstock charity partner (doc 1446). Artists in the battle donate their payout.

6. **Photography/video:** ZAOstock will photograph and video the show. Footage may be used for ZAO marketing.
