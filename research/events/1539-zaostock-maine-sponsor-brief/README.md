## Why Local Sponsors Matter

1. **Fisher grant**: "community partnerships" is a scoring criterion (doc 1436) — local business support = evidence of community benefit
2. **MAC grant**: Maine Arts Commission requires local community connection — Monteux School (doc 1503) + local sponsors = dual evidence
3. **Event credibility**: local sponsor names on the Eventbrite and printed materials signal legitimacy to Maine attendees
4. **Potential in-kind support**: audio equipment, catering, printing — reduces cash outlay ahead of Fisher grant receipt

---

## Sponsor Tier Structure

### Tier 1: Presenting Sponsors ($500-1,000 cash or in-kind)
- Logo on all materials (digital + printed)
- Named in event announcements and ZOE posts
- One free ZAOstock ticket per rep
- Post-event coverage in ZAO Brief newsletter

### Tier 2: Supporting Sponsors ($100-500 cash or in-kind)
- Named on Eventbrite and event signage
- Social media mention (ZOE post from @wavewarz X)
- Comp tickets to the event

### Tier 3: Community Partners (in-kind only — food, print, equipment)
- Named at the event as a "ZAOstock Community Partner"
- Mentioned in post-show Telegram/Farcaster recap
