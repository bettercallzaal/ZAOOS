# 1550 — ZAOstock 2026 Volunteer Coordinator Pack (Aug–Oct 3)

**Type:** OPERATIONS-PACK  
**Topic:** Events  
**Status:** DRAFT — recruit 4-6 volunteers by Aug 22 (ZABAL S2 cohort kickoff = natural recruitment moment). Lock all assignments by Sep 15. ZOE manages reminders from Aug 22 onward.

---

## Why Volunteers Matter

ZAOstock is a 200-attendee event with a run-of-show across 6+ hours (doc 1524). The day-of team is: Zaal (event director + on-stage MC), Hurricane (tech + streaming), ZOE (social media). Everything else — door check, audio assist, artist liaison, videography — needs 4-6 human volunteers with clearly assigned roles.

**Without volunteers:** Zaal is pulled off-stage to solve logistics. The live governance vote (3PM, the most important moment) gets disrupted.

**With volunteers:** Zaal stays on stage. ZOE gets content. Artists get a professional experience.

---

## Volunteer Roles

### Role 1 — Door + RSVP Check-In (2 people)
**Time:** 11:30AM–1:30PM setup, 12PM–3PM door hours  
**Tasks:**
- Print Eventbrite RSVP list morning of (ZOE sends link the night before)
- Check in attendees at door (name + "Supporter" badge if applicable)
- Count headcount (report to Zaal every 30 min during door hours)
- Direct "Free RSVP" vs. "Supporter $20" ticket holders to separate lines if needed
- Carry: laptop or phone with Eventbrite check-in app, badge labels, sharpie

**Skill needed:** Organized, friendly, comfortable with light tech (Eventbrite app)

### Role 2 — Artist Liaison (1 person)
**Time:** 11AM–5PM  
**Tasks:**
- Meet artists at venue arrival (text each artist their arrival slot)
- Walk artists to green room / backstage
- Ensure artist has: water, phone charger, local contact for Hurricane re: audio check
- Communicate sound check times from Hurricane
- Collect: each artist track submission for community + MAIN battle (coordinate with Hurricane by 1:30PM)
- After performance: thank artist, photo opp with Zaal, collect consent for social post

**Skill needed:** Calm under pressure, confident with artists, has a phone to text

### Role 3 — Videographer / Photographer (1 person)
**Time:** 12PM–4:30PM  
**Tasks:**
- Document crowd arrival (12PM–1PM)
- Film each musical performance (full length recommended; minimum 30-sec clip per artist)
- Film the WaveWarZ battle moment: (a) Zaal opening battle, (b) live vote count on screen, (c) result announcement, (d) charity payout announcement
- Photograph: crowd + stage + artist close-ups + ZOR holder voting (hands/phones)
- Deliver: Google Drive folder link to Zaal by Oct 4 noon
- Optional: live Instagram stories from @wavewarz (if they have access — ZOE grants access)

**Skill needed:** Can operate a smartphone camera; artistic eye a plus but not required

### Role 4 — Social Media / ZOE Assist (1 person)
**Time:** 12PM–4PM  
**Tasks:**
- Post to @wavewarz Instagram Stories live during event (ZOE gives access)
- Reply to comments on @wavewarz X posts during event (ZOE manages, assist reviews)
- Capture crowd quotes: "what do you think of WaveWarZ?" — text to ZOE's Telegram
- Post ZAOstock hashtag content to personal account (optional, encouraged)
- Coordinate with videographer for Story clips within 10 min of capture

**Skill needed:** Social media native; knows Instagram, X; can compose on-brand posts

### Role 5 — Logistics Float (1 person, optional but recommended)
**Time:** 11AM–5PM  
**Tasks:**
- Handle supply runs (ice, water, paper, anything that runs out)
- Backup for any of the above roles if someone is pulled away
- Coordinate vendor/catering if applicable (Jordan's Restaurant liaison)
- Manage tip jar / merch table if ZAO sells any merch
- Troubleshoot anything not listed above

**Skill needed:** Resourceful, has a car

1. **Collapse roles:** Role 1 + Role 5 can share (check-in station + supply runs = same person)
