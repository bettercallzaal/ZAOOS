## Section 1: What Artists Need to Know

### What is WaveWarZ?

WaveWarZ is an on-chain music battle platform. Two artists compete, an audience votes, and the loser earns a share of the wager pool.

**The loser earns.** This is the core mechanic:
- A battle has a SOL wager pool (set by the operator — for Africa Battle Week, Hurricane sets a standard amount)
- ZOR holders vote during the battle window
- Winner takes the majority of the pool, loser takes a smaller guaranteed share
- Payouts fire automatically via Solana smart contract — no one needs to manually release funds

This is not a traditional music battle where losing means nothing. In WaveWarZ, **losing is a paid engagement.** The Africa Battle Week charity battle on Sep 26 routes 100% of the pool to the community-voted charity.

### Time zones: West Africa (WAT) vs Eastern Time (ET)

West Africa Time = UTC+1. Eastern Time (ET) = UTC-4 in Sep 2026 (daylight saving time).

| Event | ET | WAT |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Battle launch | 9:00 AM ET | 2:00 PM WAT |
| Battle vote window closes | ~11:00 AM ET | ~4:00 PM WAT |
| Payout settles | ~12:00 PM ET | ~5:00 PM WAT |
| ZOE result post | ~12:30 PM ET | ~5:30 PM WAT |

Artists can follow their battle from anywhere. WaveWarZ is fully online. No physical presence required.

### How a WaveWarZ Quick Battle works

**Before the battle:**
- Hurricane (WaveWarZ operator) creates the battle in the WaveWarZ system
- Both artists confirm their track selections
- Battle is scheduled for the day/time (9 AM ET launch)

**During the battle:**
- Battle page goes live at wavewarz.info — public can see both artists and tracks
- ZOR holders vote using their ZOR token weight
- The battle runs a fixed window (typically 30-60 minutes for Quick Battles)
- Live vote counts visible to audience

**After the battle:**
- Smart contract closes the vote and calculates payouts
- Winner and loser payouts sent automatically to their Solana wallets
- Zaal (or ZOE) announces the result on Farcaster + Telegram
- Sep 26 charity battle: entire pool routes to the charity wallet, not to the artists

### How to set up a Phantom wallet (for artists who don't have one)

1. Go to phantom.app — download browser extension or mobile app
2. Create a new wallet — save your seed phrase offline (never share it)
3. Note your public wallet address (starts with a random string, e.g. `7qK...`)
4. Send Zaal or Hurricane your public wallet address — this is where payout goes

Artists do NOT need SOL in their wallet before the battle. Payouts come TO the wallet. They only need SOL if they want to wager on other battles.

### How to set up a WaveWarZ account

1. Go to wavewarz.info
2. Connect your Phantom (or Backpack) wallet via the "Connect" button
3. Your wallet address becomes your WaveWarZ identity
4. Upload 1-2 battle tracks (MP3, standard quality, ≤10 minutes each)
5. Complete your artist profile: name, genre, location (city/country)
6. Share your WaveWarZ profile URL with Hurricane so he can link you to the battle

If you run into issues: DM @bettercallzaal on Farcaster or Telegram.

## Section 4: Day-Of Artist Reference (Sep 22-26)

Artists can share this with their communities.

### What to tell your audience

- Battle is live at **wavewarz.info** at **2:00 PM West Africa Time**
- Your fans can't vote directly — only ZOR token holders vote
- Your fans CAN watch, share the battle link, and follow /wavewarz on Farcaster
- Payout is automatic — you don't need to "claim" your earnings

### What the loser earns

The exact SOL amount depends on the wager pool for each battle. Hurricane sets the per-battle wager amount based on available liquidity. Africa Battle Week target (from doc 1415): each artist earns a meaningful amount whether they win or lose.

The phrase for artists to use with their community: "The loser still gets paid. That's the point."

### What happens on Day 5 (charity battle)

Day 5 artists: you're fighting for the charity, not personal payout. The community voted for the charity Jul 24-25 (via ZOR Snapshot vote). Your SOL goes to the charity wallet automatically.

This is a public moment — ZOE posts the charity tx hash live from the settlement. Your name is permanently associated with the charity battle in the on-chain record.
