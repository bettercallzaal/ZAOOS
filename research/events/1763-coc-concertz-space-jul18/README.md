# COC Concertz Space - Zaal "Day in the Life" Power Hour

**Date:** 2026-07-18  
**Duration:** ~85 minutes  
**Platform:** X Space (public broadcast)  
**Host:** Rev / Miss Evie  
**Featured Guest:** Zaal (ZAO founder)  
**Format:** Interview + musical showcase + community Q&A

## Goal

Showcase a day in the life of Zaal across ZAO ecosystem projects (Wave Wars, ZABAL Games, Sparks), discuss consistency as a core value in web3 building, and celebrate the COC Concertz community and its cross-cultural appeal.

## Summary

Zaal opened the space with a song loop (BetterCallZaal theme) and talked through 2026 progress: joining COC in 2023 during music community research, collaborating with Django (7-year relationship), and scaling from early experiments (Zao Palooza 2024, Zao Chella 2024) into Wave Wars as a bootstrapped, multisided music marketplace.

Key narrative threads:

**Consistency & Philosophy:** Success = progressive realization of worthy ideals (Earl Lightingale quote). Emphasis on staying consistent through market cycles, staying in the building when hype fades, earning trust through durable work not hype.

**Wave Wars Status:** 520 SOL traded, 700+ paid out to artists, 1224 battles on-chain, growing across geographies (Africa, India, planned Latin America + Russia), all bootstrapped with no external investors. Zaal spoke on gym bangers and newer Hurricane tracks that inspired him.

**Sparks (New Creator Coin Launcher):** Zaal introduced a new product idea for customizable creator coins on Clanker V5, with recursive smart-contract splits so each new token auto-adds treasury wallets to fee recipients. Allows full creator customization (splits, governance, ZAO stakes, utility). Launching demos + tests starting with an upcoming Clanker space (Thursday 10am EST).

**Candy's Role:** Extensive tribute to Candy Toybox as the linchpin who believed in Zaal's vision early, took on design/video/operations work, and freed Zaal to focus on systems/networking. She runs the Wave Wars operations and manages the team with Zaal.

**Zao Fund & Artizen:** Push for community members to apply to Zao Fund via Artizen (grants/support for music/media in The ZAO ecosystem). Next payout cycle ~6 months away, so building foundation now.

**Open Source & GitHub Commits:** Zaal expressed belief that GitHub commits will become a powerful signal of intent and direction in the digital age, especially for open-source infrastructure.

**DAO Lessons:** Acknowledged that DAOs in the 2021 hype cycle failed because they chased trends without permanent brand identity. Emphasized durable, authentic branding over hype-cycle products.

**Wave Wars as Crowdfunding:** Floated idea of using Wave Wars battles themselves (benefit battles) to crowdfund travel to DevCon India and other events, turning the platform into a fundraising mechanism while entertaining the community.

## Decisions & Announcements

1. **Clanker Thursday 10am EST:** Zaal hosting a space on Clanker V5 launch focusing on "Clanker Empire Builder" feature set and how to leverage it for token launches.

2. **Sparks Launch Plan:** Rolling out creator coin configurator with smart defaults, word-of-mouth kickoff = "back the album," targeting music creators first.

6. **Zao Fund Push:** Encouraging all community members with media/music involvement to apply via Artizen for grants. Building foundation for next payout cycle (6mo out).

## Key Moments & Quotes

**"Success is the progressive realization of a worthy ideal."** - Earl Lightingale (1900s), cited by Zaal as core philosophy. Understanding goals + daily progress toward them = success.

**"The one thing I always say about community: if I put 10% of my energy in and get 100x return, it's a good community. If I'm putting in a lot and getting nothing back, that's not healthy."** - Zaal on community health as a return multiplier.

**On consistency:** "The growth of the on-chain music space while the space got smaller are the individuals that kept in and stayed consistent... Consistency is everything."

**On Candy:** "She is without a doubt the only reason everything is possible today... The first individual who actually believed in me enough to say, hey, what we're doing is cool right now, but what we can do with your vision is better."

**On Wave Wars as a platform:** "The platform itself is a crowdfunding mechanism... We can use Wave Wars to crowdfund travel to DevCon, to events, using the platform he's built itself. How beautiful of a story would that be?"

**On DAOs:** "Following trends that are short term and putting permanent brand identities towards things like that can be very negative for your reputation in the space."

**On Wave Wars' organic growth:** "When you're doing it the right way and you're not scamming people or bodying everyone, just doing it organically, it's a lot harder to reach those big numbers. But we've all been consistent."

**Rev on Zaal's impact:** "You came into our community years ago and hit the ground running and just took us with you... You stood on business from day one."
