## Summary

Arun demoed DreamStarter's production-ready workflows, all currently running on Artisan Fund's community platform. The conversation covered four live automation features (video clipper, auto reel, video content card, lead discovery CRM), team access architecture, and strategic alignment on India-focused Web3 community building ahead of Edge City (Oct 11-31, India) and DevCon (Nov, India).

Key insight: DreamStarter's value proposition has sharpened dramatically since June call (doc 805) - from early-stage testing to live, automated posting daily on multiple social channels. Arun's focus remains on people/organizations who can't code or don't have time to, while Zaal's angle is Claude-native API/MCP integration for agentic posting workflows.

## Decisions

1. **VIP Email Setup:** Arun will create a `zaal@dreams` vanity email (or `zaal@dreamstarter.io`) for Zaal's team to use as a shared login, avoiding the need for a collaborator feature (which doesn't exist yet). Team members use forwarding to their own email addresses. Zaal can give the password to his intern.

2. **DreamStarter Access Granted:** Zaal + intern will get free/subsidized access to DreamStarter workflows via the VIP email once Arun creates it. Zaal will explore the video clipper and lead discovery workflows in depth.

3. **Creator Coins + Farcaster:** Zaal is designing a creator-coin system using Farcaster (separate from DreamStarter). Zaal will send Arun the doc/idea for feedback/potential integration.

4. **India Activation:** Both are aligned on tapping India's Web3 community for the next 6 months. Zaal will attend Edge City (Oct 11-31) + DevCon (Nov). Arun already has Edge City lodging booked and is connecting Zaal with Telamon (Edge City co-organizer). Arun is also in talks with two artist residencies (Loops House, Dayden) and will introduce Zaal.

5. **Artisan Fund Participation:** Arun is pitching Zaal to get added to the Artisan Web3 fund as a co-founder/advisor. The fund is a 2027 fund (all capital is held until 2027; projects get "bags" of returns over the next 6 months as usage/returns compound). Arun will coordinate timing.

6. **API/MCP Deferred:** Arun acknowledged API/MCP possibility "down the line" but is keeping early access focused on non-technical users. Zaal's MCP use case (Claude-native posting trigger) is valid; Arun will revisit this after workflows stabilize.
