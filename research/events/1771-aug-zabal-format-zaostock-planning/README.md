## The core question: August ZABAL Games format

Zaal talked through three options for the final month (August):

1. **Daily opt-in + quadratic voting.** Any builder/artist/creator opts in that day; a shared repo (builders), shared song (artists), and a piece of art/culture posted daily; everyone does something related and the community quadratic-votes on engagement. Weekly, the top 7-10 work with mentors.
2. **Category teams.** Split everyone into artist / builder / creator teams; whoever opts in joins that team (e.g. ~40 artist, ~20 builder, ~10 creator); team challenges each week.
3. **Simple one-on-ones (leaning here).** Just do one-on-ones with **4 from each of the 3 categories**. Zaal's read: the individualistic and team options both create "way too many logistical issues," so keeping it simple is likely the move.

**Sam's input:** likes keeping the distinct categories; suggested pairing builders within the same category rather than cross-category head-to-head; floated "everybody gets a shot, best ones go first."
