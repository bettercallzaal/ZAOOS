## What's New: FarmDrop Statewide Network Profile

### Active 4-Region Network (refines doc 457's data)

| Region | Locations | Counties |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Downeast FarmDrop** | 15 locations | Hancock + Washington |
| **Northern Maine FarmDrop** | 5 locations | Aroostook |
| **Southern Maine FarmDrop** | 6 locations | Cumberland + Androscoggin |
| **Midmaine FarmDrop** | 6 locations | Somerset + Franklin + Kennebec + Penobscot + Waldo |

Total: **32+ community markets** (network-wide).

### Network-Wide Numbers

- **~120 farm partners** statewide
- **2,400+ individual customers**
- **50+ institutional buyers** (schools, nonprofits, stores)
- **200+ weekly orders** fulfilled across retail + institutional
- **20+ pickup/delivery points**
- **$800K+ cumulative sales** since founding
- **12+ part-time jobs** (HQ + regional coordinators, packers, drivers)
- Maine food security partnerships: **Healthy Acadia, Healthy Island Project, ACAP**

### Customer Segmentation

**For-profit:** Families, restaurants, retail stores, buying clubs, multifarm farm stands
**Non-profit:** Public health orgs, Early Childhood centers (ECEs), schools, hospitals, food pantries, meal sites

## 5-Year Vision (2026-2031)

- **2025-2026:** Tech platform pilot in TBD region, Southern ECE pilot, Downeast Washington County expansion
- **2026-2031:** Replicable national template (software + SOPs + shared logistics)
- **2028 target:** 3-5 pilot regions nationally beyond Maine
- **Long-term mission:** Nationally available technology for food waste reduction + food access + shared distribution infrastructure for rural local food economies

|--------|------|
| Maine food insecurity | 13.1% of Mainers, 18.7% of children (2022) |
