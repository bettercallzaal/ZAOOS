## Tanja's project - "The Reference Book"

A living, crowd-contributed reference book: **"A work of many voices - The Reference Book for Permaculture Villages, Family Homestead Settlements and Intentional Communities."**

- Not a finished work - a continuously evolving collection of contributions from practitioners, dreamers, builders, families.
- Structured around **13 points** (further developed from a 2012 manifesto): Family & Children, Health & Healing, Education & Learning, Research/Knowledge/Wisdom, Environment/Infrastructure/Building, Animals & Agriculture, Spirituality & Consciousness, Art & Creativity, Media & Communication, Law & Security, Economy & Sharing, Self-Organization & Community, External Relations & Peace.
- Core question: **"How do we want to live?"**
- Contribution rules: max 2 A4 pages, assigned to 1 of the 13 points, concrete + applicable, English, optional name/country/year/website.
- Submissions to **peasandlove@zohomail.eu** -> contributor receives a Substack link for ongoing exchange.
- Plan: actively organized at first, **over time develops into Fractal Circles** for self-organized continuation. Once a year a revised printed edition.
- Recruitment method Tanja proposed: the **Panurge-Sheep Method** - build a trusted circle of 8-30 people, share only within the circle, each person repeats with their own circle, exponential reach in ~3 hops. Groups of 8 = deep trust, groups of 30 = mass mobilization.

## Thread 1 - How the ZAO Fractal works (Zaal explained)

- **Synchronous** format. Previous fractal games were async; Zaal built a Discord bot that does it live in a 1-hour block.
- Flow: 15-min late start -> 6:15 welcome presentation -> 6:15-6:45 each person shares (4 min max) -> last 15 min voting -> on-chain submission. Everyone out within the hour.
- `/zaofractal` slash command starts the fractal. Bot pulls everyone in the voice channel into groups (4-6 people, max 6). Splits into 1-3 circles so a group of 12-16 still finishes in an hour.
- Voting: rank the group, level 6 (top contributor) down. 3 votes locks a level. Removed person drops out, repeat until all ranked.
- Vote criteria: own contributions (art / entrepreneurship / dev), collaborations, innovations, onboarding, supporting other members - all toward "advancing music, art, and technology toward the ZAO's long-term goals."
- Result submitted on-chain - all 6 mark the transaction = immutable verification of consensus.
- **ZAO Respect** = Soulbound, illiquid respect token, earned weekly through peer consensus. New members earn points just for introducing themselves week 1; returning members share what they contributed since last fractal.
- Camera incentive: extra points for turning video on (only needs a few seconds) - pushes authentic energy vs profile-pic meetings.
- ZAO framing Zaal used: "decentralized impact network for the creator economy", Z-talent artist organization, 4 objectives - empowerment, transparency, sustainability, equitable distribution.

## Thread 2 - The Fractal documentation gap

- Tanja repeatedly: she does not have the language for the technical side, cannot explain it to others, "that's the point where I lost people."
- Zaal's diagnosis: the Fractal group went from **1000+ to 6 active contributors**. ~6 people building for 2 years, volunteer, no money. Many "great idea" people leave for lack of bandwidth.
- Documentation is the bottleneck - "the documentation is the challenging piece." Zaal will use this transcript to build ZAO Fractal docs.
- Mikael built Fractal Circles, demoed it once, then went unreachable - same pattern as many half-built Fractal tools.

## Thread 3 - Fractal Circles + Farcaster concept

- Tanja's interest: use Fractal Circles so people pre-prepare contributions through the week. Biggest pain - people do great work but forget it when on the spot with 4 minutes.
- Async also lets a facilitator read an absent person's contribution; the rest of the group still votes consensus.
- Zaal's idea: combine the async side of Fractal Circles with Farcaster's social feed into one app. ~10k daily active users on Farcaster; easy contribution sharing could pull real contributors in.
