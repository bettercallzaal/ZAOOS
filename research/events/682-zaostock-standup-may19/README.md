## Notes and Context

**ZABAL Games is the re-energizer.** Zaal framed the build-a-thon as the lever that pulls attention back to ZAOstock over the coming weeks. June presentations come from ZAO members plus DeFi/regen-space volunteers (30 min each). The August finals are streamed - 8 mentors, 8 finalists. The winning build is meant to "help with ZAOstock." Each brand (ZAOstock, ZABAL, WaveWarZ, The ZAO) gets a context prompt builders can use as a track. Note: Zaal called it "The Z of All Games" on the call as wordplay; the program name is ZABAL Games.

**Sponsor friction is shaping process.** The 1K is committed but unpaid; Limonae is chasing the big sponsors. Zaal's takeaway: slow big-brand payouts make the organizer look bad to hackathon winners, so ZABAL Games needs formal T-minus deliverable tracking, more formal than typical Web3 sponsorship.

**Three new buildables (full research in doc 681):**
- NFC pro-ticket digital twins - every ticket buyer (virtual or IRL) gets an NFC networking card; a "Wall of Pros" displays virtual supporters; Trey could livestream the physical wall.
- ZAO Cards - new team under Canon, part of ZAO Festivals; mass-produce the existing ZABAL card with NFC chips, hand out at the event, teach attendees how it was made.
- ZAO Scribe - a Discord `-join` transcription bot, a second version of Craig, to log todos / conversation / history asynchronously.

**Also raised:** an AI-driven contribution-scoring algorithm (1-10 value per contribution, weekly points, token reward later); an AI DAO voting agent that synthesizes proposals and a member's Farcaster history into suggested votes; Zaal's DJ direction (Vibe Code DJing, community music as synthesis, crypto giveaways mid-set); Sisla from Flix.Fun joining as an advisor for the India crowd; a Devcon partnership conversation for a November India push; WaveWarZ revenue starting to flow back to wallets.

**Timeline anchors:** 138 days to ZAOstock (event ~Oct 3, 2026), ~87 days to the DCoop event (Aug 15 suggested test day - note: this is later than the "July" framing in earlier memory), 10 weeks of AV-crew access available.
