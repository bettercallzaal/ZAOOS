## Thread 1 - Zee3 onboard + ZABAL Games briefing

### The Idea

Zaal opened by walking Zee3 through ZABAL Games: a three-month Build-A-Thon. Month one (June) = open-ended workshops where every ZAO-network builder gives a ~30-min presentation on their tool/skill. Month two (July) = open-ended Build-A-Thon, anyone submits anything. Month three (August) = eight ZAO teammates pick a champion from the open submissions; the two of them build something; whoever wins, wins V0. Iterates again next summer.

Zaal's framing of the loop: "us going hard and getting out there and hitting the vitality loop - having a lot of content, having ways to distribute it, having a lot of people coming in, and eyeballs on our stuff." Target audience: Empire Builder, WaveWarZ, iBand, COC Concertz, plus Zee3's own network. The workshop month doubles as a content bank any AI harness can be pointed at.

Zee3's role: two workshops (COC + CapCut/video editing) and pulling his people in. He said yes immediately: "I'd be more than happy to do that."

### Why It Matters

This is the third recorded ZABAL Games onboarding ([Tyler doc 714](../714-tyler-zabal-zaostock-may22/), [Arthur doc 711](../711-arthur-wavewarz-base-call-may19/), now Zee3). Each mentor commitment makes the June workshop month real. The whitepaper dependency is now explicit - it's not parallel work, it's a gate.

### Next Step

Zaal finishes the ZAO whitepaper. Zee3 calls his people, starts drafting his two workshop sessions.
