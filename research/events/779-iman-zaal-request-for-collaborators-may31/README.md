## How the feature works (as described)

1. A member holding X amount of ZAO token posts a project request: the idea, what is already done under their brand, what still needs doing, the vision/goal, who they would love as collaborators, and who is already interested.
2. The request is notified out to everyone.
3. Any ZAO holder can step in to support: "here is my availability and how much time I can invest this month." If they have no time, they can offer amplification (retweets/support) instead.
4. The requester reviews applicants by ZAO respect, tenure in the community, and reputation signals (X activity, Farcaster red flags) to decide who should actually be on the team.
5. Quorum idea: hold ~1,000 ZAO to propose your own project.
