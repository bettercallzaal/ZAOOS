## What FounderCheck is

A founder/product-validation mini app that scores an idea across **four pillars**:
1. **Who** - the user / ideal customer (consistently the lowest score; JC has data; "if you get Who right, everything else falls in place").
2. **What you're building** - including jobs (what the job does for the user).
3. **Channels** - where users hang out, why they should care, how you reach them.
4. **Adoption / friction / monetization.**

Token-gated (you spend tokens to run reports / copy the plan). JC's **ICP**: SaaS / micro-SaaS founders who "shipped to crickets" - built for months only to find no one wanted it because they never asked. The **Meme Lord** feature (in progress) optimizes the *memetic* version of your app - "you walk into a room, say something, everyone feels seen, they repeat it." 15 markers; Claude can also do a 15/15 pass agency-style.
