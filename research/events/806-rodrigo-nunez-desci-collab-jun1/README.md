## Who is Rodrigo Nuñez

Mexico-based decentralized-science (DeSci) and regen builder, focused on coral / reef / ocean conservation. Met Zaal through the ETH Denver / ETH Boulder regen circuit. Runs a conservation DAO - heard as **MezuriDAO** (spelling unconfirmed, also garbled in transcript as "Mesuri DAO" / "mezzo reefed") - which he describes as a proto-DAO. It is getting incubated by **Molecule** (the DeSci funding platform) and is building a **knowledge graph for conservation**. Because a Mexican NGO cannot legally hold a token (even a governance token - it would trigger Bank of Mexico regulation), he set up an **LLC in Prospera** (the special-jurisdiction zone) so the project can scale with a token-friendly wrapper. Currently organizing **Ocean Week in Cozumel** (this week, 5-8pm daily) and supporting a Reef Awareness Day. Deep hackathon background: organized a science hackathon at Harvard ~a year ago and an RSI science hackathon, and has competed globally (Bangkok, Mexico, Argentina, Monaco). Has an **Artizen** curated project at 10,000/10,000 with a "B2" launch imminent (call with Rene). Also converting his childhood home into a coliving space.
