## What the workshop covers

### The ZAO Fractal - the basics
- The ZAO Fractal is presented as the **first ZAO project**. It meets **weekly, Mondays 6pm Eastern**, at `discord.thezao.com`. The Fractal app is at **zao.frapps.xyz**.
- **The ZAO = a decentralized impact network for the creator economy.**
- **ZAO = ZTalent Artist Organization** - a community-driven guild where musicians, visual artists, and builders reclaim their art, data, and revenue.
- Powered by **soulbound, illiquid $ZAO Respect tokens**, earned weekly through peer consensus.
- **Four objectives:** Empowerment, Transparency, Sustainability, Equitable Distribution.

### The Respect Game (how the meeting runs)
- Members split into **groups of max 6** and share **4-minute updates** - new members give an intro, returning members share what they have contributed since the last Fractal.
- Results are **submitted on-chain** at the end.
- **Turn on your camera = +10 ZAO Respect.**

### What counts as a contribution (voting criteria)
You are contributing anything you believe advances the ZAO's vision of advancing music, art, and technology. Five ways:
1. **Contribution** - impactful work that made ZAO stronger this week (what you are developing / making as an artist / building as an entrepreneur).
2. **Collaboration** - teamwork, uplifting others, working with anyone out there.
3. **Innovation** - creative thinking, cutting-edge or groundbreaking work.
4. **Onboarding New Members** - bringing people into ZAO, on-chain music, or crypto as a whole.
5. **Supporting ZAO Community Members** - amplifying a member's work on socials, attending their show, buying merch, supporting them beyond the group.

### The Discord tooling (two bots)
- **/timer (Dash Timer)** - starts the session and posts a "Meet Your Group" embed so everyone sees who is in their group (pulled from each member's `#introductions` post). Shows who is currently speaking with a 4-minutes-per-speaker countdown, a "Pick who goes next" dropdown, and a "Come Back" option to return to a skipped speaker. Tracks how many speakers have gone and how many are left. Members without an intro are flagged under "Missing Intros."
- **/zao-fractal (Discord Consensus Bot)** - created by the facilitator. The group is built from whoever is in the voice channel. Everyone votes the **Level 6** person first (who contributed most this week), then continues down Level 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 until everyone is ranked, producing the ZAO Fractal results.

### Respect token mechanics (from the deck)
- **OG ZAO Respect (ERC-20):** one-time distributions for contributions like introductions, articles, and being featured. Used **pre-Fractal 73**.
- **ZOR Respect (ERC-1155 via ORDAO):** weekly consensus results from the Respect Game, submitted **on-chain on Optimism**. **Fractal 74 onward.**
