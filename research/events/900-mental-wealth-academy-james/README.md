## Findings

### What Mental Wealth Academy is

- A DeSci "micro-university for intellectual refreshment" - a **12-week**, fully self-paced cohort where you "do the work on yourself" through journaling (Morning Pages), games (e.g. spotting cognitive distortions), and activities instead of lectures.
- Built across **four psychological pillars**, "designed by a psychologist with 12 years of research experience." Each week is sealed as you finish it, kept **fully private** (no private data collected, async), and unlocked with prizes after completion.
- **Gamified like an MMO** - earn points, unlock quests, level up - with on-chain governance, an AI co-pilot, and statistical dashboards tracking progress. Tagline: "Helping people reclaim their agency, reshape their future, own their mental wealth."
- Positioned as a "next-gen cohort for scientists & agents" - entry for both humans and AI agents - with a proprietary AI model, **B.L.U.E.**, for "simulate new worlds... earn while you learn."
- Under the hood it is a modular **LMS toolkit** for "the digitization and token-based governance of physical communities" in Web3, with a deliberate design focus on safety/OPSEC "as empowerment, not fear."
- Founded **2025** (Sheridan, WY / Philadelphia). Ecosystem logos shown: Anthropic, Aragon, Gitcoin, the American Psychological Association, ElizaOS, Ethereum, Base, Chainlink, Supabase, Artizen.

### Who James is

- **jamesdesign.eth** on Farcaster ("Optimistic Boy"), also Jhinn / **jhinnbay** (Twitter), **@mentalwealthdao** (project X). Based in Philadelphia.
- **UI/UX designer + behavioral researcher, Psych BA**, working in health tech / DeSci. Runs Metawave Studio.
- Builds the AI agents too - **Azura OS** ("the love bot", @azuraos / @daemonagent) is part of the MWA stack.
