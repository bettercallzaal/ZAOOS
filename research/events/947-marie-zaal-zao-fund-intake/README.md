He positioned the fund as a tool for longer-term collaboration and offered to introduce Marie to Civil Monkey (Berlin-based Web3 organizer) and share details of his own music events project in Maine.

## Quotes

"So our vision is to bring the profit margin, the data and IP rights back to independent artists. So big goal ultimately is like I'm building a bunch of software myself and it's all open source, all free for anyone to use. And the goal is I want to teach any musician at any point how to make a Web site, how to make a smart contract and how to get paid from someone halfway across the world and get 100 percent of the dollar that that person spends."

"So if you're directly looking for the $12,000 over the next two weeks, it might not be the best place to spend time specifically geared towards funding.
