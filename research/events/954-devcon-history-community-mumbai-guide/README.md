## Mumbai Ground Guide (condensed; full data in agent transcript)

- **Venue:** Jio World Centre, BKC - business district, 400K workers, walkable inside, dead at night.
- **Stay:** Bandra West (music/cafe/nightlife scene, 5-10 min Uber to venue, ~2km). Sofitel BKC bridges both. Book by early Sept; Diwali surge 40-60%.
- **Airport-BKC:** 9 miles. Metro Line 3: 19 min, ₹20 (~$0.25), every 4-8 min - most reliable. Uber ₹300-500 ($4-6).
- **Getting around:** Metro Line 3 (airport-BKC-Colaba spine) + Uber/Ola (₹20.66-22.72/km; BKC-Bandra ₹150-250). Autos banned in South Mumbai. Local trains: first class ₹15-20, avoid 8-10am/5-8pm.
- **Music venues:** antiSOCIAL (Lower Parel, 450-500 cap, indie/electronic hub), Bonobo + Veranda + Kitty Su (Bandra West), NCPA (Nariman Point, 700+ events/yr). No major festival Nov 3-6; Magnetic Fields is Feb in Rajasthan. Check Bandsintown near date.
- **Diwali Nov 8 (Sun), festival Nov 6-10:** fireworks + festive energy after Devcon ends; heavy traffic Nov 6-8; some closures Nov 8-9; EF timed this deliberately - staying through Nov 9 = cultural payoff.
- **Weather:** dry season, 82-93F days, no rain. Cotton, sunscreen, light jacket.
- **Money:** UPI One World - foreign tourists can now load a prepaid UPI wallet (passport + visa at airport kiosk, up to ₹50,000). Plus ₹5-10K cash. Tipping light (5-15% fine dining only).
- **SIM:** Jio kiosk at BOM arrivals, ₹299/28 days, 1.5GB/day + unlimited 5G, 15 min with passport. Or Airalo/Holafly eSIM pre-loaded.
- **Safety/food:** bottled water only, street food fresh-cooked is fine (vada pav ₹15-30), app-cabs not street hails, Bandra West/BKC/Colaba all safe zones. Mumbai is heavily vegetarian-friendly.
