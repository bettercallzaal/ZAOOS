## Engagement Playbook (condensed)

- 5 formats: summit ($10-25K), showcase/builder night ($5-10K), VIP dinner ($3-8K), after-party ($10-30K), hackathon workshop + performance ($5-15K).
- T-45 timeline: sponsor prospecting T-45, venue + 3 sponsor commits T-30, Luma live T-21, 3 amplification waves T-10/7/3, soundcheck T-5, post-event content T+7 to T+30.
- 80% of budget to artists + marketing, 20% to venue/bar. Thesis in one sentence. Luma is the RSVP rail.
- Precedents: Refraction (membership pass funds recurring Art Basel/NFT.NYC residencies), rAIve (Devcon after-party + gasless NFT hunts), DCentralize (conference roadshow + DAO lineup voting).
