## Communities Most Relevant to ZAO

### 1. Eden Fractal — Primary Collaborator
- Only major community on Superchain (Base)
- Uses same stack: ORDAO, Fractalgram, Hats Protocol
- ZAO's Optimism deployment is Superchain-compatible
- **Action:** Attend bi-weekly events, learn their process, explore cross-fractal Respect

### 2. Art Fractal — Creative Parallel
- Local artist community in San Diego using fractal processes
- Closest parallel to ZAO's music focus
- **Action:** Connect to learn how they adapted fractal ranking for creative output

### 3. Aquadac — Process Innovation
- Uses fractal ranking for personal development goals (not code/governance)
- 12-week seasons with rotating themes
- **Action:** Borrow their season/theme rotation concept for ZAO music seasons

### 4. Roy Fractal — Scale Proof
- 700+ participants proves fractal can scale beyond small groups
- Uses YouTube livestreams for events
- **Action:** Study their scaling approach if ZAO grows beyond 100 members

## ZAO's Unique Position

ZAO is:
1. The **only music-focused fractal** in the ecosystem
2. The **only active fractal on Optimism** (since OF paused)
3. One of only **2 communities on Superchain** (with Eden Fractal on Base)
4. The only fractal with a **full social client** (ZAO OS on Farcaster)
5. Building the first **fractal-powered music curation** system

No other fractal community has:
- Audio playback in breakout rooms
- Music submission + peer ranking
- Artist reputation tied to governance power
- Integration with a social protocol (Farcaster)
