## Why Nouns and Why Now

Nouns DAO is the largest on-chain DAO by treasury ($40M+) that actively funds community-led work at the small scale. Nouns Small Grants and Prop House rounds run continuously — unlike Gitcoin rounds with fixed timelines or Optimism Retro Funding which is retrospective.

ZABAL is a natural fit for Nouns:
1. **Community governance receipt**: 100+ consecutive Fractal governance weeks makes ZABAL credible — Nouns values operational DAOs over paper DAOs.
2. **Builder output**: ZABAL S2 will produce 10-20 builder projects. Nouns gets attribution for the cohort output.
3. **Cross-chain narrative**: ZAO is on Optimism; Nouns is on mainnet. A Nouns-funded ZABAL cohort = first mainnet-Optimism DAO builder bridge in music.
4. **ZAOstock showcase**: Nouns community members could attend ZAOstock Oct 3 in person (ZAOville, Maine) — tangible community connection.
