### Full Proposal (~500 words)

```
**Background: The ZAO and ZABAL**

The ZAO is a decentralized music organization on Optimism Mainnet. Since March 2024, we've run 63 consecutive weekly governance sessions using Fractal Respect — a peer-ranking system where ZOR tokens flow to contributors based on community assessment, not token holdings.

ZABAL (ZAO Builder and Artist League) is our 13-week cohort program for builders and musicians. Season 1 ran with 30+ participants. Season 2 (Sep 1 – Nov 30, 2026) will have two tracks:

**Builder Track**: Ship at least 1 working artifact (app, tool, ZAOOS research doc, design) + attend 10/13 sessions
**Musician Track**: Complete 3 WaveWarZ battles + upload 1 track to Arweave + attend 10/13 sessions

**Why Micro-Grants Work**

ZABAL participants commit 1 hour/week for 13 weeks — without compensation. The program is run on ZOR governance tokens, which are earned but non-transferable. A small completion grant ($320 ETH equivalent) at the end of the program is a meaningful incentive for participants who completed the full commitment.

Proof of work is onchain. Builders have public GitHub repos or deployed URLs. Musicians have Arweave-archived tracks and WaveWarZ battle records. This isn't a trust-based grant — it's a proof-of-work payout.

**The ZAOstock Connection**

ZAOstock (October 3, 2026) is a free outdoor music festival in Ellsworth, Maine — the Season 2 capstone event. ZABAL S2 musicians will perform on stage. ZABAL S2 builders will support the live WaveWarZ battle from the technical side. The micro-grants fund the participants who make this possible.

**Why Nouns?**

Nouns is the gold standard of public goods funding in the onchain ecosystem. ZABAL aligns with Nouns' values: open source, CC-BY, consistent community cadence, and proof-of-work rather than proof-of-concept.

The ZAO has 63 consecutive governance sessions — we don't abandon projects. We document everything (1,450+ CC-BY docs). Our participants ship real things. The 10 ZABAL S2 completers who receive this grant will be the kind of builders and musicians who proved it over 13 weeks.

**How 1 ETH is used**

- 10 micro-grants at 0.1 ETH each (current ~$320/grant)
- Grant disbursement: on completion of ZABAL S2 (Nov 30, 2026)
- Disbursement method: direct Optimism or Base wallet transfer (no multisig complexity)
- Recipients: verified via ZAOOS participant log + onchain WW battle records

All grant recipients will be documented in the ZAO OS (CC-BY, permanent public record).

**Timeline**

- Aug 1: Submit to Prop House
- Aug 22: ZABAL S2 applications close
- Sep 1: ZABAL S2 Week 1 kick-off
- Nov 30: ZABAL S2 graduation ceremony
- Dec 1-7: 10 grants disbursed

**About Zaal**

Zaal Panthaki is the co-founder of The ZAO and WaveWarZ. He has run 63 consecutive weekly governance sessions and built a 1,245-battle music platform. He is the primary ZABAL S2 facilitator. @bettercallzaal on X and Farcaster.
```
