# 1573 — ZAO Fractal New Member Welcome Packet

> **Send this to every new Fractal member on their first session day.** This is the single doc that answers every first-session question.

---

## Welcome to the ZAO Fractal

The ZAO Fractal is a weekly governance session where members rank each other's contributions and earn Respect (ZOR tokens) based on peer judgment. It has run **100+ consecutive weeks without a miss** — no capital required, no tokens needed to start, just showing up and contributing to The ZAO.

Your first session will feel unfamiliar. That's normal. This packet tells you exactly what to do.

---

## Before Your First Session (Do These Today)

**1. Tell ZOE you're coming.**
Message @zaoclaw_bot on Telegram: "I'm joining the Fractal this week." ZOE will send you the session link and add you to the pre-session reminder list.

**2. Create an Optimism wallet (if you haven't).**
You'll need one to receive ZOR tokens (your on-chain Respect). Use Coinbase Wallet (simplest) or MetaMask and set it to Optimism Mainnet. You don't need any funds yet — wallet creation is free.

**3. Read the session guide (5 min).**
Doc 1475 walks through the 60-minute session flow. Skim it so the format isn't a surprise.

**4. Prepare one contribution to mention.**
Think about one thing you did for The ZAO or WaveWarZ in the past week. Even small things count: "I promoted a WaveWarZ battle to my community" or "I read the fractal docs to prepare." In your first sessions, showing up prepared is itself a contribution.

**5. Submit your pre-session log by 6PM Thursday.**
ZOE posts a reminder at 6AM. Send a quick message to @zaoclaw_bot or reply in the ZAO main Telegram with the log format (see doc 1561). First-session log can be short — just "I attended my first session and read the docs."

## What Happens in the Session (60 Minutes)

| Time | What |
|------|------|
| 7:00 PM | Welcome + week-in-review. Zaal opens and shares ZAO news. |
| 7:10 PM | Fractal formation. Members split into groups of 3–4. |
| 7:12 PM | Breakout round. Each person shares contributions (1–3 min). Group ranks. |
| 7:35 PM | Report-back. Each group shares who ranked highest. |
| 7:45 PM | Respect score submission. Top contributors are recorded. |
| 7:50 PM | Community updates + close. |

**Your job in the breakout:** Be specific. "I did [thing] for ZAO/WaveWarZ this week" — name it, point to it if you can (PR #, doc, event you attended, artist you onboarded). Vague contribution ("I was active") doesn't rank well.

**Your job after the ranking:** Accept your rank. In your first sessions, you'll likely rank at the bottom — this is expected and normal (see "Sessions 1–6" below). Don't argue; come back next week.

## How ZOR Tokens Work

**ZOR (Respect1155)** is an ERC-1155 token on Optimism Mainnet (`0x9885CCeEf7E8371Bf8d6f2413723D25917E7445c`). You earn it by ranking well in Fractal sessions.

What ZOR does for you:
- **Governance weight** — the more ZOR you hold, the more weight your vote carries in ORDAO decisions
- **Leaderboard rank** — your position on the ZAO Fractal leaderboard (visible at zao.frapps.xyz)
- **Season record** — your ZOR balance is your on-chain participation history
- **WaveWarZ vote** — ZOR holders vote on MAIN battle winners and charity selections
- **ZAOstock** — ZOR holders are the governance body at the live Oct 3 governance vote

What ZOR does NOT do:
- It's not tradeable on exchanges — don't look for it on Uniswap
- It doesn't automatically compound or earn yield
- It doesn't grant financial returns

**Check your ZOR balance:**
- Optimism Blockscout: optimism.blockscout.com → paste your wallet address → Tokens tab
- ZAO dashboard (if available): zao.frapps.xyz → your wallet → Fractal tab

## Your First 6 Sessions: What to Expect

| Session | Typical experience |
|---------|--------------------|
| 1 | You rank last or near-last. Your group doesn't know you yet. **This is fine.** |
| 2 | You might rank higher if you came prepared and contributed something specific. |
| 3 | Group starts to recognize your name and contribution style. |
| 4–5 | Consistent attendance is noticed. Steady contributions build trust. |
| 6 | You have a track record. Peers can vouch for your consistency. |

**Rule of thumb:** By session 6, if you've shown up consistently and done one concrete ZAO thing each week, you'll be ranking in the middle of your group.

Don't try to rank first in session 1. Try to show up, learn the format, and do one real ZAO contribution.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need crypto to join?**
A: Not for your first session. You'll need an Optimism wallet by session 3 or so to receive ZOR. Wallet creation is free.

**Q: Can I join from anywhere in the world?**
A: Yes. Sessions run Thursday evenings EST. Remote attendance via Telegram or Juke is fully supported. Africa Fractal sessions run on a different schedule — ask ZOE if you're in Africa.

**Q: What if I can't make a session?**
A: Let ZOE know you'll miss it. Your ZOR balance won't be affected by a single miss, but consistency is weighted in peer ranking (see sessions 1–6 ramp).

**Q: How do I know if my contribution is "enough"?**
A: There's no minimum. Read the contribution rubric (doc 1542) to see what peers typically value. One specific, verifiable thing per week is a good baseline.

**Q: Can I propose something for the group to vote on?**
A: Yes. Read the governance proposal template (doc 1568) and submit via ZOE or GitHub PR. Proposals for the Oct 3 ZAOstock vote are due Sep 15.

**Q: How do I know who "The ZAO" is?**
A: ZOR holders and active Fractal participants are The ZAO. ZAO leadership is Zaal Panthaki. Check thezao.xyz for the broader ZAO ecosystem.
