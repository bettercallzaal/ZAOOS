# 1579 — WaveWarZ × Fractal Flywheel

> **The two-surface play:** WaveWarZ gives artists the stage. The ZAO Fractal gives artists the governance rights. ZOR tokens are the bridge. An artist who earns ZOR in Fractal sessions becomes a co-owner of the WaveWarZ outcomes. An artist who votes in WaveWarZ battles becomes part of the community that runs Fractal governance.

---

## Why This Matters Now

Africa Battle Week runs **July 24–25, 2026**. ZOR holders vote on the MAIN battle winner and the charity allocation. Every WaveWarZ artist in Africa who earns ZOR before July 24 gets a real say in the outcome.

The ZAO Fractal is the fastest way to earn ZOR before Africa Battle Week.

---

## The Flywheel in Three Steps

```
WaveWarZ artist battles             →   joins ZAO Fractal to earn ZOR
ZAO Fractal peer recognition        →   earns ZOR each week
ZOR voting power                    →   influences WaveWarZ battle outcomes + ZAOstock governance
WaveWarZ battle outcomes            →   validates artist's community standing
Community standing                  →   ranks higher in Fractal → earns more ZOR
```

Each surface reinforces the other. An artist who only battles has no governance voice. An artist who only attends Fractal has limited connection to live music. The combination creates full membership in The ZAO.

---

## Surface 1: WaveWarZ (The Stage)

WaveWarZ runs weekly music battles where community members vote for the best-performing artist. A MAIN battle decides the top ZAO-championed artist. Artists who win or place well gain:

- ZAO network visibility (Farcaster, Telegram, ZAO social channels)
- Booking interest from ZAO-connected festivals and events
- Recognition in the ZAO Fractal community as a contributor

What WaveWarZ does NOT give without Fractal: **governance rights**. Battle participation alone does not earn ZOR. You can't vote on ZAOstock decisions or charity allocations from WaveWarZ participation alone.

---

## Surface 2: ZAO Fractal (The Governance)

The ZAO Fractal is a weekly governance session where members rank each other's contributions and earn Respect (ZOR tokens) based on peer judgment. Running **100+ consecutive weeks** on Thursdays at 7PM EST.

What Fractal gives an artist that WaveWarZ doesn't:

| What | Why It Matters to an Artist |
|------|----------------------------|
| ZOR tokens | Vote weight in WaveWarZ MAIN battles + ZAOstock decisions |
| Peer recognition | Weekly acknowledgment for music, promotion, and community contributions |
| Governance history | On-chain record of consistent participation |
| Season standings | Visible ranking across all ZAO contributors |
| Network trust | Being known by ZAO builders, connectors, and researchers, not just other artists |

---

## The ZOR Bridge

ZOR (Respect1155, contract `0x9885CCeEf7E8371Bf8d6f2413723D25917E7445c` on Optimism) is what connects the two surfaces.

**How you earn ZOR:** Weekly Fractal sessions. Peer ranking. The higher you rank, the more ZOR you earn that session.

**What ZOR unlocks in WaveWarZ:**
- **MAIN battle vote:** Each ZOR holder casts one vote for the MAIN battle winner. More ZOR = more vote weight (exact weighting determined by ORDAO).
- **Charity vote:** Africa Battle Week charity allocation is decided by ZOR holder vote (Jul 24–25, Snapshot, see doc 1575).
- **ZAOstock:** Oct 3, 2026 governance vote at the live ZAOstock festival. ZOR determines your vote weight for all proposals.

**The math:** An artist who attends 4 Fractal sessions before July 24 earns ZOR from those sessions, enough to participate in the Africa Battle Week vote. An artist who attends 12 sessions before Oct 3 has meaningful governance weight at ZAOstock.

## For WaveWarZ Africa Artists Specifically

Africa Fractal sessions run on a **separate schedule** adapted for African time zones (ask @zaoclaw_bot for the current time). The Africa Fractal is the fastest onboarding path for Africa-based artists into ZAO governance. See doc 1474 for full onboarding plan.

The Africa Battle Week charity vote (Jul 24–25) is the first major governance moment for ZOR holders. Artists who earn ZOR before then vote. Artists who missed it will have the next opportunity at ZAOstock (Oct 3) and in ongoing WaveWarZ MAIN battles.

**Talking points when recruiting Africa artists into Fractal:**

> "You battle in WaveWarZ. Now earn the right to vote on who wins. Join the Fractal this Thursday — show up for 4 sessions before July 24 and you vote on the charity for Africa Battle Week."

> "You've already demonstrated you're serious about your music. The Fractal is where The ZAO community formally recognizes that. You earn ZOR, which means you're part of the governance body that shapes WaveWarZ's direction."

> "ZOR is not a speculative token. It's your governance credential. You can't buy it — you earn it by showing up and contributing."

## The WaveWarZ × Fractal Onboarding Path

For a WaveWarZ artist who has never attended Fractal, the sequence:

**Day 1 (or as soon as possible before Africa Battle Week):**
1. Message @zaoclaw_bot: "I'm a WaveWarZ artist, I want to join the Fractal to earn ZOR before Africa Battle Week."
2. ZOE sends the session link and adds the artist to the Thursday reminder list.
3. Artist creates an Optimism wallet (Coinbase Wallet, free, 5 minutes — see doc 1573 step 2).

**Thursday session:**
1. Artist attends Fractal session at 7PM EST (or Africa schedule equivalent).
2. In breakout: shares their WaveWarZ battle this week, any promotion they did, any new artists they referred.
3. Peers rank the contribution. ZOR earned that session goes to the artist's wallet.

**Session 2–4 (before July 24):**
- Artist has enough ZOR to participate in the Africa Battle Week charity vote (Snapshot).
- Artist is now part of ZAO governance with an on-chain track record.

**Long term (Sessions 6+, ZAOstock Oct 3):**
- Artist has accumulated enough ZOR to be a meaningful voice in ZAOstock governance decisions.
- Artist is recognized by the full ZAO community (builders, researchers, connectors) not just music community.

## For Fractal Facilitators Recruiting WaveWarZ Artists

**What to say:**

Do say:
- "You've already done the work. Fractal is where you get recognized for it."
- "WaveWarZ battles count as contribution. You can rank on that alone."
- "ZOR is the credential that lets you vote on what happens to WaveWarZ."

Don't say:
- "Join a DAO" — it sounds technical and distant
- "You need to earn governance tokens" — sounds speculative
- "We use on-chain voting with Snapshot" — too mechanical for a first pitch

**What qualifies as WaveWarZ artist contribution in a Fractal session:**
- Entering a battle (especially a MAIN battle)
- Promoting a WaveWarZ battle to your community
- Referring a new artist to WaveWarZ or The ZAO
- Voting on a battle (shows community engagement)
- Creating content about the battle (social media, voice memo, reel)

All of these are valid Fractal contributions. Use the rubric (doc 1542) column for "musician" role.

---

## Why This Flywheel Stops Without Both Surfaces

| If you have WaveWarZ without Fractal | If you have Fractal without WaveWarZ |
|--------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Artists battle but have no governance stake | Members earn ZOR but lack a live performance community |
| Community is an audience, not a governance body | Governance is technical, not tied to creative output |
| Winners are chosen by fans, not by committed stakeholders | No shared event or competition to contribute TO |
| Artists leave when they stop winning | No concrete way to demonstrate music contribution |

Both surfaces together: artists who are invested in outcomes (Fractal members with ZOR) watch and vote on battles they care about, creating a community of stakeholders rather than spectators.
