# 1580 — Africa Battle Week Charity Vote — ZOR Holder Guide

> **Time-sensitive.** ZOR holders vote on the Africa Battle Week charity allocation **July 24–25, 2026** via Snapshot. This guide tells you exactly how.

---

## What You're Voting On

The ZAO Fractal community allocates a portion of Africa Battle Week proceeds to a qualifying African music/arts charity. ZOR holders — people who have earned Respect tokens through the ZAO Fractal — decide which charity receives the allocation.

**Vote window:** July 24–25, 2026  
**Platform:** Snapshot (gasless, wallet-signed vote — no ETH fees)  
**Eligibility:** Any wallet holding ZOR (Respect1155, `0x9885CCeEf7E8371Bf8d6f2413723D25917E7445c`) on Optimism Mainnet

---

## Step-by-Step: How to Vote

### 1. Confirm you have ZOR

Your wallet must hold at least 1 ZOR to vote. Check:

**Option A — Optimism Blockscout:**
1. Go to `optimism.blockscout.com`
2. Paste your wallet address in the search bar
3. Click the **Tokens** tab
4. Look for "ZOR" or "Respect1155" — any balance qualifies you

**Option B — ZAO dashboard:**
1. Go to `zao.frapps.xyz`
2. Connect your wallet
3. Check your Fractal tab — your ZOR balance is shown

If you have 0 ZOR, you are not yet eligible. You can still earn ZOR by attending ZAO Fractal sessions before July 24 (see doc 1573 for how to join).

---

### 2. Go to the Snapshot space

The ZAO Snapshot space holds the active vote. Get the link from @zaoclaw_bot on Telegram or from the ZAO main Telegram channel (ZOE posts it when the vote opens).

**ZAO Snapshot space:** `snapshot.org/#/zaofractal.eth` (confirm with ZOE — exact space slug from doc 1575)

---

### 3. Connect your wallet

On the Snapshot page:
1. Click **Connect Wallet** (top right)
2. Select your wallet type (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect)
3. Set your wallet to **Optimism Mainnet** (not Ethereum mainnet)
4. Approve the connection

Snapshot reads your ZOR balance automatically. If it shows 0, confirm your wallet is on Optimism Mainnet, not Ethereum.

---

### 4. Review the charity options

The vote will show 2–4 qualifying African music/arts charities. Read each description before voting. The charities are pre-vetted by ZAO leadership for:
- Active in African music/arts communities
- Able to receive crypto or fiat transfer
- Mission aligned with WaveWarZ Africa (artist development, community access, music education)

---

### 5. Cast your vote

1. Select the charity you want to support
2. Click **Vote**
3. Sign the message with your wallet (this is gasless — no ETH required, just a wallet signature)
4. Your vote is recorded

You can change your vote until the window closes. The last vote you cast stands.

---

### 6. Check results

After July 25, the results are visible on the Snapshot page and will be announced in ZAO Telegram by ZOE. The winning charity receives the allocation.

---

## Common Issues

**"I can see the vote but my balance shows 0"**
- Make sure your wallet is on Optimism Mainnet (chain ID 10), not Ethereum mainnet (chain ID 1)
- In MetaMask: Settings → Networks → Optimism. In Coinbase Wallet: tap the network selector and choose Optimism.

**"I earned ZOR but the snapshot doesn't see it"**
- Snapshot takes a "snapshot" of balances at a fixed block height, set when the vote was created (doc 1575). If you earned ZOR AFTER that block, it may not count for this vote.
- Your ZOR counts for future votes. Check with @zaoclaw_bot for the exact snapshot block.

**"My wallet isn't connecting"**
- Try a different browser (Chrome/Brave work best with MetaMask)
- Clear your browser cache and reconnect
- Message @zaoclaw_bot for support

**"I don't have a wallet yet"**
- This vote requires a wallet that holds ZOR. If you're just joining the Fractal, you'll earn ZOR starting from your first session and can vote in the next eligible vote.
- For setup instructions: doc 1573 (new member welcome), step 2.

## Why Your Vote Matters

ZOR-weighted charity voting is one of the few places in music/arts philanthropy where the community that runs the program — not a board or executive — decides where money goes. Your ZOR is your governance credential. Use it.

If you're a WaveWarZ Africa artist who earned ZOR by attending Fractal sessions: this vote is directly about your community. The charity you vote for receives support from the same event that featured your music.

---

## After the Vote

The Africa Battle Week governance vote is practice for ZAOstock Oct 3, 2026 — where ZOR holders vote on larger ZAO direction decisions at the live festival. Your ZOR balance at that point determines your vote weight. For how ZAOstock voting works: doc 1568 (governance proposal template) and doc 1553 (ZOR holder voting guide).
