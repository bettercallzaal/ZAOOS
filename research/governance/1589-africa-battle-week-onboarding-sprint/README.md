# 1589 — Africa Battle Week Onboarding Sprint (Jul 19–24)

> **5-day sprint** to get as many WaveWarZ Africa artists as possible into the ZAO Fractal before the Africa Battle Week charity vote opens July 24. Goal: every artist who has battled in WaveWarZ Africa should know they can vote — and know how to earn ZOR to do so.

---

## Sprint Goal

By July 24 (vote open): at least 10 new WaveWarZ Africa participants have attended ≥1 Fractal session and hold ZOR on Optimism Mainnet, qualifying them to vote on the Africa Battle Week charity allocation.

Secondary goal: establish these artists as regular Fractal participants beyond the vote.

## Day 1 (Jul 19): Artist Identification + DM Outreach

**Who to reach:**
1. Every artist who has entered a WaveWarZ Africa battle (check WaveWarZ battle history)
2. Artists in ZAO Telegram who are Africa-based
3. Artists in @ZAOstockTeamBot who have Africa connections
4. Artists referred by existing ZAO Africa contacts (Zambia, Nigeria, Ghana, etc.)

**Prioritize:** Artists who have already submitted for WaveWarZ Africa MAIN battle — they have the highest motivation to vote on the outcome.

**DM script (use Block 10 from doc 1586, adapted):**

> "Hey [Name] — You've been doing incredible work in WaveWarZ Africa battles. 
>
> This week (Jul 24-25) ZAO Fractal members vote on the Africa Battle Week charity allocation — which African music/arts charity gets a direct allocation from the event. 
>
> To vote, you need ZOR — the Respect token that ZAO Fractal members earn by attending the weekly session. Thursday's Fractal session is this week at 7PM EST.
>
> If you come to Thursday's session, share what you've been creating or promoting, and earn your first ZOR — you'll be eligible to vote on Jul 24.
>
> Want the link? Just say yes."

**Send via:** Telegram DMs, WaveWarZ Discord (if available), WhatsApp (for Africa contacts who prefer it).

## Day 4 (Jul 22): Wallet Setup Day

For any confirmed attendee who doesn't have an Optimism wallet:

**Wallet setup help (5 min per person):**
1. Download Coinbase Wallet app (easiest for mobile)
2. Create wallet → save recovery phrase somewhere safe
3. Switch to Optimism Mainnet (Settings → Networks → Optimism)
4. Send the wallet address to @zaoclaw_bot so Zaal can note it for the session

No ETH or funds needed for wallet creation or for Snapshot voting. ZOR is gasless to receive.

**For the technically hesitant:**
> "Creating the wallet takes 5 minutes and costs nothing. You don't need to buy any crypto. You'll earn ZOR from the session — it arrives automatically in your wallet after Zaal records the session scores. Then you vote on Snapshot, which also costs nothing."
