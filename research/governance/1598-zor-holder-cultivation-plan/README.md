## Why ZOR Holder Growth Matters

1. **MAIN battle integrity:** More ZOR holders = more diverse governance vote. Single-holder dominance weakens credibility.
2. **OP RF evidence:** Doc 1525 (OP RF evidence package) — ZOR holder growth demonstrates ecosystem adoption.
3. **ZAOstock:** ZOR holders vote in the live ZAOstock MAIN battle from the audience. More holders = more on-chain proof from the event.
4. **Citable:** "ZAO has [N] ZOR token holders participating in weekly on-chain governance" is a grant claim that needs to be true and growing.
5. **ZABAL S2 pipeline:** ZABAL S2 graduates → ZOR holders → governance participants → future COC selectors.

## ZOR Token Mechanics (How to Acquire)

ZOR is an ERC-1155 token on Optimism Mainnet. Acquisition paths:

| Path | How | Who |
|---|---|---|
| WaveWarZ MAIN battle participation | ZOR distributed to participants after MAIN battles | All WW MAIN artists + active governance contributors |
| Zaal direct allocation | Zaal holds ZOR treasury; can allocate to contributors | ZOR holders Zaal recognizes for contribution |
| ZABAL S2 graduation | Top graduates receive ZOR token (Tier 3 micro-grant option) | ZABAL S2 Excellence Award recipients |
| Fractal Democracy Respect → ZOR | High Respect scorers can receive ZOR through OREC-linked allocation | Consistent Thursday session attendees |

**Current gap:** There is no public-facing ZOR acquisition guide. New artists and ZABAL S2 participants don't know how to get ZOR or why they want it. This plan fixes that.

## Cultivation Strategy: 3 Pipelines

### Pipeline 1: COC Concertz Attendees → ZOR

COC shows (monthly) are the primary new ZOR candidate pipeline. Every COC attendee has seen WaveWarZ governance in action.

**Actions:**
1. **Post-show DM (doc 1559 Phase 5):** Winner + loser DMs from COC shows reference "join ZOR governance"
2. **ZOE COC post-show ZOR invite:** After each COC show, ZOE sends 1 Telegram post: "If you were at COC tonight and want to vote in the next MAIN battle — message Zaal or join the ZAO Telegram"
3. **COC #8 live:** Zaal mentions ZOR from stage at COC #8: "ZOR holders voted for tonight's artists. If you want that vote for next month's show — ask me after."

**Target from COC pipeline (Jul–Sep):** +5–10 new ZOR holders (2–3 per COC show)

---

### Pipeline 2: ZABAL S2 Participants → ZOR

ZABAL S2 runs Sep 1 – Nov 21 with 15–25 participants. ZABAL S2 participants are already committed ZAO contributors.

**Actions:**
1. **Week 4 session (Sep 22 — doc 1588):** Fractal Democracy session introduces ZOR voting. Participants who show up get visibility into how ZOR works.
2. **Week 4 post-session:** ZOE sends ZABAL S2 Telegram: "If you want to participate in future WaveWarZ MAIN votes, you need ZOR. After today's session, DM Zaal about ZOR eligibility."
3. **Tier 3 micro-grant (Nov 21):** Excellence Award includes ZOR token as part of the award (doc 1555).
4. **ZABAL S2 graduates list → ZOR allocation:** At graduation, Zaal reviews completers who participated in governance sessions and allocates ZOR to eligible graduates.

**Target from ZABAL pipeline (Sep–Nov):** +10–15 new ZOR holders (from 15–25 cohort)

---

### Pipeline 3: ZAOstock Attendees → ZOR

ZAOstock (Oct 3) is ZAO's highest-profile IRL event. ZOR holders vote in the MAIN battle during the event.

**Actions:**
1. **Pre-event welcome (doc 1585, Sep 30 sub-sequence):** ZOR holder sub-sequence sent to existing holders — "You can vote in person at ZAOstock"
2. **ZAOstock onsite:** After MAIN battle, Zaal mentions from stage: "The people who voted in that battle hold ZOR tokens. If you want that vote at next year's ZAOstock — come talk to me."
3. **Post-event DM (Oct 4):** ZOE sends post-event follow-up to all RSVPs with: "Want to vote in the next WaveWarZ MAIN battle? Ask Zaal about ZOR governance."

**Target from ZAOstock pipeline (Oct 3):** +5–10 new ZOR holder signups (convert 5–10% of attendees)
