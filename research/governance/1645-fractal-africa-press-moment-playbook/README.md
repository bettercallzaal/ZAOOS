# 1645 — Fractal × Africa Battle Week Press Moment Playbook (Jul 19 – Aug 3)

> **Who this is for:** Zaal. The Africa Battle Week (Jul 24-25 vote) + Mirror Article 3 publish (Jul 24, doc 1623) create a 2-week press window. This playbook tells you who to pitch, what angle, and in what order.

---

## The Story

**One-sentence pitch for journalists:**
> "ZAO Fractal has run weekly music governance sessions without missing a single week for 100+ consecutive weeks — and this week, they're voting to give the proceeds of Africa Battle Week to an African charity, with West African artists joining the governance cohort."

**What makes this press-worthy right now:**
1. The 100+ week streak is citable fact (on-chain, public)
2. Africa expansion is a "first" — first music DAO to expand governance internationally
3. Africa Battle Week charity vote is a concrete governance ACTION, not just a whitepaper
4. Mirror Article 3 (doc 1623) gives journalists a complete story to link to
