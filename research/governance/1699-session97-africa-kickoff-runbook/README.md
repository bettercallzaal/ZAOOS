# 1699 — Session 97 Kickoff Runbook: The First WaveWarZ Africa Session

> **Who this is for:** Zaal (facilitator). This is the operational script for Session 97 — the first ZAO Fractal session of Season 9 (WaveWarZ Africa). Date: TBD August 2026 (first Thursday in August after Africa Battle Week). Session 97 is the transition session where Africa artists are formally welcomed into the governance community.

---

**Telegram announcement (ZAO main group):**
Use the Season 9 announcement copy from doc 1635 (the Telegram block). Post 24 hours before and 1 hour before.

**ZOE reminder:**
If ZOE is configured with a reminder trigger, set it for 1 hour before. ZOE should post: "[Session 97 in 1 hour — this is the first WaveWarZ Africa Fractal session. Link: ZOOM_LINK]"

## 30 Minutes Before Session 97

**Tech checklist:**
- [ ] Zoom link active (test join from a second device)
- [ ] Breakout rooms pre-configured for groups of 6 (if 12+ attendees expected, set up 2 rooms; if 6-11, 1-2 rooms)
- [ ] Screen share ready: have the session agenda slide/doc open
- [ ] ZOR contribution log template (doc 1561) open in a separate tab
- [ ] OREC ready: know which wallet you'll use to submit the session's rankings on-chain after the session

**Attendee list:**
Have the confirmed attendees visible. Mark who is ZAO-US and who is Africa cohort. Balance the breakout groups so each group has at least 1 Africa artist.

## Session 97 Opening Script (Verbatim)

*Read this at the start, or use as a guide for your own words:*

---

"Welcome to Session 97 of ZAO Fractal. For those of you who are new — welcome. This is the first session of Season 9, the WaveWarZ Africa season.

For the veterans in the room: you've been doing this for almost two years. 96 sessions without missing one. That streak continues today, and today it means something new — because today, for the first time, we have artists from West Africa joining us as governance participants.

[Acknowledge Africa artists by name or by saying: 'If you're joining us from West Africa for the first time, welcome.']

Let me explain what's happening in the next hour for the people who are new:

We'll break into groups of 6. In your group, each person takes about 90 seconds to share what they contributed to ZAO or WaveWarZ this past week. It can be anything — a battle, a social post, showing up to the Africa sprint call, even just staying informed. Then your group will rank each other in a poll: who contributed most? The rankings aggregate into your Respect score, which becomes ZOR — your on-chain governance token.

For Session 97, if this is your first session, you're NOT expected to rank. You're invited to introduce yourself, listen, and see how it works. You can also contribute a ranking if you feel comfortable, but there's no pressure.

The goal for today is simple: everyone in the room knows who the Africa cohort is, and everyone in the Africa cohort knows what ZAO Fractal is.

Any questions before we start? [Pause]

Alright. Let's open the breakout rooms."

## During Session 97: Facilitator Notes

**Session format (60 minutes total):**

| Time | What |
|------|------|
| 0:00–0:10 | Opening (use script above) |
| 0:10–0:12 | Assign breakout groups (aim for mixed ZAO-US/Africa in each group) |
| 0:12–0:35 | Breakout groups run (~23 min: each person 90s contribution share + ranking poll) |
| 0:35–0:40 | Return to main room |
| 0:40–0:50 | Whole-group share: 2-3 people share what stood out from their group |
| 0:50–0:58 | Season 9 context: what happens in Sessions 97-108, what ZOR will unlock |
| 0:58–1:00 | Close |

**Breakout room assignment for Session 97:**
If you have more Africa artists than can fit in one group, split them so each group has exactly 1-2 Africa artists. This prevents Africa artists from being siloed and ensures cross-pollination with the ZAO-US veterans.

If you have fewer than 6 total attendees: run a single group without breakout rooms.

**During breakouts:**
Pop in and out of breakout rooms to check in. For Africa artists, check that they understand the contribution share format. If someone is confused, coach them: "What did you do this week that was related to ZAO, WaveWarZ, or music governance? Even one small thing."

**Contribution log:**
While participants are in breakouts, note who is present and their main contribution for the week in the log template (doc 1561).

## Things That Might Go Wrong

| Problem | Response |
|---------|----------|
| Africa artists have audio/video issues | Have them type in chat instead of unmuting. Their contribution is still valid. |
| Africa time zone confusion | Sessions are Thursdays at [TIME] ET. West Africa = [TIME] WAT. Confirm the time in the DM. |
| Less than 4 total attendees | Run session without breakout rooms. Single group, same format. |
| An Africa artist shows up without a wallet set up | Walk them through wallet setup live or schedule a 15-min call within 48 hours. Their ZOR for this session can be credited retroactively. |
| A ZAO-US veteran is dismissive or condescending | Address it directly after the session. The culture of belonging-first is non-negotiable. |
| OREC submission fails | Note the rankings in a backup doc (plaintext list: name → rank). Resubmit within 24 hours. |
