## What It Is

Bread Cooperative (brand: **bread.coop**, legacy name **Breadchain Cooperative**) is a **worker-owned, not-for-profit collective** building "post-capitalist" financial tools on crypto rails. Emerged in **2022** out of the "Crypto Leftists" community (Discord/Reddit). Explicitly anti-VC, anti-"number go up," public-goods-first. Co-founded by **Joshua Dávila** (host of *The Blockchain Socialist* podcast, author of *Blockchain Radicals*).

It calls its building blocks **"solidarity primitives"** — the solidarity analog to DeFi's financial primitives: composable open-source smart contracts that encode cooperative relationships (mutual aid, shared savings, participatory budgeting) instead of speculation.

## How the Core Mechanism Works (Solidarity Fund / Crowdstaking)

This is the flagship primitive and the part worth understanding in detail:

1. A user deposits **xDAI** (Gnosis Chain's USD stablecoin) into the Crowdstaking smart contract — this is "baking $BREAD."
2. The contract converts the xDAI into **sDAI** (yield-bearing Savings DAI).
3. The user receives **$BREAD**, minted **1:1** with the deposited xDAI. $BREAD is pegged 1:1 to USD, transferable, and usable as a community currency (peer-to-peer payments via the Citizen Wallet app, a marketplace, etc.).
4. **The depositor keeps their principal claim** — they can burn $BREAD anytime to reclaim the underlying xDAI. They never "lose" their money.
5. **Only the yield** generated by the sDAI is distributed to the cooperative's member projects. The principal is never spent.
6. **$BREAD holders vote every 30 days** on how that yield is split. Framed as a "post-capitalist credit union" running participatory budgeting.

### Yield Distribution Formula (the reusable part)

Disbursement is **50/50**:

- **50% pre-determined** — split evenly across all current member projects. This is the deliberately capital-resistant floor: it stops token wealth from deciding everything.
- **50% token voting** — $BREAD holders each distribute 100 percentage points across the projects. Weighted by **voting power = average $BREAD held over the previous 30-day cycle** (cannot be gamed by last-minute buys). Liquidity providers preserve their voting power via **LP Voting Vaults**.

Worked example from their docs — 200 BREAD monthly yield: 100 split evenly, 100 by vote (e.g. Symbiota 40%, Crypto Commons 25%, Bread Core 20%, Regen Coordination 15%).

**Governance nuance:** the *organization itself* is a one-member-one-vote worker cooperative with **no governance token**. $BREAD only governs *yield allocation*, not the coop's internal decisions (those run over Google Docs / Meet / Telegram / Discord / Signal). This is a real distinction from a normal DAO and part of why the Platform Cooperativism Consortium wrote that Bread "feels real."

## Findings — Why This Matters to ZAO

Bread and ZAO are solving adjacent problems from adjacent politics. The overlap is real but the stacks are separate:

- **Deposit-and-keep funding** is the headline transferable idea. ZAO's contribution circles and any future member-project fund could use "principal stays with the depositor, only yield funds the collective" instead of asking people to donate capital they lose. This is more sustainable than one-time grants.
- **Whale-resistance by construction.** The 50% flat floor + time-weighted voting power is a published, working answer to the exact concentration problem ZAO tracks on Respect (156 holders, Gini 0.73). See [[reference_zao_respect_onchain_facts]].
- **Same thesis family as DreamNet + Fractal.** "Values are designed into infrastructure," solidarity over speculation, cooperative governance — this is Brandon's DreamNet trust-layer thesis and the Fractal whitepaper's political core, expressed in a shipped product. See [[project_dreamnet_trust_layer]], [[project_zao_fractal_whitepaper]].
- **Culture-coin design input.** Sparkz / culture-coins ([[project_culture_coins_meme_engine]], [[project_sparkz_configurable_ai_advisor]]) can borrow the "external project inclusion via staking" pattern Bread is considering (stake 10k BREAD to be added to the vote for one month) as a spam-resistant way to let outside creators into a funding vote.
- **What ZAO would NOT copy:** Gnosis/xDAI dependency, the no-governance-token purity (ZAO's whole model is tokenized reputation via Respect/ZOLs), and the deliberately non-speculative token (ZAO culture-coins want price discovery as a signal).

## Contradictions / Caveats

- The "100,000+ participants" figure is marketing aggregate reach, not on-chain holder count; on-chain supply (~16k BREAD) implies a far smaller active-depositor base. Do not cite the 100k as membership.
- The bread.coop marketing site is lighter on mechanics than the docs; several mechanic details (redemption, voting formula) only appear in docs.bread.coop, not the landing page. All mechanics above are sourced from the docs, not the marketing copy.
