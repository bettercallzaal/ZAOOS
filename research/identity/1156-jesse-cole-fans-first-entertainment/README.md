# 1156 - Jesse Cole & Savannah Bananas: The Fans First Operating System

> **Goal:** Extract Jesse Cole's "Fans First Entertainment" operating ideology from the Savannah Bananas story — the yellow tux, Banana Ball invention, $500M+ valuation built without traditional sports revenue. Who he is, real quotes, the Five E's playbook, and the ZAO map. Part of the [personal development roster](../1168-operating-ideology-synthesis/).

## 1. Grounded Facts

**Who Jesse Cole Is:**
- Born March 13, 1984, Scituate, Massachusetts. Attended Wofford College (graduated 2006).
- Originally pursued baseball; a shoulder injury redirected him to theatre and entertainment.
- Founder of **Fans First Entertainment** (2015).
- International keynote speaker; author of three books: *Find Your Yellow Tux* (2017), *Fans First* (2022), *Banana Ball* (2023).
- Married to Emily McDonald Cole (co-founder of the Bananas); one biological son, two adopted daughters.
- Founded non-profit Banana Foster supporting foster care community.

**How the Savannah Bananas Started:**
- Launched Savannah Bananas in 2016 alongside Emily.
- Cole's prior: at age 23, became GM of a failing college summer baseball team in Gastonia, NC — 200 fans, $268 in the bank, losing $150,000/year.
- Turned it around by treating fans as the product. Eventually sold out all seven venues, opening the path to found the Bananas.
- Started as a Coastal Plain League team in Savannah, Georgia. Has since dropped all traditional league affiliation to focus entirely on Banana Ball tours.

**The Yellow Tuxedo:**
Cole's personal signature — wears it to every game and public appearance.

> *"It's something I believe in. It's standing out. It's being different. And if your owner is dressed up in a yellow tuxedo, I mean, I think that gives permission to everyone else to not take themselves too seriously, to have fun."*
> — Jesse Cole, 60 Minutes interview (CBS News)

**Current Scale (2026):**
- Sold out EVERY game since the 2016 inaugural season.
- **4 million people** on the tour waiting list (Forbes, 2026).
- **~$500M valuation** (Forbes, September 2025).
- **Revenue:** >$45M in 2024; >$100M projected 2026. ~$80M from tickets, $50M+ from merch (1.96M items sold in 2025, 80% in-person). **Zero** traditional sponsorship revenue (<1% of total).
- **190 games** in 75 stadiums, 45 states scheduled for 2026; expects 3M+ tickets sold.
- **35M+ social media followers** (8M+ TikTok — more than every MLB franchise).
- All-inclusive ticket pricing (food, drinks, service fees included; starts at $35).

## 2. Banana Ball: The Game

**What It Is:**
Banana Ball is Cole's invented hybrid sport — "the fastest and most entertaining game of baseball." It keeps baseball's core elements but inverts everything that makes it slow or boring.

**Origin:**
> *"I'm sitting in the dugout and I realized something. I'm bored out of my mind. And if I'm bored, there's gotta be other people that are probably bored with baseball as well."*

Cole's method: *"What are all the normal rules of a baseball game? What would be the exact opposite?"*

First tested: November 2, 2018 at Lander University.
First official game: June 26, 2020 at Grayson Stadium.

**Core Rules (inverted baseball):**
- 2-hour time limit (vs. unlimited innings in MLB)
- NO walks (batter must swing or run to first — "Think about this: a walk is called a walk. It's unathletic. So we said, what's the exact opposite? A sprint.")
- NO bunting. NO stepping out of the box. NO mound visits.
- Fans can catch a foul ball = automatic out (fans become defensive players)
- "Golden Batter" rule: your best hitter faces their best pitcher every matchup
- Scoring: most runs per inning wins that inning EXCEPT final inning (every run counts)
- Trick plays, backflip catches, between-the-legs throws are encouraged

**Why purists hate it / why fans love it:**
> *"We are not for most baseball purists. We are for people who want to have fun."*

> *"I believe if you're not getting criticized, you're playin' it too safe."*

Cole keeps every hate message he receives as fuel. His response to critics is to keep building fan value, not defend the sport's tradition.

## 4. The Fans First Operating System: The Five E's

Cole's systematic blueprint for turning customers into raving fans. Every principle is actionable and interconnected.

### E1 — ELIMINATE FRICTION
Remove every obstacle between fans and enjoyment.

- All-inclusive $35 tickets (food, drinks, taxes, service fees — no surprises)
- Simplified parking and entry
- Removed the macro friction: baseball is boring → Banana Ball
- Cole: *"The greatest businesses look inward, putting themselves in their customers' shoes and eliminating the friction points."*

### E2 — ENTERTAIN ALWAYS
Entertainment is baked into EVERY customer touchpoint, not just the game.

- Dancing first-base coaches
- Singing players
- Banana Nanas (senior citizen dance team)
- Mananas (male cheerleading team)
- In-game circus acts, acrobatics
- Pre-game player parade
- Promotions every half-inning
- Even rain delays feature entertainment

> *"Entertainment shouldn't be limited to the product — it should be baked into the entire experience."*

### E3 — EXPERIMENT CONSTANTLY
Rapid testing; fail fast, scale wins; never stop inventing.

- Cole generates **10 new ideas every day** (written practice)
- Tests wild, outrageous ideas (teaching players to dance was an "experiment" that went viral)
- Analyzes what works, doubles down on winners
- *"One experiment can change everything."*

### E4 — ENGAGE DEEPLY
Personal connection and individual memorable moments build true loyalty.

- *"Do for one what you wish you could do for many."*
- Staff empowered to create personalized surprises unscripted
- Thank-you notes to individual fans
- Celebrate proposals, birthdays, fan milestones
- *"True loyalty is built through deep, personal engagement with individual fans."*

### E5 — EMPOWER ACTION
Grant team members authority to execute exceptional experiences without bureaucracy.

- Frontline staff write thank-you notes and create special moments WITHOUT approval
- Culture is a movement, not a memo
- Leadership enables frontline creativity
- *"Nothing matters more than making people feel like they matter."*

**Meta-question driving all Five E's:**
> *"What if?"* — Cole calls this "the most powerful question" and the core of a "plussing mindset" (Disney's term for continuous enhancement). "What if?" turns the ordinary into something completely new.

## 5. Marketing & Social: No Ads, Just Experience

**The Strategy: Experience IS marketing**

> *"When you prioritize the fan experience, the marketing takes care of itself because every attendee becomes an influencer for your brand."*

**How they built 35M followers with zero paid advertising:**
- 2023: a single intern posted raw game footage to TikTok without corporate approval → viral → proved authentic > polished
- Social channels are **not promotional** — they're a standalone entertainment show
- Content designed for native platforms (vertical video, trick play moments, fan reactions)
- Zero in-stadium advertising (no brand logos, no sponsored content in games)
- 2.5x engagement rate vs. average MLB team despite a fraction of resources

**The key insight:** Fans feel they "discovered" the Bananas themselves. They share because the content genuinely entertains, not because they were targeted. The distribution strategy is trust, not reach.

## 6. The ZAO Map

How Cole's Fans First system translates to The ZAO / ZABAL / Sparkz:

| Cole Principle | ZAO Application |
|----------------|-----------------|
| **"If it's normal, do the exact opposite"** | ZAO is the anti-record-label. No gatekeeping, no extractive royalties, no 360 deals. The opposite of how music industry works. |
| **Eliminate Friction** | Sparkz: AI validates readiness so artists don't have to guess. Zero hidden fees. One-click token launch. The boring parts of music distribution are automated. |
| **Entertain Always** | ZAOstock is entertainment-first (music festival, not just a conference). ZABAL Games is entertainment with economic stakes. The Fractal starts with music and play, not admin. |
| **Experiment Constantly** | The Fractal is an experiment lab: each session tests a governance proposal in real-time. ZOE (the AI bot) experiments with new capabilities each cycle. |
| **Engage Deeply** | "Do for one what you wish you could do for many" — ZOE/ZAO member onboarding is personal. New members get a personal DM, a context brief, a path. Not a form email. |
| **Empower Action** | ZAO members are empowered to propose, vote, build. The Fractal gives EVERYONE equal speaking time (4-minute timer). No permission-by-hierarchy. |
| **"Nothing matters more than making people feel like they matter"** | ZAO's 188-member community is small enough that every member IS someone. The Respect token makes this visible. |
| **Experience = marketing** | Build-in-public: every ship (PR, tool, Sparkz launch) is a content event. The community IS the marketing machine. |

**The core lesson:**
Cole inverted who the Bananas serve — fans over baseball tradition. The ZAO inverts who music serves — artists and community over labels and aggregators. Both built around obsessive fan (member) experience. The playbook is identical at the values level; the execution differs by medium.
