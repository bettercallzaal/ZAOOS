What is The ZAO?

Verified 2026-07-17 · data sourced from on-chain records + ZAO OS research corpus

The ZAO (ZTalent Artist Organization) is a decentralized impact network for independent music artists. It was founded by Zaal Panthaki (@bettercallzaal) and has run over 100 consecutive weekly Fractal governance votes since July 2024, with Respect settled on Optimism mainnet.

The ZAO measures member contribution with Respect — a non-transferable weight earned by peer ranking, not by holding tokens. Members earn Respect by contributing, showing up, and building.

The flagship application is WaveWarZ — live-traded song-vs-song battles on Solana where artists are paid 1% of every trade instantly onchain. As of July 2026: 1,245+ battles, 524+ SOL volume, $1,497 raised for charity.

Mission: profit, data, and IP ownership back to independent artists. Not a label. Not a DAO treasury with a spending vote. A contribution-tracked impact network where earning follows doing.

How to join

  • Battle on WaveWarZ — wavewarz.info. No wallet required to watch; SOL wallet to trade. Artists earn 1% of every trade instantly onchain.
  • Attend a weekly Fractal call — Every week on Farcaster Spaces since July 2024. Members rank each other's contributions to earn non-transferable Respect.
  • Apply for ZABAL Gamez — The ZAO's 3-month builder cohort. Ship for the community, keep earning from what you build. Open enrollment 2× per year.

Frequently asked questions

What makes The ZAO different from a regular DAO?
Most DAOs distribute governance by token balance (more tokens = more votes). The ZAO uses Respect — a non-transferable weight earned by peer ranking. You can't buy your way in; you earn your Respect by contributing. This means governance follows contribution, not capital.
What is the ZAO Fractal?
A weekly peer-ranking session where members break into small groups and rank each other's contributions. The rankings produce non-transferable Respect scores that accumulate onchain. 100+ consecutive weekly sessions since July 2024. Respect settled on Optimism mainnet via two contracts: OG ERC-20 (0x34cE89…C216957) and ZOR ERC-1155 (0x9885CC…7445c).
How does WaveWarZ work?
Two songs battle head-to-head. Traders buy shares in the song they think will win. After 24 hours, the song with more buy volume wins; winners' shares are redeemable, losing shares burn. Artists receive 1% of every trade instantly onchain — they earn whether their song wins or loses, just by being in the arena. Platform program on Solana: 9TUfEHvk5fN5vogtQyrefgNqzKy2Bqb4nWVhSFUg2fYo.
What is ZABAL Gamez?
The ZAO's 3-month builder cohort. Builders ship products or features for The ZAO community — and keep earning from what they build even after the cohort ends. Open enrollment twice per year. Past cohort projects include wwtracker (the open-source WaveWarZ analytics dashboard) and multiple ZAO infrastructure pieces.
Who founded The ZAO?
The ZAO was founded by Zaal Panthaki (@bettercallzaal). WaveWarZ was co-founded within The ZAO by Hurricane (platform operator), Candy, and Ohnahji. Full contributor roster tracked via the ZAO's onchain Respect system — every contribution is on-chain and public.
How does The ZAO generate revenue?
WaveWarZ charges a 3.16% take rate on buy volume, generating 17.44 SOL in platform revenue to date. Artists receive a separate 1.79% direct payout (9.07 SOL total). Revenue above a 3.5 SOL operating floor distributes: 33% operations, 22% each to co-founders Hurricane, Candy, and Zaal.
