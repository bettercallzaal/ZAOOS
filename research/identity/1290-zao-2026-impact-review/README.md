## Chapter 1: WaveWarZ — A Music Prediction Market Built in Public

In August 2025, The ZAO launched WaveWarZ: a music prediction market where fans bet on song battles using real money (SOL on Solana), winners earn, losers earn, and artists get paid automatically on every trade.

**By July 2026:**
- **1,245 battles** have been fought
- **921 unique songs** have been battled — every one permanently recorded on Solana
- **524 SOL (~$39,000)** has traded hands between fans betting on music
- **$9.07 SOL (~$680)** has been paid directly to artists — automatically, instantly, no middleman
- **$1,497** has been raised for charity through benefit battle rounds for HuRya

The artist payout rate is approximately **1.73% of each trade** — compared to Spotify's ~$0.004 per stream. For a song that trades $1,000 in a single battle, the artist earns $17.30 automatically. That same song on Spotify would need 4,325 streams to match it.

WaveWarZ is live at wavewarz.info. The dashboard — built and maintained by The ZAO as open-source software (MIT-licensed) — is free to use, requires no account, and provides a real-time public API. Any fan, journalist, or developer can access every stat at wavewarz.info/api/public/stats.

## Chapter 2: ZAO Fractal — 100+ Consecutive Weeks of Governance

Every week, without fail, The ZAO runs a Fractal governance session. Members gather, split into small breakout groups, peer-rank each other's contributions, and earn Respect tokens based on their scores. The session results settle onchain on Optimism — permanently, publicly, tamper-proof.

**By July 2026:**
- **100+ consecutive Fractal sessions** have been run (no missed weeks since launch)
- **63 of those weeks** have been settled on the Optimism blockchain
- **505 on-chain transactions** record the governance history
- **157 unique members** hold ZAO Respect tokens — the credentialed core of the community

The ZAO's governance architecture uses OREC (Optimistic Respect Execution Contract) — an open-source contract that allows proposals to execute automatically after a 72-hour window unless vetoed. The ZAO is **the only active organization deploying OREC on Optimism** as of July 2026. Eden Fractal, the organization that pioneered this approach, has been inactive since 2025.

This makes The ZAO the living proof of concept for Fractal Democracy in music — the only community that has continuously run this form of governance and built real products with it.

## Chapter 3: ZABAL Games — Building in Public

**June 2026 (completed phase):**

The ZABAL Games builder cohort is what makes The ZAO different from a typical DAO: it's not just a governance community, it's a production floor. The products the community builds — including WaveWarZ analytics tools, Farcaster mini-apps, and governance infrastructure — are shipped publicly and used by real people.

August 2026 will bring the ZABAL Games finals, where builders present their projects and the community votes on winners using Respect-weighted ballots.

## Chapter 4: COC Concertz — Monthly Live Music in the Metaverse

Since March 2025, The ZAO has been running COC Concertz — a monthly virtual concert series on Spatial.io (a browser-native metaverse venue), live-streamed to Twitch.

**Show #7 (July 18, 2026):**
- First show with wallet gate turned **OFF** (open access pilot)
- **Live WaveWarZ battles** running during the show — audience bets on song matchups in real-time
- Real SOL settlement while the music plays
- Artists earn automatically as the audience votes

COC Concertz has established The ZAO as the operator of the first live onchain music battle circuit — where virtual concerts generate real prediction-market volume, and artists leave the show with actual earnings in their wallets.

Every COC Concertz archive is preserved on Arweave (permanent decentralized storage) with Universal Data License (UDL) — meaning the content is permanently accessible and the rights are defined onchain.
