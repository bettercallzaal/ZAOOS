# 1297 — The ZAO Manifesto (July 2026)

> **The founding vision, operating principles, and long-term mission of The ZAO.** This is the document that answers "what is The ZAO trying to build, and why?"

---

## The Problem

Music is the most consumed art form on the planet. And the people who make it are paid last, paid least, and paid late.

A song on Spotify earns $0.004 per stream. The artist sees a fraction of that. The label takes 50-85%. The distributor takes a cut. By the time the money moves — months later — the artist has earned less from a million streams than a busker earns from an hour in a subway station.

This is not a streaming problem. It is a structural problem. The music industry was built for physical distribution in an era when you needed someone with a truck to get your music to a store. That infrastructure is gone. The contracts built around it are not.

At the same time, something new has emerged: cryptographic protocols that let value move between people instantly, transparently, and without permission from anyone in the middle.

The question is whether anyone will use those protocols to build something for artists.

---

## The Answer

The ZAO was founded to be that something.

We are a decentralized artist collective — 157 members, 32 active builders, a weekly governance session that has run for over 100 consecutive weeks without a missed meeting. We are not a company. We do not have a CEO. We do not have a board. Every decision gets made the same way every week: our community meets, splits into small groups, peers rank each other, and the results settle on the Optimism blockchain.

This is not how most music organizations work. It is how we work.

---

## What We're Building

### WaveWarZ — Music as a Prediction Market

WaveWarZ is our flagship product. It is the first prediction market built specifically for music.

Two songs go head to head. Fans bet on which song they think will win — using real money (SOL on Solana). Both sides earn. Artists receive automatic onchain royalties on every single trade. No label. No intermediary. No delay.

As of July 2026: 1,245 battles. 921 unique songs. 524 SOL in trading volume. $1,497 raised for charity. Artists have earned 9.07 SOL in automatic payouts — that's approximately 1.73% of every trade, versus Spotify's $0.004 per stream.

We built WaveWarZ because we wanted to prove one thing: **fans can support artists directly, and the architecture of how that happens can be transparent, automatic, and fair.**

### COC Concertz — Music Events for a New Era

We run a monthly virtual concert series on Spatial.io, live-streamed to Twitch. Show #7 runs tonight (July 18, 2026). Every show has a live WaveWarZ battle running during the music — real fans betting real SOL while the artists perform. Every archive is preserved on Arweave with Universal Data License, meaning the art and the rights are defined onchain.

### ZABAL Games — Building in Public

We run a music-tech build-a-thon. In June 2026: 32 builders, 28 workshops, 30+ pull requests merged to our public GitHub. These builders are creating the next generation of music-technology tools — analytics platforms, governance interfaces, AI agents, artist tools.

They build in public. Everything is open source. Everything is verifiable.

### ZAOstock — Music Where You Are

This summer, we're putting on ZAOstock — an outdoor music festival in Ellsworth, Maine. WaveWarZ artists will perform live for the first time. The same community that runs a DAO on the Optimism blockchain will stand in a field in coastal Maine and listen to music together.

We believe in building institutions that live both onchain and in the world.

## The Principles

**1. Transparent governance is the foundation.**
The ZAO does not run on vibes and trust. It runs on weekly peer ranking, Respect tokens, and Optimism blockchain settlement. If you want to know who made a decision, you can look it up. If you want to verify a claim, the data is onchain.

**2. Artists are paid first, not last.**
WaveWarZ pays artists automatically on every trade. Not monthly. Not after a minimum threshold. On every trade, automatically, in seconds. This is the principle: if a fan engages with your music, you earn. No intermediary decides when or if that happens.

**3. The community owns what it builds.**
The ZAO does not have investors. It has members. The decisions about what to build next, which direction to take, what to prioritize — these are made by the people who show up every week and do the work. Respect tokens measure contribution. Governance weight follows contribution. There is no shortcut.

**4. Open source is the default.**
wwtracker — the analytics platform for WaveWarZ — is MIT-licensed and has a free public API. ZAOOS — our research library — is publicly available on GitHub. We build in the open so that anyone can verify what we've done, fork what we've built, and build something better.

**5. Show up every week.**
The ZAO has run 100+ consecutive Fractal governance sessions. No missed weeks. This is not an accident. It is a practice. Communities that show up consistently outlast communities that only show up when it's exciting.

**6. The chain is the receipt.**
We do not ask you to trust us. We ask you to check the Optimism blockchain. 63 governance sessions settled onchain. 505 transactions. 157 unique Respect holders. The receipts are public. The math is verifiable. The history is permanent.

## What We Are Not

We are not a label. We are not trying to own artists' music.

We are not a streaming service. We are not trying to replace Spotify.

We are not a record company. We are not selling access to distribution networks that we control.

We are a community that has decided to use the tools available to us — cryptography, smart contracts, blockchain settlement, open-source software — to build economic infrastructure that serves artists instead of intermediaries.

---

## The Case We're Making

There is a version of the music industry where:
- Fans have a direct stake in the music they love
- Artists earn automatically when fans engage
- Governance decisions are made by the community doing the work
- The history of who decided what is permanently on the blockchain
- The code that makes it work is open for anyone to study, fork, and improve

This is not a hypothetical. It exists. It is WaveWarZ. It is The ZAO. It is live right now, on Solana and Optimism, with real money and real artists.

We are not the only people who could build this. We are the people who did.
