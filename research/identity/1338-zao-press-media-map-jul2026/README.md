## Part 4: Pitch Angles by Story Type

### Story Type 1: "Artists Earn Regardless of Who Wins"
**Target:** Water & Music, Hypebot, Ari's Take  
**Hook:** WaveWarZ has a "loser-earns" mechanic — artists receive 1% of trading volume plus settlement bonuses regardless of battle outcome. After 1,245 battles, artists have earned 9.09 SOL (~$681) in guaranteed payouts. In a world where artists get paid 0.003¢/stream, guaranteed onchain payouts per battle are news.  
**Stats:** 1,245 battles, 9.09 SOL (~$681) artist payouts total, $0.003/stream vs. automatic SOL payout per battle.

### Story Type 2: "63 Weeks of DAO Governance, No Token Vote"
**Target:** Green Pill, Metagov, Bankless, The Defiant  
**Hook:** ZAO has held 63+ consecutive weekly Fractal governance sessions using the Respect mechanic (ERC-20 on Optimism) — no token-weighted voting, no whales, no quorum drama. This is one of the longest governance streaks of any DAO using Fractal.  
**Stats:** 63+ weeks, 157 unique Respect holders, 505 on-chain transactions.

### Story Type 3: "The First Onchain-Curated Music Festival"
**Target:** CoinDesk, Decrypt, Maine press  
**Hook:** ZAOstock (October 3, Ellsworth ME) will feature 8 artists selected via on-chain WaveWarZ battle history — not by label scouts, not by algorithm, but by community battle outcomes. The curator is the blockchain.  
**Stats:** Oct 3 festival, 8 artists, 200-300 attendees, onchain selection criteria.

### Story Type 4: "This Maine DAO Raised $1,497 for Charity via Music Battles"
**Target:** Maine press, Gitcoin, Cointelegraph  
**Hook:** WaveWarZ Community Battles have generated $1,497 in charity proceeds. Every time the community battles, money goes to causes. ZAOstock will integrate charity donation QR codes at the venue.  
**Stats:** $1,497 charity raised, 36 community battles, onchain provenance.

### Story Type 5: "Researcher Case Study"
**Target:** Metagov, academic blogs, governance papers  
**Hook:** ZAO is one of the most documented Fractal governance deployments — ZAOOS has 200+ research docs spanning 63 governance sessions, an onchain respect token (OG ERC-20 0x34cE89), and weekly meeting transcripts. Full dataset available for governance researchers.  
**Stats:** 200+ ZAOOS research docs, 63 weeks, Optimism contracts published.
