**Why X matters:** X is where most music + Web3 discovery happens for non-Farcaster users. The press contacts in doc 1340 (Water & Music, Hypebot, Ari's Take) are all X-native.

### Growth tactics

**1. Battle announcement threads (highest engagement format)**

Template (already in doc 1332 as TMP-S03):
```
🥊 MAIN EVENT TONIGHT
[Artist 1] vs [Artist 2]
[Time] · wavewarz.info
#WaveWarZ
```

Add engagement hook: "RT if you're betting on [Artist 1]" → algorithmic boost from replies/RTs.

**2. Quote-tweet battle results into narrative**

After each MAIN event:
```
.@wavewarz just ran MAIN Event #[N].
Result: [Artist] won.

Stats from tonight:
• [N] trades
• [X] SOL volume  
• [Y] SOL to losing artist (loser-earns)

The music battle platform where losing still pays. wavewarz.info
```

This format consistently outperforms single-post announcements because it tells a story people want to share.

**3. ZAOstock countdown (Aug 15–Oct 3)**
```

8 artists. Selected by onchain battle history.
No label. No politics. Just who won.

Tickets → [link]
```

Post every 7 days from Aug 15. The countdown narrative is shareable.

**4. WaveWarZ stat milestone posts**

When API hits round numbers (1,300 battles, 550 SOL, etc.):
```

[X] SOL in volume.
[Y] SOL to artists — paid night-of.

wavewarz.info
```

ZOE should monitor the API and auto-draft these when milestones hit.

**5. Tag artists in their spotlight posts**

When ZAO posts the ZAOstock artist spotlight, tag the artist. The artist will often like/RT, exposing @wavewarz to their followers. If artist has 1,000 followers and 5% follow @wavewarz = 50 new followers per artist spotlight.

## Part 4: Farcaster /zao + @bettercallzaal Growth

### Why Farcaster matters differently

Farcaster is where ZAO's highest-trust audience lives. Follows on Farcaster are more deliberate than X follows. The /zao channel (93 followers) is the ZAO community hub — growing it matters for governance participation and grant credibility.

### Farcaster-specific tactics

**1. Post ZAO governance session recaps as Farcaster-first content**

Each week after the Fractal session, post the governance summary in /zao first (before X or newsletter). Farcaster users value being first. Add: "Full recap in this week's ZAO newsletter → [link]" to drive cross-channel subscribe.

**2. Host a Farcaster ZAO Town Hall (once per month)**

A cast in /zao: "ZAO Town Hall — ask us anything about WaveWarZ, ZAOstock, or ZAO governance." 60-minute window of Zaal responding to replies. Creates a "Farcaster is where ZAO is most responsive" reputation.

**3. Warpcast channel growth tactic**

Submit /zao to the Warpcast channel discovery list if not already listed. Ask 5-10 ZAO participants to invite their Farcaster follows to /zao. Target: each ZAO governance participant has 50+ Farcaster followers → 10 participants × 5% follow-through = 25 new /zao members.

**4. ZAOstock Farcaster frame (high-value)**

A Farcaster Frame for ZAOstock tickets (if Empire Builder builds it per doc 1343) = in-feed ticket purchase = major channel growth driver. Every person who opens the frame sees /zao. Frame shares = passive /zao discovery.
