## Part 2: Season 2 Format

### Two tracks: Builders + Musicians

ZABAL Season 2 runs two parallel tracks:

**Track A — Builders:**
- Who: Web3 developers, designers, product people building in the music/creator space
- What they build: Tools that integrate with WaveWarZ, ZAO governance, or the broader music-on-chain ecosystem
- Milestones: Weekly demo, monthly milestone, Season 2 finals demo day
- Graduation prize: ZAO Respect allocation + featured in ZAOOS doc

**Track B — Musicians:**
- Who: Independent producers and artists who want to grow their WaveWarZ presence
- What they do: Battle strategy, fan building, onchain presence development
- Milestones: First WaveWarZ battle, first MAIN event, newsletter feature (doc 1347)
- Graduation prize: Priority consideration for ZAOstock 2027 + ZAO Respect allocation

**Combined sessions:**
- Bi-weekly joint cowork session (builders + musicians together) — builders demo tools, musicians demo tracks, cross-pollination
- Monthly ZAO governance session = ZABAL required attendance (grows governance participation)

### Season 2 format specifics

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Duration | Sep 1 – Nov 30 (13 weeks) |
| Sessions | Weekly async updates + bi-weekly live cowork (Juke audio rooms — doc 1343) |
| Applications | Aug 1 – Aug 22 (3-week window) |
| Cohort size | 6-10 builders + 6-10 musicians (cap at 20 total) |
| Cost | Free (ZAO community program) |
| Finals | Week of Nov 17 — demo day for builders, showcase battle for musicians |
| Season prize (builders) | Best project gets ZAOOS documentation + Gitcoin grant nomination |
| Season prize (musicians) | Best performer gets ZAOstock 2027 priority + COC Concertz #9 slot |

## Part 3: Applications + Recruitment

### Where to find Season 2 participants

**Builders:**
- Farcaster /builders + /dev channels
- Gitcoin grants community (builders already interested in DAO infra)
- ETHGlobal alumni networks
- ZAOOS contributors (people who submit PRs)
- Neynar/Arthur EVM review contacts (doc 1343)

**Musicians:**
- ZAOstock 2026 artists (attend ZAOstock → apply to ZABAL S2)
- WaveWarZ top battlers from Jul-Sep (active on platform)
- "Battle Your Way to ZAOstock" campaign alumni (doc 1342 — Sep 12 ends)
- Maine music scene contacts
- RAM SongChain artists (Africa Battle Week Sep 22-26 participants)

### Application form (self-hosted or Tally.so)

**Builders application fields:**
1. Name + wallet address + GitHub/Farcaster handle
2. What are you building? (1-3 sentences)
3. How does it connect to music, WaveWarZ, or ZAO governance?
4. Biggest technical challenge you're working through
5. What do you want from ZABAL? (accountability / feedback / ZAO connections)

**Musicians application fields:**
1. Name + Audius profile + WaveWarZ battle history (how many battles?)
2. What genre/style?
3. Have you used WaveWarZ? If yes, what was your experience?
4. What's your goal for the next 90 days?
5. Would you be interested in performing at ZAOstock 2027?

## Part 5: Program Delivery

### Bi-weekly cowork (ZAOcowork infrastructure)

Doc 1343 (partner activation) includes Juke audio rooms as a cowork tool. Season 2 uses Juke for the live bi-weekly sessions:
- Open cowork room on Juke (always-on, participants drop in)
- Bi-weekly structured session: 30 min check-ins, 60 min open cowork
- Recorded for ZAOOS (Bonfire episode per session)

### Weekly async updates (ZOE)

ZOE manages the async communication:
- Monday: "What's your goal this week?" prompt to all participants
- Friday: "What did you accomplish?" prompt
- ZOE compiles highlights and posts to Farcaster /zao each Friday

### Monthly milestone review (Zaal)

Zaal reviews participant progress monthly:
- Oct 1: Month 1 check-in (builder demos, musician battle recaps)
- Nov 1: Month 2 check-in
- Nov 17: Season 2 finals
