## What Makes This Unusual

### DAO governance base rates

| Governance category | What typically happens |
|--------------------|----------------------|
| DAOs with 1 governance vote | Very common — many DAOs vote once (token launch) then go quiet |
| DAOs with 2-10 governance sessions | Common among "real" DAOs — but sporadic |
| DAOs with weekly governance cadence for 3+ months | Rare — requires organizational discipline |
| DAOs with 60+ consecutive weekly on-chain sessions | Extremely rare — fewer than 5 documented globally |

ZAO is in the last category.

### Why streaks end

Understanding why most DAOs' governance streaks collapse makes ZAO's persistence more remarkable:

- **Founder fatigue:** The person who calls sessions burns out → sessions stop
- **Token holder apathy:** Nobody shows up, sessions get cancelled
- **Technical failures:** Contract bugs, gas cost spikes, chain outages
- **Community conflict:** Governance vote goes wrong → community splinters → sessions stop
- **Interest pivots:** Team gets distracted by another project

ZAO has navigated all of these. Session attendance has varied, but the session has always happened.
