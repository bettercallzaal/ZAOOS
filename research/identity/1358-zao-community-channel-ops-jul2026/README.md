## Part 3: Telegram Operations (ZOE Primary)

### Daily posts (ZOE maintains)

**Battle day (when MAIN event scheduled):**
1. Morning: "🥊 MAIN Event tonight — [Artist A] vs [Artist B]. Betting opens at [time]. wavewarz.info"
2. During battle: "🔴 LIVE: [battle link]"
3. Result: "✅ Winner: [Artist]. [Stats summary]"

**Non-battle days:**
- Weekly stats digest (Thursday, ~1pm ET)
- ZAOstock countdown (Aug 15+, 1x/week)
- Governance session reminder (Wednesday morning)
- Governance session recap (Wednesday evening)

### What ZOE should NOT post in Telegram

- Long threads (Telegram isn't Twitter)
- Multiple messages in quick succession (looks like spam)
- Anything that requires Zaal's voice (escalate to Zaal)

### What requires Zaal to respond manually

ZOE should tag Zaal in Telegram when:
- A community member asks a question ZOE can't answer confidently
- A new member introduces themselves
- Someone mentions a potential partnership or press inquiry
- Someone expresses frustration or criticism

## Part 4: Farcaster /zao Operations

### Why Farcaster /zao is different from Telegram

Farcaster users are early Web3 adopters with a higher tolerance for on-chain discussion and governance content. They care about:
- Governance transparency (on-chain proof, not just claims)
- DAO mechanics (how decisions get made)
- Community building in Web3
- ZAOstock as an on-chain cultural event

### Post types that work in /zao

1. **Governance recap** (after each Wednesday Fractal session): "ZAO governance session [N] complete. [What was decided]. Week [63+] streak."
2. **ZAO research highlights**: "New doc: [title]. [1-sentence why it matters]. Read: [link]"
3. **WaveWarZ context posts**: "WaveWarZ crossed [milestone] battles. [Stat context]. What this means for artists: [1 sentence]"
4. **ZAOstock onchain angle**: "ZAOstock artists were selected by on-chain battle history — 8 artists whose songs won the most WaveWarZ battles over 12 months. Oct 3, Ellsworth ME."
5. **Farcaster-first announcements**: New partnerships, new docs, milestone celebrations

### Optimal frequency: 3-5 posts/week in /zao

Too little (< 1/week) = channel feels dead.
Too much (> 2/day) = feels like spam and members mute.

## Part 8: ZOE Escalation Protocol

ZOE should escalate to Zaal (tag in Telegram or flag for weekly review) when:

1. **A community member asks about partnerships** — ZOE acknowledges, Zaal responds
2. **A community member makes a serious criticism** — ZOE acknowledges ("Thanks for bringing this up — Zaal will respond"), Zaal responds
3. **A press inquiry comes through community channels** — immediate escalation
4. **A community member goes beyond community norms** — Zaal handles
5. **Telegram member count drops 5%+ week-over-week** — something changed, Zaal investigates
6. **A governance topic needs Zaal's input** — ZOE surfaces it in Wednesday pre-session message
