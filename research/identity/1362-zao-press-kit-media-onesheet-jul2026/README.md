## Part 6: FAQ (Anticipated Journalist Questions)

**Q: Is this a real DAO or a paper DAO?**
> It's a real DAO. Every governance vote is on-chain at `0xcB05F9254765CA521F7698e61E0A6CA6456Be532` on Optimism Mainnet. You can verify 63+ consecutive weekly sessions yourself on the block explorer.

**Q: What does "the losing artist earns" actually mean?**
> When two artists battle on WaveWarZ, the community bets on who wins. The loser gets a percentage of the total battle pot — paid automatically on Solana, same night. The winning artist also earns. The point is that both artists have skin in the game and both get compensated.

**Q: Who are the artists at ZAOstock?**
> 8 artists selected by their WaveWarZ battle records. The lineup will be announced progressively starting August 2026 (doc 1313 reveal calendar). Two confirmed: Fellenz, Dcoop.

**Q: Is this a "crypto" event or a music event?**
> It's a music event. The technology is infrastructure — the audience doesn't need a wallet to attend. Artists are paid in SOL, which they can immediately convert to USD. The on-chain mechanics are invisible to most attendees.

**Q: How is ZAOstock funded?**
> Ticket revenue + Fisher/MaineCF grant applications + local Ellsworth business sponsorships. No token sales, no ICO. The organization is applying for fiscal sponsorship through Fractured Atlas.
