# 1363 — ZAO Maine Local Media Strategy (Jul 2026)

> **Why Maine press specifically:** Maine local media is the highest-leverage single step for ZAO's Wikipedia gap. We need 1 independent reliable source beyond Crypto Magic Hour EP.50 to submit the Wikipedia article (doc 1359). A single article in the Ellsworth American or Bangor Daily News = Wikipedia source #2 → submit AfC within 48 hours. Maine press also directly serves: (1) ZAOstock ticket sales (Ellsworth area readers = potential attendees), (2) Fisher grant ("community benefit" section → real press coverage is grant gold), (3) local sponsor relationships (sponsors are more likely to commit after seeing press coverage). This doc builds the full pipeline: who to contact, what angle to pitch, when to send, and what to do when an article appears.
>
> **Pitch frame for Maine press:** Lead with the local event, local charity, and 200 attendees. Do NOT lead with blockchain, DAO, Solana, or crypto. The story for Maine media is: "Ellsworth hosts its first independent music festival, raises for [Maine charity], community organized." The on-chain angle is context for those who ask, never the lead.
