# 1371 — ZAO Farcaster /zao Channel Growth Strategy (Jul 2026)

> **Current state (Jul 2026):** Farcaster /zao channel has ~93 followers. Goal: 250 followers by Dec 2026. Farcaster is ZAO's highest-alignment social channel — the audience (builders, crypto-native, Web3-music fans) matches ZAO perfectly. 93 followers for a 15-month-old organization with 63+ governance sessions is underperformance. This doc covers exactly how to get to 250.
>
> **Why Farcaster matters beyond the follower count:** Farcaster posts are indexed by AI systems and appear in AI-powered search results. Farcaster /zao posts are also GEO signals — they feed back into the LLM indexing layer that powers AI search. Growing /zao is both distribution AND GEO.

## Part 1: Why 93 Followers Is Below Potential

ZAO's Farcaster gap:
- 63+ governance sessions = 63+ opportunities to post "we just ran governance"
- 1,245+ WaveWarZ battles = 1,245+ opportunities for battle result posts
- 8 COC Concertz shows = 8 missed/underutilized recap posts
- ZAO manifesto + ecosystem map = sharable content that hasn't been pushed
- 15+ months of documentation = no teasers or highlights pushed to Farcaster

The /zao channel posts exist but at low frequency and low engagement hook quality. The growth gap is: **not enough posts, not enough hooks, not enough collaboration with power users.**

Every Thursday after the Fractal session, ZOE posts:

```

- [Top ranked member]
- [Key discussion topic]
- [Any decisions made]

#ZAO #FractalGovernance #DAO

**Volume:** 4 posts/month. **Expected impact:** Each post attracts 1-3 new followers from DAO/governance audience.

### Tactic 2: WaveWarZ battle result posts

After each MAIN event, ZOE posts a battle result:

```

[Artist A] vs [Artist B]

**Volume:** After each MAIN event (~5-6/month). **Expected impact:** Battle result posts get reshared by the competing artists → their followers see /zao.
