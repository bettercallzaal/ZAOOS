## 7 Tactics (Mostly ZOE-Automated)

### Tactic 1: Weekly Governance Recap Post
**Frequency:** 4x/month (after every Fractal session)  
**Owner:** ZOE (auto from fractal bot data)  
**Template:**
> ZAO Fractal session [N] complete.
>
> [X] people voted. [Top Respect earner] led this week. On-chain since session [M].
>
> 63+ consecutive weeks of on-chain governance in music. That's [Y] months. No breaks.
>
> @wavewarz | /zao

**Why it works:** Governance cadence posts attract the DAO governance audience that already follows Metagov, Eden Fractal, and similar accounts on Farcaster. Weekly cadence builds "why haven't I heard of this" familiarity.

### Tactic 2: MAIN Battle Result Posts
**Frequency:** After each MAIN event (~1-2x/month)  
**Owner:** ZOE (auto from /api/public/stats)  
**Template:**
> MAIN event [N] results:
>
> 🏆 [WINNER] vs [RUNNER-UP]
> [Total battles] battles | [SOL volume] SOL | Both artists earned
>

**Why it works:** The "loser earns" stat is the most memorable and surprising fact about WaveWarZ. Repeating it after every event trains the Farcaster audience to associate ZAO with this framing.

**Frequency:** 2x/week Aug 1–Sep 12; daily Sep 13–Oct 3  
**Owner:** ZOE (scheduled; Zaal reviews artist reveal timing with doc 1313)  
**Template variants:**
- **Artist reveals:** "Performing at ZAOstock Oct 3: [Artist]. They earned this slot with [N] WaveWarZ battles."
- **Ticket updates:** "Tickets: [N] sold. [X] left at $15. Oct 3, Ellsworth ME. wavewarz.info/zaostock"

**Why it works:** ZAOstock is the biggest follower acquisition event of the year. Every countdown post has a built-in call to follow for more updates.

### Tactic 5: Cross-Posts to /music and /dao Channels
**Frequency:** When high-performing /zao posts are written  
**Owner:** ZOE (auto repost from @zaalcaster)  
**How:** Identify /zao posts with >3 recasts or >10 reactions → ZOE reposts to /music and /dao with attribution

**Why it works:** /music and /dao have larger audiences than /zao. Cross-posts bring new followers back to the /zao channel.

### Tactic 6: Power-User Engagement by Zaal
**Frequency:** 3-5 manual replies per week  
**Owner:** Zaal (manual — ZOE can't do authentic replies)  
**Targets:** Posts from Metagov, Joshua Tan (@jst), Water & Music crew, DAO governance researchers, independent music Web3 builders  
**Approach:** Reply with a specific ZAO data point that's relevant to what they posted. Not promotional — additive to the conversation.

**Example:**
> [Reply to a DAO governance post] "We've been running weekly on-chain governance in music for 63+ sessions. Fully agree that cadence matters more than treasury size in early DAOs. Here's what we've learned: [specific point]."

**Why it works:** Authentic engagement from a founder in relevant threads drives more follows than any automated post.
