## The 5 Instagram Content Types

### Type 1 — ZAOstock Artist Announcement Cards (Iman creates)
**Frequency:** 1 per artist, staggered Aug 1–Sep 30
**Format:** 1080×1080 static image card

**Design spec (send to Iman):**
- Background: ZAOstock brand color (dark/moody)
- Artist name: large, centered
- Text: "ARTIST ANNOUNCEMENT — ZAOstock Oct 3"
- WaveWarZ stats: "[N] battles | [W]% win rate" (pull from API)
- @wavewarz tag visible
- Hashtag: #ZAOstock

**Caption template:**
> [ARTIST NAME] is performing at ZAOstock — Oct 3, Ellsworth ME.
>
> [N] WaveWarZ battles. That's how they earned this slot.
>
> Community-governed music. The ZAO picks artists by battle history, not by who they know.
>
> Tickets: [Eventbrite link] #ZAOstock #WaveWarZ

---

### Type 2 — WaveWarZ Battle Reels (ZOE coordinates, artists create)
**Frequency:** 2-3/week
**Format:** Reels (vertical, 15-60 sec)

**What goes in a battle Reel:**
- Short clip of the artist's track (from WaveWarZ or Audius)
- Text overlay: "Now battling on @wavewarz"
- CTA: "Vote at wavewarz.info"
- Result Reel after: who won, community vote count

**How ZOE gets this content:**
- Ask each artist to send a 15-sec clip of their battling track
- ZOE or Iman creates the Reel template
- After battle: update with result

**Reel caption:**
> Two artists. One battle. Community votes.
>
> [Artist A] vs [Artist B] — on @wavewarz right now.
>
> Vote and earn: wavewarz.info #WaveWarZ #MusicBattle
