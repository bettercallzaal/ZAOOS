## Wikipedia Source Potential

For Wikipedia, Mirror articles qualify as independent sources IF they have:
1. A byline (Zaal Panthaki — named author)
2. A permanent URL (Mirror provides blockchain-backed URLs)
3. Material not replicated from ZAOOS or the Wikipedia draft itself

Articles 1, 3, and 5 are the strongest Wikipedia source candidates because they contain distinct facts, quotes, or event reports not verbatim in ZAOOS.

**Trigger protocol:** Any external publication of ZAOstock reporting (Maine newspaper, podcast, or Mirror Article 5) → 48 hours → add citation to doc 1359 Wikipedia draft → submit to AfC.

**Note:** Mirror self-published articles are lower weight than Hypebot/Water & Music coverage for Wikipedia. The goal is to have Mirror articles ready to share with journalists as social proof, so those external sources get written.
