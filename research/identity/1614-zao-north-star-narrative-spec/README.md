## What ZAO Is NOT (Disambiguation)

These confusions appear in press coverage and must be actively corrected.

| Misconception | Correct statement |
|---|---|
| "ZAO is a streaming platform" | ZAO is not a streaming platform. WaveWarZ uses Audius for track submission; the platform is a prediction market. |
| "Artists are paid from streaming royalties" | Artists are paid from trading volume — 1% of every trade, plus settlement pools. Not royalties. |
| "ZAO is on Ethereum" | WaveWarZ battles are on Solana. ZAO governance contracts are on Optimism Mainnet. The $ZAO identity token is on Base. Three chains, distinct purposes. |
| "WaveWarZ is just for rappers" | WaveWarZ is genre-agnostic. Past battles include hip-hop, R&B, electronic, and pop artists. |
| "The DAO votes on everything" | ZOR token holders govern MAIN battle artist selection, charity partner selection, and Fractal Respect scores. Day-to-day operations are handled by Zaal + ZOE (AI). |
| "ZAO is a record label" | ZAO is a DAO. Incubated under ZTalent Network. Not a record label — ZAO does not own artists' music or take royalties. |
