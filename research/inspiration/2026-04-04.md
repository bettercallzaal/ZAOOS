# Steal Like an Artist — 2026-04-04

**North star:** How does ZAO OS become the operating system for a decentralized music label?
**Apps studied:** Audius · Zora · Paragraph

---

## 1. Audius — Artist Coins (October 2025)

**What they did:** Audius launched "Artist Coins" — verified artists mint their own Solana-based token directly from the Audius UI in minutes. The coin launches via a fair-launch bonding curve (no insider pre-allocation). Fans holding the coin unlock gated content, Discord access, in-app badges, leaderboards, and direct artist DMs. Artists earn 50% of trading fees plus 50% of token supply over a 5-year unlock. The critical UX decision: this entire flow — token creation, fan gating, reward distribution — lives natively alongside the artist's music profile. No external launchpad, no separate wallet UI, no context-switch.

**Pillar:** Revenue + Artist Development

**The lesson:** The token *is* the fan club. Collapsing the token launchpad into the artist profile page removes the biggest friction point (navigating away). The bonding curve + fee-share is a self-sustaining flywheel: artist promotes their music, fans buy the coin, trading fees flow back to the artist.

**ZAO adaptation:** ZAO already gates access by Respect token. The next layer: let individual ZAO artists mint a *personal* fan token (ERC-20 on Base, bonding curve via a lightweight factory contract) from their profile page. Holding the artist token could unlock: priority queue position in Radio, early Spaces access, exclusive XMTP channel. Implementation: new `src/lib/tokens/artistToken.ts` (contract factory calls via Viem), a "Launch Fan Token" button in the artist profile component, and token-gating logic extending `src/lib/gates/`. Related: [Doc 029 — Artist Revenue & IP Rights](../029-artist-revenue-ip-rights/), [Doc 143 — 0xSplits Revenue Distribution](../143-0xsplits-revenue-distribution/).

## 2. Zora — Attention Markets on Solana (February 2026)

**What they did:** Zora pivoted from NFT marketplace to "Attention Markets" — users create and trade speculative positions on internet trends, memes, and cultural moments (not content). Each "Trend" costs 1 SOL to create (spam gate). Under each Trend, users create "Pairs" that earn creator rewards on trade volume. A real-time PnL dashboard tracks positions. Zora chose Solana specifically for near-instant price updates. This is a deliberate departure: Zora is no longer a content platform — it is a cultural derivatives market.

**Pillar:** Culture + Revenue

**The lesson:** Speculation on cultural moments is a product. Zora's pivot acknowledges that the thing people actually do with on-chain culture — trade it — can itself be the product, not just the side effect. For ZAO, community proposals and weekly Snapshot polls are already cultural signals. What if those signals had tradeable weight?

**ZAO adaptation:** Surface a lightweight "signal market" inside ZAO governance: when a community proposal is submitted, members can stake Respect tokens on whether it will pass. Correct stakers earn a small bonus from incorrect stakers' stakes (no external token needed — purely Respect). This makes governance participation have positive expected value even before a proposal passes. Implementation: extend `src/app/api/proposals/` with a `stakes` table in Supabase, add a "Stake on this proposal" UI in `src/components/governance/`, resolve stakes in the existing post-vote webhook. Related: [Doc 100 — Governance Architecture](../100-governance/), [Doc 012 — Gating](../012-gating/).

## 3. Paragraph — Farcaster Mini App with In-Feed Reading (2025)

**What they did:** Paragraph (rebranded from paragraph.xyz to paragraph.com in March 2025) launched as a Farcaster Mini App. Shared Paragraph posts now load instantly *inside* the Farcaster feed — readers never leave the app. The key UX decision: views from in-feed reading are tracked back to Paragraph's own analytics dashboard under each post's total "Views" metric, with a per-platform breakdown planned. Farcaster discussion threads also pull directly into the post page on paragraph.com, so the conversation follows the content wherever it lives. The result: one piece of content, multiple surfaces, unified analytics.

**Pillar:** Distribution + Culture

**The lesson:** "Write once, read everywhere" only works if analytics follow the content too. Paragraph's insight is that creators need to see the full picture — not just their website views — to understand what's resonating. The conversation layer (Farcaster threads embedded in the post) also creates a social proof loop that drives more engagement.

**ZAO adaptation:** ZAO already auto-publishes governance proposals to Farcaster, Bluesky, and X. The gap: there's no analytics loop back. Adapt Paragraph's pattern: after a cross-platform publish, poll engagement metrics (Farcaster reactions via Neynar, Bluesky likes via AT Protocol, X via API) and surface them in a simple "Post Performance" view in the admin panel. Implementation: extend `src/lib/publish/` with an `engagementPoller.ts`, store results in a `post_analytics` Supabase table, render in `src/app/(auth)/admin/` as a read-only dashboard. Related: [Doc 063 — Cross-Platform Publishing](../063-cross-platform-publishing/), [Doc 068 — Farcaster Integration](../068-farcaster-integration/).

## Decentralized Music Label Gap Analysis

| Pillar | Score | Status |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **Artist Development** | 4/10 | Identity, Radio, social exist. Missing: artist-level analytics, fan CRM, fan token tooling. Audius shows the fan token collapses multiple gaps at once. |
| **Distribution** | 3/10 | Cross-platform publishing (Farcaster/Bluesky/X) exists but has no analytics feedback loop. Paragraph shows unified cross-platform analytics is the missing piece. |
| **Revenue** | 2/10 | Fourth consecutive day at 2/10. 0xSplits scaffolded but no actual payment rails. Audius fan tokens are the clearest path to an artist earning real money through ZAO. |
| **Governance** | 6/10 | Strongest pillar. Zora's attention markets suggest adding *skin in the game* (Respect stakes) could deepen participation quality, not just quantity. |
| **Culture** | 5/10 | Spaces, Radio, Farcaster social all active. Paragraph's "conversation follows content" pattern could strengthen the culture-to-governance feedback loop. |

**Highest-leverage next build:** Revenue (2/10), via Artist Fan Tokens. Audius compressed a token launchpad + fan club + revenue share into a single native profile action. ZAO has the gating infrastructure and the community — the missing piece is the bonding curve contract factory and the "Launch Fan Token" UI. This is the unlock that makes ZAO the *operating system* rather than just the community hub.
