## Part 3: Holly Herndon - Pioneer Profile

### Current Status (2026)
- **Holly+**: Real-time voice clone enabling anyone to sing in Herndon's vocal style. Users upload audio, get transformed "Holly+" versions. Community of stewards determines which creations can be co-sold.
- **Spawning** (co-founded 2022 with Mat Dryhurst): Building the "consent layer for AI"
  - **Have I Been Trained?**: Web tool for artists to discover if their work is in AI training datasets
  - **Source+**: Standard for artists to opt in/out of training data
  - **Kudurru**: API for automated flagging of non-consenting data
- **Awards**: Ars Electronica STARTS Prize (2022), Austria Digital Human Rights Award (2024), Kairos Prize (2025)
- **Exhibition**: "Starmirror" at KW Institute Berlin + Kunstsammlung NRW Dusseldorf (through Oct 11, 2026)
- **SXSW 2025**: Keynote on "AI, Music & Creativity in the Imagination Age"
- **X/Twitter**: @hollyherndon (Holly), @matdryhurst (Mat Dryhurst)

### Why She Matters for ZAO
Holly and Mat are building exactly what web3 music communities need - consent-based AI, artist ownership, collective governance of AI voice models. Their philosophy aligns perfectly with ZAO's values. Mat Dryhurst is also a prolific podcaster and educator (Interdependence podcast).

### ZAO Stock Potential
- **Likelihood**: Low-medium for in-person (Berlin-based, major exhibition running), HIGH for virtual session
- **Angle**: Consent in AI music, Holly+ as a model for community-owned AI voices, Spawning tools for artist protection

## Part 4: Grimes - Elf.Tech Program Update

### How It Works
- Users upload instrumental or vocal tracks
- Elf.Tech applies Grimes AI voice model (timbre, articulation, expression)
- 50/50 royalty split with Grimes
- Must credit "GrimesAI" in metadata

### What Worked
- "Artificial Angels" release (Oct 2025) - culmination of 2-year Elf.Tech experiment
- Established artist-permission and royalty-distribution as industry standard
- Influenced other platforms to adopt consent-based AI voice models
- One of the first to prove the model: artist opens voice, fans create, both earn

### What Didn't
- Quality control challenges with fan-generated content
- Legal gray areas around derivative works
- Difficulty scaling curation of thousands of submissions

### 2026 Status
- AI voice modeling is now mainstream, with Elf.Tech cited as the pioneer
- Ethical frameworks have become compulsory across the industry
- Other artists following the model (see Holly+)

## Part 8: Producers Using AI in Workflow

### How Producers Actually Use AI (2026)

| Tool Category | Examples | Use Case |
|---------------|----------|----------|
| **Composition** | Suno, Soundverse Agent, AIVA | Text-to-song, orchestral generation, natural language commands |
| **Stem Separation** | Various AI tools | Extract individual tracks (vocals, drums, bass) - previously top-producer-only capability |
| **Mastering** | LANDR | AI-powered polished masters without engineering knowledge |
| **Beat Creation** | Drumloop AI | Break creative blocks with unique royalty-free loops |
| **Voice Cloning** | RVC, ElevenLabs | Voice conversion for covers and features |
| **Arrangement** | Various | AI fills out arrangements, suggests harmonies |

### Workflow Philosophy
- AI removes repetitive steps that don't need human judgment
- Frees producers to focus on arrangement decisions, performance choices, mix moves
- Cross-tool orchestration: modern platforms link multiple tools in one conversation
- Voice input simplifies complex editing tasks

### Notable Producer Approaches
- **Timbaland**: Uploads demos to Suno, gets reworked versions, adds human lyrics after
- **David Guetta**: AI prototyping for beats, audience response scenarios
- **Arca**: Bronze AI for generative compositions, real-time ML vocal manipulation

## Key Takeaways

1. **The landscape has matured**: AI music went from novelty to Billboard charts, $3M record deals, and 87% artist adoption in 2 years
2. **Consent is the differentiator**: Holly Herndon's Spawning and Grimes' Elf.Tech established that consent-based AI is the path forward - this aligns perfectly with ZAO's web3/ownership values
3. **The real opportunity is education**: Most artists use AI to fill skill gaps and work faster, not to replace themselves. ZAO can be a leader in teaching this
4. **Community > solo creation**: The AI Song Contest, Suno Discord (400K+), Reddit communities (400K+) show that collaborative AI music creation is where the energy is
5. **Farcaster + AI music is underexplored**: Almost no one is combining Farcaster's social graph with AI music tools - this is ZAO's unique lane
