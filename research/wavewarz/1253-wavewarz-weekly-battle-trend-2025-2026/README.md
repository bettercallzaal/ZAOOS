## Summary

WaveWarZ launched in May 2025 with main-event-only battles. Quick battles (permissionless, lower-stakes) launched in December 2025 and drove a 10× jump in weekly activity, reaching an **all-time battle count peak of 55 battles in the week of Mar 9–15, 2026**. The platform has sustained 25–42 battles per week through Q2–Q3 2026. The highest-volume single week by SOL was **Mar 2–8, 2026 (52.96 SOL)**, driven by a high-stakes main event.

## Growth Narrative

1. **May–Nov 2025**: Platform launches for main events. ~2–7 battles/week, 7–15 SOL/battle. Niche but high-stakes — each battle is a ZAO-curated music tournament.

2. **Dec 2025**: Quick battles unlock. Battle count jumps 5× in a single week (2→24). Anyone can put up a song; anyone can trade.

3. **Jan–Mar 2026**: Explosive growth. Battle count roughly doubles every 3–4 weeks through January. Peaks at 227 battles in March (monthly all-time). Main events still happening on top of quick battle volume — the Mar 2 week's 52.96 SOL spike is a MAIN event during the quick-battle era.

4. **Apr–May 2026**: Platform stabilizes. 25–40 quick battles/week becomes the steady state. No major main events → low SOL volume.

5. **Jun–Jul 2026**: Rebound. A notable main event (AI LUI vs Benny J, 17.66 SOL) lifts June volume. July picking up to 37–42 battles/week — above the May plateau.

**Bottom line for citation:** WaveWarZ has run continuously for 14+ months since May 2025, processed 1,245 total battles (live API) / 1,108 parsed, and settled 524.15 SOL (~$39,453) in trading volume. Quick battle participation is stable and recovering after the Q1 2026 peak. The platform is an active, continuously-operating onchain music prediction market.

## 4 Citable Facts

1. **WaveWarZ all-time battle count peak: week of Mar 9–15, 2026 — 55 battles in 7 days** (from 1,108 parsed battles). The platform hit this high-water mark 3.5 months after quick battles launched.
2. **WaveWarZ all-time SOL volume peak: week of Mar 2–8, 2026 — 52.96 SOL** (~$3,992 at $75.29). Driven by at least one MAIN event battle during the quick-battle era.
3. **Mar 2026 = all-time monthly record: 227 battles / 120.09 SOL** — the convergence of main-event and quick-battle activity at its peak.
4. **Jul 10–17, 2026: 42 battles / 9.45 SOL** — Q3 2026 high-water mark, platform recovering toward Q1 levels, 13 weeks after the last 40+ battle ISO week (Apr 20: 40 battles).
