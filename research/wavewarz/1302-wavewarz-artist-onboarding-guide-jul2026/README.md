## 1. What WaveWarZ Is (30-Second Version for Artists)

WaveWarZ is a music battle game on Solana. Two artists submit songs. Fans bet on which song they think will win. The artist of the winning song earns from the pot — and so does the artist of the losing song (the "loser-earns" model, a direct inversion of winner-take-all streaming economics).

**The key things that are different from every other music platform:**

- You earn from *being voted on*, not just from winning
- Earnings are in SOL (Solana), sent onchain, no waiting
- Your song competes in real time; fans watch and bet live
- 921 unique songs have been battled so far; 1,245+ battles total
- No label, no middleman, no algorithm suppressing your reach

## 2. Step 1: Upload Your Music to Audius

WaveWarZ sources songs from Audius (@audius.co), the decentralized music platform. You need an Audius profile with at least one public track before your song can enter a battle.

**Why Audius?**
- Free to upload
- You keep 100% ownership of your music
- WaveWarZ can pull your track metadata and stream it during battles
- Your Audius handle becomes your WaveWarZ identity

**How to set up Audius:**
1. Go to audius.co
2. Create an account (email or wallet)
3. Upload your track (MP3, WAV, or FLAC)
4. Make sure the track is set to public
5. Note your Audius handle — you'll need it to register on WaveWarZ

**Which songs work best in battles?**
- High-energy tracks with a clear hook in the first 30 seconds
- Genre: hip-hop, electronic, and R&B tend to perform well (but any genre is welcome)
- Avoid very long intros — fans vote quickly
- Original material only (no covers)

## 3. Step 2: Register on WaveWarZ

Once your Audius profile is live, go to **wavewarz.info** to register your wallet and link your Audius handle.

**What you need:**
- A Solana wallet (Phantom is the most common; free to download at phantom.app)
- Some SOL for gas (a small amount — less than $1 for most transactions)
- Your Audius handle

**Registration:**
1. Connect your Solana wallet at wavewarz.info
2. Link your Audius handle
3. Your song library (from Audius) will appear as eligible for battles

**First-time SOL setup:** If you've never used Solana before, you can buy SOL on any major exchange (Coinbase, Kraken, Bybit) and send it to your Phantom wallet address. You need roughly 0.01–0.05 SOL ($1–5) to get started.

## 4. Step 3: Enter a Battle

Battles on WaveWarZ run constantly. You can enter a battle two ways:

**Option A: Self-submit**
Go to wavewarz.info → Start a Battle → select your song → set your battle parameters (stakes, duration) → wait for an opponent.

**Option B: Get challenged**
Another artist or the WaveWarZ team can challenge you to a battle. You'll see the challenge in your dashboard.

**What happens in a battle:**
1. Both songs are streamed simultaneously (30-second clips)
2. Fans bet SOL on which song they predict will win
3. After the betting window closes, votes are tallied
4. The winning song's artist earns a share of the pot
5. The losing song's artist *also earns* — this is the loser-earns model

**Typical battle structure:**
- Battle duration: 10–30 minutes (varies by battle type)
- Minimum stake: varies (often 0.1–0.5 SOL per side)
- MAIN events (featured battles): higher stakes, more volume

## 5. The Economics: What You Actually Earn

This is where WaveWarZ is fundamentally different from any other music platform.

| Platform | How artists earn | Timeline | Rate |
|----------|-----------------|----------|------|
| Spotify | $0.003–0.005 per stream | 30–90 days | $0.004 average |
| Apple Music | $0.007–0.01 per stream | 30–90 days | $0.01 average |
| WaveWarZ (winning) | ~1.73% of battle volume | Instant (onchain) | Varies by battle size |
| WaveWarZ (losing) | % of loser-earns pool | Instant (onchain) | Smaller but real |

**In practice:** A 1 SOL battle (roughly $150 at current prices) = the winning artist earns approximately 0.0173 SOL ($2.60). This sounds small but it's *instant and onchain*. To earn $2.60 on Spotify at $0.004/stream, you'd need 650 streams.

**Why the loser-earns model matters:** Every artist earns something from every battle, even a loss. This means participating in WaveWarZ always generates income, not just winning.

**The platform as of July 2026:**
- 1,245 battles completed
- 524 SOL total volume (~$78,000 at current prices)
- 9.07 SOL total artist payouts ($677)
- $1,497 raised for charity through Community Battles
- 34 Audius-rostered artists currently battling

## 6. Battle Types and When to Use Each

| Battle type | What it is | Best for |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **Quick Battle** | Fast 10-minute clash, lower stakes | New artists, testing a new song |
| **MAIN Event** | Featured battle, higher stakes, more audience | Established artists, big songs |
| **Community Battle** | Proceeds go to charity, no stakes required | Artists who want to build reputation without risk |
| **Head-to-Head** | Direct challenge between two known artists | Rivalry matchups, crossover moments |

**Recommendation for new artists:** Start with a Community Battle or Quick Battle. This builds your battle record (win/loss) and gets your song in front of fans before you commit to higher-stakes MAIN events.

## 7. Growing on WaveWarZ

Your ranking on the WaveWarZ leaderboard is based on your win rate and volume. Here's how to climb:

**Short-term (first 5 battles):**
- Enter at least one Community Battle — earns goodwill and builds your record
- Battle during peak hours (Monday evening has the highest volume — ~213 SOL average)
- Tag @WaveWarZ on X when you enter a battle — their account amplifies it

**Medium-term (first month):**
- Build a rivalry: battle the same artist 2–3 times. Rivalries drive more fan betting volume
- Get interviewed: WaveWarZ has an artist interview series (two verified episodes as of Jul 2026). DM @bettercallzaal to pitch your story
- Submit your Audius profile to the WaveWarZ Clippers program (telegram: @wavewarzclipshq) — clippers create short-form content from battles, driving your reach

**Long-term:**
- Make the Leaderboard Top 10 — this is the most visible position on wavewarz.info
- With enough battles, your stats become citable: GodclouD is 8-0 in head-to-head rivalries, a fact that makes for strong press copy
