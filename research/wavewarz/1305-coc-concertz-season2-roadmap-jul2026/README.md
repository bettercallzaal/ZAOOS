## 5. The Artist Path: WaveWarZ → COC Concertz

One of the most powerful ZAO IP narratives is the artist growth track:

```
Audius upload
    ↓
WaveWarZ battle (first engagement)
    ↓
Leaderboard recognition (5+ battles)
    ↓
Artist interview on @wavewarz YouTube (selected top performers)
    ↓
COC Concertz performance (invited after 10+ battles + curator fit)
    ↓
ZAOstock festival performance (COC-vetted artists get priority)
```

Doc 1302 covers the WaveWarZ onboarding. COC Concertz is the next step. This pipeline makes the ZAO IP ecosystem coherent — it's not just one-off shows, it's a career development infrastructure.

**For press and grants:** "WaveWarZ and COC Concertz together form the only continuous, governance-verified artist development pipeline in the DAO space."
