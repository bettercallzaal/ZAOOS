## Partner 1: Coinflow (fiat on-ramp)

**Integration:** Coinflow ISV with merchantID "wavestation." SOL transaction fees covered. Enables fiat users to participate without owning SOL first. Runs through BCZ Strategies LLC (Zaal's legal entity).

**Current state:** Live and functional. WaveWarZ is one of Coinflow's ISV merchants. No co-marketing has been done.

**Distribution opportunity:** Coinflow serves WaveWarZ but also has its own newsletter, social channels, and partner network. A mention in Coinflow's marketing = exposure to their entire merchant/consumer base.

### Activation Play: Coinflow Feature Story

**What to do:**
1. Email Coinflow account manager: "Can WaveWarZ be featured as a merchant success story in your newsletter or website?"
2. Provide: battle count (1,245), volume ($39K), "first onchain music battle platform using Coinflow for fiat on-ramp"
3. Ask: "Can you add a co-branded callout on your website under 'Partner Success Stories'?"

**Expected yield:** 1 mention in Coinflow's channels = 500-2,000 new WaveWarZ eyeballs

**Timeline:** Email this week → feature within 30 days

**Integration:** Juke /spaces and /live integration. 9 of 11 ZAO feature asks shipped as of May 23, 2026. Audio room artists can participate in WaveWarZ battles from within Juke sessions.

**Distribution opportunity:** Juke has active audio room creators who are music-adjacent. A joint MAIN event — "Juke x WaveWarZ Battle Night" — where Juke /spaces hosts the pre-show and WaveWarZ hosts the battle — crosses the two audiences.

### Activation Play 1: Joint MAIN Event (next available date)

**Timeline:** DM Juke team by Jul 25 → joint event by Aug 15
