## How WaveWarZ Works (Artist Perspective)

WaveWarZ is a **music prediction market**. Two artists battle head-to-head. The audience bets SOL on which artist will win. When the battle closes:

- **Winner's traders** earn from the losing side's pool
- **Losing artist** receives a guaranteed payout from the loser-earns pool
- **Protocol** keeps 3% of total volume
- **Winning artist** doesn't earn directly from the market (their traders earn on their behalf)

The key innovation: **the losing artist always earns.** The amount depends on the total SOL bet against them.

## The Loser-Earns Math in Detail

The loser-earns rate (1.73%) is set by the WaveWarZ protocol. Here's how it works:

```
[Battle closes with results:]

Total SOL volume: [V] SOL
Winning side: [W] SOL (these traders split the loser pool)
Losing side: [L] SOL (these traders lose their SOL)

Protocol fee: 3% of V = 0.03V
Loser artist payout: 1.73% of L
Trader payouts (winners): remainder of L after protocol fee and artist payout

Net to losing artist: 0.0173 × L SOL

Example: L = 20 SOL → 0.0173 × 20 = 0.346 SOL (~$69 at $200/SOL)
```

**Why 1.73%?** This rate was set by Hurricane (WaveWarZ developer) to ensure artists always earn something meaningful from battles without disrupting the trader incentive structure. It is protocol-level — not optional, not subject to artist negotiation.

## How to Maximize Your WaveWarZ Earnings

### For any artist:

1. **Get your community betting on your opponent.** Counterintuitively: the more people bet against you, the more you earn as the loser. High-volume losses = big payouts.

2. **Compete in MAIN battles whenever possible.** MAIN battles have higher volume targets. One MAIN battle can earn 10x a quick battle.

3. **Apply for community battles.** Community battles have charity allocation but still include loser-earns. Being associated with a charity battle improves your narrative.

4. **Promote your battles.** The more people who know about your battle (and bet on it), the higher the volume — and the higher the loser-earns payout if you lose.

5. **Use the ZOE content templates.** Post your battle results, wins, and losses on X and Farcaster. ZOE provides the templates (doc 1302, 1385). Artists who post consistently build a reputation that attracts higher-volume battles.

### For MAIN battle invites:

ZAO invites artists to MAIN battles based on:
- Existing WaveWarZ battle history (quality battles, community engagement)
- Social following on X, Farcaster, or TikTok
- ZABAL participation
- Recommendation from other ZAO community members

To request a MAIN battle: DM @bettercallzaal on X or post in ZAO Telegram.
