## Summary (Snapshot: July 17, 2026)

WaveWarZ is a Solana-based music battle platform using a prediction-market model where traders bet SOL on which of two artists will win a head-to-head competition, and the losing artist earns a share of the loser-side pool.

## Battle Type Breakdown

WaveWarZ runs three types of battles:

### MAIN Battles (Curated)
- **Count:** 162 battles across 50 MAIN events
- **Format:** Organized event sets; multiple battles per event
- **Typical volume per battle:** ~3.2 SOL average (calculated: 523 × ~50% / 162, rough estimate — confirm with Hurricane)
- **Significance:** The primary showcase format; COC Concertz shows feature live MAIN events

### Quick Battles (Open Entry)
- **Count:** 1,047 battles (84% of all battles)
- **Format:** Any registered artist can challenge any other; no event structure required
- **Significance:** The growth engine; shows WaveWarZ as a peer-to-peer market

### Community/Charity Battles
- **Count:** 36 battles
- **Format:** Battle where a portion of trading volume is directed to a nonprofit
- **Significance:** ZAO's "mission layer" — every charity battle is a WaveWarZ + nonprofit partnership

## Economic Summary

The WaveWarZ economic model distributes SOL across four parties per battle:

| Party | % of trading volume | As of Jul 17, 2026 |
|-------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Winning traders | ~24.3% | 127.343 SOL claimed |
| Protocol fee | 3% | ~15.72 SOL (calculated) |
| Loser-earns pool | ~1.73% of losing side | 9.0988 SOL to artists |
| Remainder circulates | — | Volume net of payouts |

**Total volume: 523.991 SOL**

At $200/SOL (approximate Jul 2026 price):
- Total volume: ~$104,798
- Artist payouts: ~$1,820
- Trader claims: ~$25,469
- Protocol fee: ~$3,144

## What $1,820 in Artist Payouts Means

For context on the 9.0988 SOL in artist payouts:

WaveWarZ paid **$1,820 to artists who lost music competitions.**

For comparison:
- Spotify pays approximately $3-5 per 1,000 streams
- $1,820 in Spotify earnings requires approximately 364,000-607,000 streams
- Those streams may never come for many WaveWarZ artists
- WaveWarZ artists earned $1,820 while **losing** — with no streaming required

This framing is used in: doc 1421 (artist earnings guide), doc 1414 (music press pitches), doc 1390 (press release).
