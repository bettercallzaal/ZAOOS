## COC Format Recap (for pitch context)

- Each artist submits 1 original track (2–5 min)
- Both tracks mint simultaneously on Arweave under ZAO CC-BY
- Community votes via WaveWarZ battle — loser earns from the losing pool
- Winner gets COC Champion title + ZOE promotion for 7 days
- No fee to enter; artists keep all future royalties on their track

**Key pitch line:** "You can't lose — the loser still earns SOL from the battle pool."
