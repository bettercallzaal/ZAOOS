## What Is a Quick Battle

A quick battle is the standard WaveWarZ battle format — two artists submit tracks, fans trade prediction tokens on the outcome, both artists earn from trading volume in real time, and the loser receives a guaranteed payout at settlement.

- **No application required** — any artist can submit a quick battle
- **No ZOR required** — ZOR is only required for MAIN battles
- **Minimum setup:** Audius account + Phantom wallet + SOL for gas (~0.001 SOL)
- **Duration:** typically 24-48 hours (current default — check wavewarz.info for live setting)

**As of July 2026:** 1,047 quick battles completed. 1,245 total battles.

---

## The Core Mechanic (Artist Perspective)

Before you submit: understand this so you don't panic when you lose.

1. **Both artists earn while the battle runs** — every trade (buy or sell of prediction tokens) generates 1% that goes to the artists. Volume during the battle is income.
2. **The winner takes the larger settlement pool** — but the loser is NOT paid zero.
3. **The loser receives a guaranteed payout** from the settlement pool — the exact amount depends on total volume, but it is never zero.
4. **Payouts are automatic and on-chain** — no invoice, no wait, no claim needed. SOL goes directly to your connected Phantom wallet when the battle closes.

**The headline:** Even if you lose, you get paid. That is not common in music anywhere.

## Step-by-Step: How to Submit a Quick Battle

### Prerequisites

- [ ] Audius account with at least one published track (audius.co — free)
- [ ] Phantom wallet (phantom.app — free, install browser extension or iOS/Android app)
- [ ] SOL in your Phantom wallet for gas (minimum ~0.005 SOL to be safe; ~$0.37 at $74/SOL)

### Step 1 — Find an opponent (or let WaveWarZ match you)

Two options:
- **Self-match:** Go to wavewarz.info, click "Start Battle." Choose your opponent from the artist directory or enter their Audius handle.
- **Open challenge:** Post in the WaveWarZ X community (@WaveWarZ) or ZAO Telegram that you're looking for a battle. Another artist will respond.

**Tips:** Pick an artist in a similar genre for fair matching. Genre-matched battles get more trading volume (fans know both artists).

### Step 2 — Submit your track

At wavewarz.info → "Start Battle":
1. Connect your Phantom wallet (top-right)
2. Enter your Audius track URL or handle
3. Enter your opponent's Audius track URL or handle
4. Choose battle duration (default or custom)
5. Confirm the transaction in Phantom (~0.001 SOL gas)

The battle is now live. Fans can start trading immediately.

### Step 3 — Promote the battle

The more people trade, the more both artists earn. Share the battle link.

**Suggested post (X):**
```
I just challenged [opponent] to a WaveWarZ battle 🎵⚡

Predict the winner and earn — trades are open now.
Even if I lose, I get paid. That's WaveWarZ.

Vote: [wavewarz.info battle link]
```

**Send to:**
- Your X followers
- ZAO Telegram: ask community to trade
- Farcaster /wavewarz: post the battle link for the WaveWarZ community

### Step 4 — Watch the real-time dashboard

At wavewarz.info you can see:
- Current token price curve (bonding curve)
- Number of trades
- Total SOL volume so far
- Your earned 1% from trades so far (accrues in real time)

### Step 5 — Settlement

When the battle closes:
- The smart contract settles automatically
- Winner and loser payouts fire to both Phantom wallets
- No action needed — just check your wallet

## What Affects How Much You Earn

| Factor | Effect |
|---|---|
| Trading volume | Higher volume = more 1% fee income for both artists |
| Battle promotion | More promotion = more trades = more income |
| Match notoriety | Known-artist matchups attract more volume |
| Duration | Longer battles can accumulate more volume, but also more volatility |

**Your best lever:** promote the battle hard in the first 2-4 hours. Early volume begets more volume as the bonding curve shifts.

## Quick Battle vs MAIN Battle: Key Differences

| | Quick Battle | MAIN Battle |
|---|---|---|
| Who can enter | Any artist | ZOR nominees only |
| Approval required | No | ZOR holder vote (governance) |
| Volume | Lower (community scale) | Higher (featured, promoted) |
| Payout | Both earn (1% fee + loser settlement) | Both earn (higher pool) |
| Duration | 24-48h default | Set by ZAO governance |
| On COC lineup | No | Yes (COC events feature MAIN battles) |

**Path to MAIN:** Participate in quick battles → build WaveWarZ SOL earned → join ZAO Fractal → earn ZOR → get nominated for MAIN battle by ZOR holders.

## Common Questions

**"What if my opponent doesn't promote?"**  
You still earn 1% of all trades, including your own network's trades. Promote it yourself — the loser still earns regardless.

**"Can I battle the same person twice?"**  
Yes. No limit on rematches. Many popular battle series are recurring matchups.

**"What's the minimum payout?"**  
There is no absolute minimum — it depends on volume. Low-volume battles can have small payouts. Higher promotion = more volume = more payout.

**"Do I need to attend the battle?"**  
No. Submit, promote, and check your wallet at settlement. It's fully automatic.

**"My Audius track isn't showing up on WaveWarZ."**  
Make sure your Audius track is published (not draft), and use the correct Audius handle (not your display name). Some Audius handles differ from X handles — use the `/` URL slug.
