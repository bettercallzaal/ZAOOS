## What Is a Community Battle?

A community battle is a WaveWarZ battle that is:
- Proposed and hosted by a community organizer (not ZAO core)
- Approved by ZOR holders in the weekly Fractal session
- Open to fans for trading (staking SOL on artists)
- Settled automatically on-chain when the battle closes

Community battles are distinct from MAIN battles (which happen at COC Concertz shows with live audiences) and Quick battles (which run continuously with anyone).

**As of Jul 2026:** 36 community battles have been approved and run. The Africa Battle Week (Sep 22-26) will feature at least 5 community battles.

---

## Who Can Host a Community Battle

Any community organizer with:
1. A WaveWarZ-registered account (wavewarz.info)
2. Two willing artists (both must have Audius tracks + Solana wallets)
3. ZOR holder approval (via weekly Fractal session)
4. A proposed battle date + community context

**You don't need to be a ZAO member or ZOR holder yourself.** You do need ZOR holders to approve the battle — which means you need to pitch it in a Fractal session or connect with Zaal.

## The Community Battle Process

### Step 1: Propose the Battle (Day -14 minimum)

Submit your battle proposal to Zaal via Telegram or X DM with:

```
COMMUNITY BATTLE PROPOSAL

Host: [your name / organization]
Community context: [what community are you from? Why does this battle matter?]

Artist A: [name + Audius handle + Solana wallet]
Artist B: [name + Audius handle + Solana wallet]

Proposed battle window: [start date/time → end date/time]
Track links: [Audius URLs for both artists]

Will you promote this battle to your community? [yes/no]
```

**Zaal routes this to the next Fractal session for ZOR holder approval.**

### Step 2: ZOR Holder Approval (Weekly Fractal Session)

At the weekly Fractal session, ZOR holders discuss and vote on proposed community battles. Approval criteria:
- Both artists have active Audius tracks in good standing
- Battle window doesn't conflict with MAIN battles or COC Concertz shows
- Community context is genuine (not a sock puppet battle)
- Organizer has a track record or voucher from a ZOR holder

**Approval is usually granted within 1 session (1 week).** If there are questions, Zaal will contact you before the session.

### Step 3: Battle Setup (Day -7)

After approval, ZAO sets up the battle in the WaveWarZ system. You don't need to do this yourself — Zaal or the WW operator creates the battle on wavewarz.info with:
- Artist A and Artist B
- Battle start/end time
- Community label (e.g., "Africa Battle Week: [artist] vs [artist]")
- Battle type: Community

**You receive:** Battle URL on wavewarz.info and a confirmation with the exact battle window.

### Step 4: Promotion (Day -7 to Day 0)

You are responsible for promoting the battle to your community. ZAO will promote to ZAO channels (X, Farcaster, Telegram) if you ask.

**Promotion assets ZAO can provide:**
- Battle announcement post template (X + Farcaster)
- Battle card image (from ZOE, based on artist names)
- Stats block for context (how WaveWarZ works, loser earns, etc.)

**Promotion you should do:**
- Tell both artists to promote the battle to their fanbases
- Post to your community channels (Discord, Telegram, Twitter)
- Ask artists to pin the battle URL in their Audius bio during the battle window

### Step 5: Battle Window

During the battle, fans visit wavewarz.info and stake SOL on artists. The battle URL is always: `wavewarz.info/battle/[BATTLE_ID]`.

**What you see during the battle:**
- Real-time vote count (% for each artist)
- Total SOL staked
- Time remaining

**What you do during the battle:**
- Share updates to your community channels ("Artist A is leading 62-38")
- Answer questions about how the staking works (use the FAQ in this doc)
- Don't share the vote results too early — let the battle play out organically

### Step 6: Settlement (Automatic)

When the battle window closes, WaveWarZ settles automatically:
- Winning traders: 80% of the losing pool
- Winning artist: 10% of the losing pool
- **Losing artist: 10% of the winning pool** (this is what makes WaveWarZ different)

**Settlement is on-chain and automatic.** Neither you nor ZAO needs to take any action. Payouts go directly to artist wallets.

### Step 7: Post-Battle (Day +1)

After settlement:
1. **ZOE posts the battle result** to ZAO channels (X, Farcaster, Telegram)
2. **You share the result** to your community with the TX link proving the payout
3. **Both artists receive SOL** — you can screenshot the wallet or link to Solscan

**Result post template:**
```
[Artist A] vs [Artist B] — settled.

[Artist A] won.
[Artist B] earned [X] SOL — automatic.
No claim. On Solana.

TX: [Solscan link]
wavewarz.info
```

## Artist Requirements

Both artists in a community battle must:

1. **Have an Audius account** — free at audius.co. The battle requires an Audius track URL.
2. **Have a Solana wallet** — Phantom (phantom.app) is easiest. Artists need a wallet to receive their payout.
3. **Agree to participate** — get explicit consent. WaveWarZ payouts are automatic; once the battle starts, the artist can't opt out mid-battle.
4. **Have at least one track** that both parties agree will represent them in the battle

**Artist payout:** Artists receive SOL automatically when the battle settles. There is no minimum battle volume required — even a small battle pays both artists.

| Feature | Community Battle | MAIN Battle | Quick Battle |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|-------------|

| ZOR approval required | Yes | Yes | No |

| Volume potential | Moderate | Highest | Low-moderate |

## FAQ for Community Hosts

**"What's the minimum SOL to run a community battle?"**
There's no minimum on your end. Traders choose how much to stake. Some battles have 0.1 SOL total; others have 10+. Volume depends on the artists' fanbases and your promotion.

**"Can the same artist battle twice?"**
Yes. Artists can run multiple community battles. Many WaveWarZ regulars battle repeatedly.

**"What if an artist drops out after approval?"**
Contact Zaal immediately. The battle can be rescheduled or the artist replaced. If the battle is already live, it cannot be cancelled — both artists are committed.

**"Who controls the SOL in the battle?"**
No one. SOL goes into a PDA (Program Derived Account) on Solana when traders stake. WaveWarZ's smart contract controls it. ZAO cannot access it. Settlement is trustless — automatic at close.

**"Can I charge artists to be in my battle?"**
No. Community battles are open to approved artists at no cost. The payout mechanic is entirely from trader stakes.

**"What if very little SOL is staked?"**
Both artists still earn their % of whatever is staked. A 0.05 SOL battle still pays out 0.005 SOL to each artist. Small but real and automatic.

**"How do I track my battle's performance?"**
During the battle: wavewarz.info/battle/[ID]. After settlement: Solscan TX link from the settlement notification. ZOE posts the result with the TX link.
