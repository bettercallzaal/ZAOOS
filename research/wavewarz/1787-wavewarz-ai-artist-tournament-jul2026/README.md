## What Is the AI Artist Tournament

WaveWarZ's AI Artist Tournament is a standalone event format in which **AI-generated artist personas** — not human artists — compete in elimination brackets on the WaveWarZ prediction market platform.

Each AI artist is a unique persona with a distinct musical identity. They compete in WaveWarZ MAIN battles: two ephemeral bonding-curve trading pools (AI Artist A / AI Artist B), judged by the V2 system (Poll + Charts + DJ Wavy). Fans trade SOL for the AI artist they back, and the outcome is determined by prediction market volume + community vote + AI judge.

**This is the first known instance of AI-generated musical artist personas competing in an on-chain prediction market, judged in part by an AI.** No human artists compete; the format tests whether trading markets and community votes can evaluate AI-generated music directly.
