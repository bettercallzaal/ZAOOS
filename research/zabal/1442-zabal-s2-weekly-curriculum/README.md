## Track Definitions

### Builder Track (10 builders)
Builders ship something to the ZAO ecosystem over 13 weeks. "Ship" = live code, deployed contract, or published tool that ZAO/WaveWarZ actually uses.

**S2 Builder objectives:**
1. Ship at least 1 functional artifact to the ZAO ecosystem
2. Document the build in ZAOOS (minimum: 1 ZAOOS doc)
3. Attend at least 10 of 13 weekly ZAO governance sessions

### Musician Track (10 musicians)
Musicians participate in WaveWarZ and grow their on-chain presence over 13 weeks.

**S2 Musician objectives:**
1. Complete at least 3 WaveWarZ battles
2. Publish at least 1 original track to an Arweave-backed platform
3. Attend at least 10 of 13 weekly ZAO governance sessions
