## Application Form Spec (Google Form)

**Form title:** ZABAL S2 Application — Builder & Musician Track (Sep 1 – Nov 30, 2026)

**Form intro text:**
```
ZABAL is the ZAO Builder and Artist League — a 13-week program where musicians and builders work together, ship in public, and earn ZOR governance tokens for the ZAO ecosystem.

Season 2 runs Sep 1 – Nov 30, 2026. Two tracks:
• Builder: ship at least 1 artifact + 1 ZAOOS doc + attend 10/13 sessions
• Musician: complete 3 WaveWarZ battles + upload 1 track to Arweave + attend 10/13 sessions

Applications are reviewed by Zaal Panthaki (ZAO co-founder). We accept 15-20 participants per season.

Deadline: August 22, 2026 at 11:59 PM EST.
```

### Section 1: Who You Are
1. **Full name or alias** (required)
2. **Email address** (required)
3. **X handle** (optional)
4. **Farcaster handle** (optional)
5. **Wallet address (Optimism or Solana)** — required for ZOR token awards
6. **How did you hear about ZABAL?**  
   - Options: ZAO Telegram, Farcaster /zao, Twitter/X, WaveWarZ, COC Concertz, friend referral, ZAOstock, other

### Section 2: Your Track
7. **Which ZABAL track are you applying for?** (required)
   - Builder (coding, research, content creation, design)
   - Musician (original music, WaveWarZ battles)
   - Both (musicians who also ship code/docs)

### Section 3: Builder Track (show only if Builder or Both selected)
8. **Describe your builder experience** (3-5 sentences)  
   "What have you shipped before? Any links to repos, apps, or projects you're proud of?"
9. **What do you want to build in ZABAL S2?** (2-3 sentences)  
   "Specific is better. 'A WaveWarZ analytics dashboard' beats 'something with music'."
10. **Link to your best public work** (GitHub, portfolio, deployed app — optional but preferred)

### Section 4: Musician Track (show only if Musician or Both selected)
11. **Describe your music background** (3-5 sentences)  
    "Genre, experience level, how you got into music."
12. **Have you done a WaveWarZ battle before?** Yes / No
13. **Link to a track you've released or uploaded** (Audius, SoundCloud, Arweave, YouTube — optional but preferred)
14. **What do you want to achieve in ZABAL S2?** (2-3 sentences)

### Section 5: Commitment
15. **ZABAL S2 runs every Monday for 13 weeks (Sep 1 – Nov 30). Sessions are ~1 hour. Can you commit to attending 10 of 13 sessions?** Yes / No / I'll try but can't guarantee
16. **ZAOstock is October 3, 2026 in Ellsworth, Maine. Are you planning to attend in person?** Yes / No / Maybe / I'm not in Maine but will watch the stream
17. **Is there anything that might prevent you from completing S2 that you want us to know about?** (optional)

### Section 6: The Last Thing
18. **Why ZABAL?** (free response, no word limit)  
    "What draws you to this program specifically? What do you hope to get out of it that you can't get elsewhere?"

## Selection Criteria (Zaal reviews Aug 25)

Prioritize in this order:

1. **S1 ZABAL alumni** with good completion record (automatic acceptance if returning)
2. **WaveWarZ artists** with 3+ battles (proven commitment to the ecosystem)
3. **COC Concertz performers** (existing ZAO artist community)
4. **Builders with shipped artifacts** — link to real work beats promises
5. **Musicians with uploaded tracks** — link to released/public music preferred

**Red flags (not disqualifying, but noted):**
- No public handle / no verifiable identity
- "Maybe" on attendance commitment without explanation
- Very generic "Why ZABAL?" answer (copy-paste energy)

**Target cohort composition:**
- 60% musicians / 40% builders (to support WaveWarZ growth)
- At least 2 Africa-based participants (prep for Africa Battle Week S2 Week 3-4)
- At least 1 ZAOstock in-person attendee
