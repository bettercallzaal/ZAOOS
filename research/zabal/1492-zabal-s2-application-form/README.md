## Application Rubric (for Zaal / Review Team)

Score each application 0-10 across 4 dimensions:

| Dimension | Max Points | What to Look For |
|---|---|---|
| Work quality (Q9M-12M or Q9B-11B) | 0-3 | Is the work real? Is there evidence? Is the "what would I build" specific and achievable? |
| ZAO alignment (Q15) | 0-3 | Does the applicant understand ZAO's core model? Do they care about loser-earns / governance / community (not just "getting exposure")? |
| Commitment (Q16-17) | 0-2 | Can they actually do 4-6h/week for 8 weeks? ZAOstock attendance is a bonus. |
| Uniqueness (overall) | 0-2 | Does this person bring something no one else on the list has? (Genre diversity for musicians, skill diversity for builders) |

**Minimum score to accept:** 6/10

**Target cohort composition:**
- 30 total (15 musician + 15 builder)
- Musician diversity: at least 3 genres, at least 1 Maine-based artist
- Builder diversity: at least 3 skill types (dev, research/doc, community)
- At least 5 participants from ZABAL S1 (returning = strong signal)

## Review Process

**Aug 22:** Applications close  
**Aug 22-28:** Review week  
- ZOE flags any applications with score <4 as auto-decline
- Zaal reviews all borderline (4-6) and high (7-10) applications
- Cohort discussion in governance session (Aug 27)
**Sep 1:** Acceptance emails sent (ZOE sends, Zaal approves template)  
**Sep 1:** Public cohort announcement (X + Farcaster + Telegram)
