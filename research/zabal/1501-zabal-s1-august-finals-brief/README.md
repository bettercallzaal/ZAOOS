## What Are the S1 August Finals?

ZABAL Season 1 ran approximately 28 workshops (Jun–Jul 2026) with 32 builders participating. S1 stalled in July (documented in doc 1466) — the August Finals are the structured close that:

1. Determines which S1 participants officially "graduated"
2. Creates the canonical alumni list used for S2 recruitment priority
3. Closes S1 so S2 can launch Sep 1 with a clean slate
4. Generates citable "cohort completion" numbers for grants (Fisher, MAC, OP RF)

---

## The S1 Completion Criteria (From Doc 1466)

A ZABAL S1 participant completes if they hit ANY of:

| Track | Completion Criteria |
|---|---|
| Builder | 1 shipped artifact (code, design, or research doc) + attended ≥10/28 sessions |
| Musician | 1 WaveWarZ battle entered + attended ≥10/28 sessions |

**Partial credit (alumni, non-completer):**
- Attended ≥5/28 sessions and contributed in Telegram
- Nominated for alumni status by another S1 participant

### Decision 2: S1 Showcase / Capstone

Should there be an S1 showcase or capstone event before S2 launches?

| Option | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| **A: Online Showcase (Aug 14-15)** | Documents S1 work; creates content for S2 recruitment | Requires planning + participant coordination |
| **B: No Showcase — Close Quietly** | Saves time; S1 was incomplete anyway | Loses grant-citability ("we ran a cohort showcase") |
| **C: Async Showcase via ZAOOS** | Each completer submits a 1-page ZAOOS doc as their capstone | Low coordination overhead; all visible in CC-BY corpus |

**Recommendation: Option C** — async ZAOOS showcase. Each S1 completer writes a 1-page ZAOOS doc by Aug 20, mentored by ZOL (the ZAOOS librarian). Zaal reviews. This creates citable outputs AND eases each participant into the ZAOOS contribution pattern before S2.

## Why This Matters for S2

The cleaner S1 closes, the stronger S2 opens:
- Alumni = built-in testimonials ("I completed S1 and it got me X")
- Incomplete participants = warm leads for S2 recruitment ("you didn't finish S1 — here's your second shot")
- Citable completion rate = grant evidence ("ZAO ran a 32-person creative cohort with [Y]% completion")

A strong S1 close is the narrative foundation for the Fisher grant narrative (S2 launch = expansion of proven model).
