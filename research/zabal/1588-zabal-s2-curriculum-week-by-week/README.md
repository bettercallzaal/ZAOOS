## Week-by-Week Detail

---

### Week 1 — Sep 1: ZAO Orientation + First WaveWarZ Battle

**Session goal:** Every participant understands what ZAO is, what WaveWarZ is, and has entered their first battle by end of day.

**Zaal opens with:**
- The ZAO North Star: THE DAO case study. ZAO IP = staple in onchain art, music, and culture.
- WaveWarZ loser-earns mechanic (from doc 1387 — 1 battle = 11,667 Spotify stream equivalents)
- ZAOOS: every session, event, and decision archived as a public document

**Session content:**
1. ZAO ecosystem tour (15 min): WaveWarZ → ZAOOS → ZAOstock → ZOR governance → ZOE automation
2. ZABAL S2 expectations (10 min): doc 1567 tracker overview — attendance, milestones, micro-grant eligibility
3. Track split intro (5 min): Artist Track vs Builder Track — confirm each participant's track
4. WaveWarZ onboarding (30 min): Phantom wallet setup → wavewarz.info registration → enter Quick Battle tonight
5. Q&A (20 min): open floor

**Deliverable:** Enter 1 WaveWarZ battle (Quick or MAIN) by Sep 5 — ZOE logs `ww_battle` milestone in Supabase (doc 1567).

**ZOE pre-session (Aug 29):**
- Post to ZABAL S2 Telegram: "ZABAL S2 starts Monday Sep 1, 2PM EST. Join link: [Zoom/Telegram]. Bring your Phantom wallet."
- DM all confirmed participants: same message + welcome
- Prepare enrollment list for Zaal: participant names + tracks

**ZOE post-session (Sep 1):**
- Post battle entry reminder to ZABAL S2 Telegram: "First milestone: enter a WaveWarZ battle by Sep 5"
- Log attendance in `zabal_s2_attendance` Supabase table

### Week 2 — Sep 8: ZAOOS Research: How to Write a ZAO Doc

**Session goal:** Every participant can write, commit, and PR a ZAOOS doc by end of week.

**Session content:**
1. ZAOOS tour (15 min): how docs are numbered, what topics exist, the PR process
2. Live demo: Zaal writes a short doc from scratch during session (10 min)
3. Writing workshop (30 min): each participant picks a topic + outlines their doc in real time
4. PR submission walkthrough (20 min): git branch → write → PR → merge
5. Q&A (15 min)

**Deliverable:** Open a ZAOOS PR by Sep 14 — ZOE logs `zaoos_doc` milestone when PR merges.

**Guest:** Optional — invite a prior ZABAL S1 participant to share their ZAOOS doc experience.

**ZOE pre-session (Sep 5):**
- Post to ZABAL S2 Telegram: "Week 2 Monday Sep 8 — bring a topic idea for your first ZAOOS doc"
- Check W1 battle milestone — DM anyone who hasn't battled yet

---

### Week 3 — Sep 15: Africa Battle Week — Cross-Cultural Music Economy

**Session goal:** Participants understand Africa Battle Week (Sep 26) as a ZAO governance event and contribute to it.

**Session content:**
1. Africa Battle Week overview (15 min): from doc 1498 — 5-day Sep 22-26, African artists only, cross-continent payout
2. Cross-cultural music economy (20 min): why Nigeria leads global P2P crypto, Afrobeats/amapiano battle culture, TechCabal press angle
3. Writing exercise (30 min): each participant writes 1 paragraph on what cross-cultural music payouts mean for their context
4. ZAOOS contribution (15 min): turn that paragraph into a ZAOOS doc stub
5. ZOR vote on charity (10 min): if Africa Battle Week charity vote is open (Jul 24-25 from doc 1557 timeline), discuss results and process

**Deliverable:** Submit an Africa Battle Week related ZAOOS doc OR attend Africa Battle Week event (Sep 26) — either counts as `ww_battle` or `zaoos_doc` milestone.

**ZOE pre-session (Sep 12):**
- Post Africa Battle Week announcement to ZABAL S2 Telegram
- Link to charity vote results (if complete)

### Week 4 — Sep 22: Fractal Democracy — Governance in Practice

**Session goal:** Participants understand Fractal Democracy, attend a live session, and earn a Respect score on OREC.

**Session content:**
1. Fractal Democracy overview (15 min): from doc 1565 — weekly sessions, Respect scoring, OREC submission
2. ZOR token role (10 min): how ZOR holders vote in WaveWarZ MAIN battles and governance sessions
3. Live governance session (40 min): run a full Fractal session during ZABAL Week 4 (this IS the week's governance session)
4. OREC submission (15 min): Zaal submits Week 4 scores to OREC live on screen
5. Reflection (10 min): what does "contribution" mean in a DAO?

**Deliverable:** Attend this session = governance attendance milestone logged in OREC + Supabase.

**ZOE pre-session (Sep 19):**
- Post Fractal session reminder to ZABAL S2 Telegram: "Week 4 = live governance session. Come prepared to share what you contributed this month."
- Send governance participation reminder to full ZAO Telegram (Thursday governance channel)

**Note:** Week 4 session substitutes for the regular Thursday ZAO governance session this week — same OREC contract entry.

### Week 5 — Sep 29: ZAOstock Week — Building Live Events On-Chain

**Session goal:** Participants are prepared for ZAOstock on Oct 3 and understand their volunteer roles.

**Session content:**
1. ZAOstock overview (15 min): from docs 1524 (day-of protocol) + 1562 (sponsor activations)
2. What makes ZAOstock on-chain (20 min): live WaveWarZ vote + charity payout from stage + ZOR governance
3. Volunteer roles (20 min): available roles — voting display operator, social media live-poster, artist greeter, ZOE support, documentation photographer
4. Role assignment (15 min): each ZABAL S2 participant picks a role
5. Logistics (20 min): venue, timing, parking, bring a phone (wavewarz.info)

**Deliverable:** Confirm ZAOstock volunteer role by Sep 30 — ZOE adds to Supabase `zaostock_role` field.

**ZOE pre-session (Sep 26):**
- Post ZABAL S2 Telegram: "Week 5 — ZAOstock is Saturday Oct 3. Week 5 session Mon Sep 29 = your ZAOstock briefing."
- Send doc 1585 Message 3 (WaveWarZ primer) to all ZABAL S2 attendees

- Oct 4: send post-event follow-up (from doc 1585 Message 5)

**ARTIST TRACK — WaveWarZ Strategy**

1. Battle strategy (20 min): how to win audience vote, promotion pre-battle
2. MAIN battle qualification (20 min): ZOR holder nomination process (doc 1559 Phase 1)
3. ZAOstock pipeline (20 min): how COC shows → ZAOstock → ZAO IP catalog
4. Individual battle plan (20 min): each artist maps their next 4 weeks of WaveWarZ activity

**Deliverable (Artist):** Enter 2 WaveWarZ battles in the following 2 weeks — logged in Supabase.

4. ZAOOS documentation of your build (20 min): how to document what you built

**Deliverable (Builder):** Write a ZAOOS tech doc about a ZOE/Hurricane feature you understand — logged as `zaoos_doc` milestone.

**ZOE pre-session (Oct 10):**

### Week 8 — Oct 20: IP, Licensing, and the CC-BY ZAOOS Model

**Session goal:** Participants understand ZAO's CC-BY licensing model and upload an artifact to Arweave.

**Session content:**
1. CC-BY overview (15 min): from doc 1506 — what CC-BY means, why ZAOOS is public domain
2. Arweave permanence (20 min): why Arweave for ZAO artifacts (music, docs, event records)
3. ZAO IP catalog (20 min): 921 songs, 34 artists, how new artist IP enters the catalog
4. Live upload (30 min): each participant uploads 1 artifact to Arweave (a song, a doc, a photo from ZAOstock)

**Deliverable:** 1 Arweave upload with ZAO attribution — logged as `arweave_upload` milestone in Supabase (doc 1567).

**ZOE pre-session (Oct 17):**
- DM participants: "Week 8 = Arweave upload. Bring: one file you want permanently archived (music file, doc, image). Arweave wallet setup: [link]."

### Week 9 — Oct 27: Grant Writing — Fisher, MAC, and OP RF

**Session goal:** Participants can write grant-ready language for ZAO programs. Builder track participants understand OP RF.

**Session content:**
1. Fisher Fund overview (15 min): from doc 1573 — arts grants, FA fiscal sponsor, ZABAL micro-grants ($1,500 line)
2. MAC grant overview (10 min): Maine Arts Commission, ZAOstock eligibility
3. OP Retro Funding (15 min): from doc 1525 — evidence-based funding, how ZAO qualifies
4. Writing exercise (30 min): each participant writes a 200-word "ZAO impact statement" in their own voice
5. Review (20 min): Zaal reviews 2-3 drafts live

**Deliverable:** Complete 200-word impact statement — ZOE collects for ZAOOS archive (logged as `zaoos_doc` if submitted as a doc).

### Week 10 — Nov 3: Community Building + Telegram Ops

**Session goal:** Participants can run ZAO community initiatives independently.

**Session content:**
1. ZAO community channels (15 min): Telegram, X @wavewarz, Farcaster /zao — what goes where
2. Community spotlight program (20 min): from doc 1564 — how to nominate and run a weekly spotlight
3. Telegram ops (20 min): from doc 1472 — moderation, welcome sequence (doc 1552), pinned message
4. ZAO Telegram leadership track (15 min): post-ZABAL S2, graduates can apply to moderate ZAO Telegram

**Deliverable:** Submit 1 community spotlight nomination — logged as `zaoos_doc` milestone if they write the spotlight doc.

### Week 11 — Nov 10: Portfolio — Compiling Your ZAOOS Contribution

**Session goal:** Every participant reviews their ZAOOS contributions and confirms micro-grant eligibility.

**Session content:**
1. Doc 1567 micro-grant eligibility criteria review (20 min):
   - Tier 1: ≥10 sessions attended + ≥1 artifact/battle + ≥1 ZAOOS doc
   - Tier 2: + 2 ZAOOS docs total
   - Tier 3: Tier 2 + participated in ZOR governance vote
2. Individual eligibility check (30 min): ZOE runs eligibility report live — each participant sees their status
3. Completion sprint (30 min): participants who need 1 more session or doc — what to submit in the next 10 days
4. Graduation statement draft (10 min): 3 sentences — what I built, what it proved, what's next

**Deliverable:** Verify ≥1 ZAOOS doc merged — logged in Supabase. Identify any gaps to close by Nov 19.

**ZOE pre-session (Nov 7):**
- Run eligibility report from doc 1567 SQL queries: Tier 1/2/3 status for all participants
- Send to Zaal before session for review
