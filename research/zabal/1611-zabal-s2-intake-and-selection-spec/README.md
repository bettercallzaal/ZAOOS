## Selection Criteria

Zaal reviews all applications by Aug 8 and selects the cohort. Scoring is holistic, not point-based.

### Must-have
- Can attend ≥10 of 12 sessions (or has a compelling reason for 2 planned absences)
- Clear intent for what they want to make or learn — vague applications are not selected

### Bonus signals (positive weight)
- Existing WaveWarZ battle participation (any level)
- ZOR holder (demonstrates governance commitment)
- Builder track applicants who can contribute to Hurricane/ZOE tooling
- Artist track applicants who are already active on Audius
- Africa-based or diaspora applicants (Africa Battle Week integration, Week 3 curriculum)

### Cohort composition targets
- 8–12 participants total
- Artist:Builder ratio ≥1:1 (builders enrich artist track, vice versa)
- ≥1 participant with ZAOstock attendance confirmed (ZABAL Week 6 = ZAOstock debrief, Week 5 = volunteer prep)
- Diversity of geography, genre, and prior ZAO exposure

### Waitlist
All non-selected applicants who met must-have criteria go on the S3 waitlist (notified in the rejection email). If a selected participant drops before Sep 1, the first waitlist person is offered the spot.
