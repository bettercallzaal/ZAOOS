# 1626 — ZABAL S2: 12-Week Curriculum Spec

**Type:** CURRICULUM-SPEC  
**Topic:** ZABAL  
**Status:** ACTIVE — S2 starts Sep 1. Applications open Jul 21 (doc 1611). This doc is the curriculum reference that ZOE shares with accepted participants (Aug 11-15 decision window) and uses in the application page description.

---

## ZABAL Overview

ZABAL (ZAO-Based Artist Learning) is ZAO's 12-week cohort for artists and builders who want to go deeper into the ZAO ecosystem. S2 runs Sep 1 – Nov 21, 2026.

**Cohort structure:** 8-12 participants. Minimum 1:1 Artist:Builder ratio.  
**Session format:** Weekly (typically aligned with ZAO Fractal Democracy session day)  
**Duration per session:** ~90 minutes  
**Platform:** ZAO Telegram + Farcaster /zabal  
**Graduation:** Nov 21, 2026 — Respect ceremony + ZABAL Marketplace (participants propose projects)

---

## Learning Tracks

### Track A: Artist Track
**Goal:** Build income, on-chain presence, and community standing as an artist in ZAO.

By graduation, an Artist track participant should:
- Have completed ≥5 WaveWarZ battles (and understand the income mechanics)
- Have an on-chain release (Audius + Sound.xyz/Zora)
- Understand how to earn Respect in ZAO Fractal and what ZOR provides
- Have participated in at least one ZOR governance vote
- Be able to describe their ZAO story for press/grant applications

### Track B: Builder Track
**Goal:** Ship at least one production tool, integration, or ZAOOS document that serves the ZAO ecosystem.

By graduation, a Builder track participant should:
- Have merged ≥2 PRs to ZAOOS or a ZAO-adjacent repo
- Understand the ZOE/Hurricane agent architecture
- Be able to run the WaveWarZ API and explain the battle mechanics to a new artist
- Have contributed at least 3 ZAOOS research docs
- Have a portfolio item: "I built [X] for ZAO"

## 12-Week Session Schedule

### Week 1 (Sep 1): ZAO Orientation
**All tracks together**
- What is ZAO: DAO structure, WaveWarZ, ZABAL S2 purpose
- North Star: ZAO = THE DAO case study; ZAO IP = onchain art, music and culture
- Tool setup: Phantom wallet, Audius account, ZAO Telegram, Farcaster /zabal
- Assignment: Set up all tools by Week 2. Artists: submit first quick battle by Week 3. Builders: read ZAOOS docs 1614 + 1615 by Week 2.

### Week 2 (Sep 8): WaveWarZ Mechanics Deep Dive
**All tracks together**  
- How battles work: bonding curve, 1% artist fee, loser payout, settlement
- Live walkthrough of wavewarz.info and the API
- Artists: find your first battle opponent (use ZAO Telegram) 
- Builders: read the wwtracker API docs (doc 1574 / PR #136)

### Week 3 (Sep 15): Track Split Begins

**Artist Track — Week 3: Your First Battle**
- Battle post-mortem: what happened, what volume you got, what payout
- Promotion strategies: X thread, Farcaster /wavewarz cast, Telegram post
- Assignment: Battle #2 by Week 5

**Builder Track — Week 3: ZAOOS Doc Writing**
- How to write a ZAOOS research doc (format, topic selection, PR process)
- Assignment: Draft first ZAOOS doc by Week 4

### Week 4 (Sep 22): Africa Battle Week — Live Event Week
**Special week — all tracks together**
- This is Africa Battle Week (Sep 22-26). Sessions align with live events.
- Watch the Africa MAIN battles live on wavewarz.info
- Artists: potential participation in quick battles during Africa Battle Week
- Builders: document Africa Battle Week in a ZAOOS doc (live research opportunity)
- ZOE posts Africa Battle Week results to /zabal daily this week

### Week 5 (Sep 29): ZAO Governance — How Fractal Democracy Works
**All tracks together**
- Walk through the Fractal Democracy session format (doc 1619)
- OREC: what it is, how to read a proposal on Optimism Etherscan
- ZOR: what it is, how artists earn it, what governance rights it grants
- Assignment: Attend one full Fractal Democracy session before Week 7

### Week 6 (Oct 6): ZAOstock Post-Event Debrief (Special Session)
**All tracks together — Oct 6, one week after ZAOstock**
- Live debrief of ZAOstock Oct 3: what worked, what to improve
- Stats recap: battle volume, charity payout, IRL governance session outcome
- Artists: how was your WaveWarZ experience at/after ZAOstock?
- Builders: ZAOstock infrastructure debrief — what did Hurricane build? What broke?

### Week 7 (Oct 13): On-Chain Music Distribution

**Artist Track — Week 7: Releasing Music On-Chain**
- Audius, Sound.xyz, Zora, DistroKid — when to use which
- 0xSplits: how royalty splits work, why they matter
- ZAO Music model (doc 1622): how to participate
- Assignment: Plan your first on-chain release by Week 9

**Builder Track — Week 7: ZOE Architecture**
- ZOE system map (doc 1615): data sources → core → output channels
- Environment variables, Neynar cast pattern, Supabase patterns (doc 1625)
- Assignment: Extend ZOE with one new cron alert or ZOL DreamLoop by Week 10

### Week 8 (Oct 20): Community and Press Strategy
**All tracks together**
- How ZAO uses press (Hypebot, Bankless, Water & Music — docs 1591, 1517)
- ZAO narrative (doc 1614): the one-sentence, three-sentence, six-sentence versions
- How to pitch your own music/project using ZAO stats
- Assignment: Write a 3-sentence ZAO story in your own words by Week 9

### Week 9 (Oct 27): Grant Writing and Funding

**Artist Track — Week 9: Grants for Independent Artists**
- Fisher Fund, Maine Arts Commission, Green Pill (doc 1612)
- How fiscal sponsorship works (Fractured Atlas — doc 1618)
- Assignment: Draft one grant application paragraph using ZAO stats

**Builder Track — Week 9: ZAO Open Source Contribution**
- How to contribute to wwtracker or ZAOOS
- PR process: branch → commit → PR → auto-merge on ZAOOS
- Assignment: Open one PR with a code or documentation improvement

### Week 10 (Nov 3): Farcaster and ZAO IP

**All tracks together**
- /wavewarz, /zabal, /zao channels: strategy and how to build an audience
- Farcaster Mini App frame embeds (doc 1480)
- ZAO IP in the wild: what's been published, cited, archived
- Assignment: Post one cast to /zabal with your ZABAL S2 progress

### Week 11 (Nov 10): Project Presentations (Prep Week)

**All tracks together**
- Each participant presents their project proposal for ZABAL Marketplace graduation
- Artist: "Here's the release/battle series I built. Here's what's next."
- Builder: "Here's the tool/doc I shipped. Here's the PR/link."
- Feedback from cohort + Zaal
- Assignment: Final project polish by Nov 17

### Week 12 (Nov 17): Final Sessions + Pre-Graduation

**All tracks together**
- Final project walk-throughs
- ZOE updates `zabal_s2_participants` status for each participant (attended ≥8/12 sessions = graduated)
- Zaal announces ZABAL Marketplace presentations (Nov 21)

### Graduation Ceremony (Nov 21): ZABAL Marketplace

Not a session — a celebration and launch event:
- Each graduate presents their S2 project to the full ZAO community (open Telegram call)
- Respect ceremony: Fractal Democracy session rates each graduate's contribution
- ZOR eligibility: graduates with top Respect scores become eligible for ZOR minting (DECISION NEEDED: exact threshold)
- ZOE posts graduation highlights to X + Farcaster /zabal: "ZABAL S2 graduates: [names]. They built [X]."

## Attendance Policy

- Minimum 8/12 sessions to graduate
- ZOE tracks attendance in `zabal_s2_participants.sessions_attended`
- Miss 3 sessions without notice → ZOE flags to Zaal for check-in call
- Participants who cannot complete S2 are eligible for S3 waitlist (not restarting — credit carries forward)

---

## ZOE Curriculum Automation

ZOE handles:
- **Weekly reminder:** Telegram + /zabal cast 24h before each session: "ZABAL S2 Week [N] tomorrow. Topic: [title]. [Any pre-reading links]."
- **Session recap:** After each session, Zaal approves a 3-bullet recap ZOE posts to /zabal
- **Attendance update:** ZOE writes session attendance to Supabase after each call (manual trigger from Zaal: "/zabal-session N attendees [handle1, handle2, ...]")
- **Graduation alert:** Nov 19, ZOE sends each confirmed graduate a Telegram DM: "You're graduating ZABAL S2 on Nov 21. Present your project to the community."
